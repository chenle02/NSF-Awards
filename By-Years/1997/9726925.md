*** CMS 9726925 Steele The Sixth Pan American Congress of Applied Mechanics
(PACAM) is being held January 4-8, 1999 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The meeting
porovides outstanding venues for enhancing communication among the engineering
scholarly communities of the Americas. Panel discussions will be held on the
topics: (a) history of women in mechanics, (b) perspective of women in
mechanics, and (c) advances in bioengineering. Selected papers from the meeting
will be published in a special issue entitled "Mechanics Pan America" of the
International Journal of Solids and Structures.***
