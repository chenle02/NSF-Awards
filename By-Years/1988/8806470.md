Dr. Jeffrey Garner Forbes has been awarded a Postdoctoral Fellowship in
Chemistry. Dr. Forbes' doctoral degree was from the University of Illinois under
the supervision of Professor Eric Oldfield. He intends to continue his research
at the University of Illinois under the sponsorship of Professor Andrew J.
Gellman. Dr. Forbes area of postdoctoral research will be atomic-scale study of
the forces between surfaces.
