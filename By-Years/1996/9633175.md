9633175 Lagorio This is an international travel grant to support the Principal
Investigator to participate in the European Conference on Structural Control,
May 29-31, 1996 in Spain. The conference addresses research issues in an area of
high priority to NSF and the involvement of a U.S. expert in the technical
activities of the conference will benefit the U.S. research and development in
this important subject through sharing of data and information and development
of future joint research efforts with European countries. ***
