Students are focusing more on concepts in a computer laboratory that supports
numerical, symbolic and graphical calculations. The laboratory consists of 15
NeXT personal workstations, supported by networked fileservers and laser
printers. A laboratory experience is being incorporated into six mathematics and
mathematics education courses.
