This proposal is for a conference to bring together researchers, curriculum
developers, teachers and educational technologists interested in developing the
potential for broad impact of dynamic geometry visualization technology in
elementary and middle school mathematics classrooms. The results of the
conference will be disseminated through the publication and distribution of its
proceedings, in printed, CD-ROM and Web format.
