DMS-9423945 Kalka This REU site award provides support for three undergraduate
researchers in mathematics. Research will be in an area of geometry or topology.
The program will run for eight weeks. Students will participate in a seminar
series and will each give a presentation. In addition, a visitor will be brought
in from outside this university to interact with the students.
