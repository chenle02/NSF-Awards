9618743 Goad This award to University of Michigan will provide new computers and
water sampling bottles for use on the R/V Laurentian, an 80-foot research vessel
operated as part of the University National Oceanographic Laboratory System
(UNOLS). The shared-use instrumentation supported here will assist researchers
to conduct studies of the Great Lakes in 1997 and in future years. ***
