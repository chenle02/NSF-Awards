CMS-9417432 Beedle, Lynn S. Lehigh University This award is to provide partial
travel funds for a group of researchers to present technical papers at the Fifth
World Congress - Habitat and the High-Rise: Tradition and Innovation. It will be
held May 14 - 19, 1995 at Amsterdam, The Netherlands. The meeting will discuss:
building systems, concrete and masonry buildings, new concepts, planning,
management, safety, deterioration, and related topics.
