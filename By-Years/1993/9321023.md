9321023 Gerbode This award assists the SESQUINET Regional Network in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks and to the rest of the Internet. The
award, made under the guidelines published in NSF Program Solicitation 93-52,
partially funds one 10 million bits per second connection. Anticipated duration
of the award is four years.
