This project will partially fund a half-day workshop on communicating science to
the public and media and is geared toward graduate students and postdoctoral
research scholars. The workshop will include sessions on the importance of
communicating science, and exercises to allow participants to practice
communication skills.&lt;br/&gt; The workshop will improve communication among
disciplines within science as well and increase scientific outreach to the
general public.
