The main objective of this workshop is to bring together researchers,
engineers, and managers in the apparel manufacturing industry to discuss and
summarize research issues related to the improvement of apparel manufacturing
operations. Major topic areas include human factors engineering, production
planning and control, total quality management, productivity improvement and
related issues in manufacturing systems. Expected outcomes include a conference
proceedings and the eventual development of an apparel manufacturing research
and technology transfer center. The activity involves significant industrial
collaboration.
