This grant provides partial support for the attendance of about twelve polymer
scientists from the former Soviet Bloc at the symposium on Polyelectrolytes,
which will be held in Denver, from 28 March - 1 April 1993. The symposium will
provide a unique international forum for the discussion of this important
scientific subject.
