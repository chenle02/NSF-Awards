Computational sustainability is an emerging field, developing and applying
computational methods, notably of articial intelligence, to difficult problems
of long-term environmental and societal sustainability. This award supports the
travel and subsistence of graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and other
junior researchers for participation at the 1st International Conference on
Computational Sustainability (June 8-11, 2009, Ithaca, NY).
