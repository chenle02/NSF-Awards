This award will provide support for approximately 20 participants (graduate,
post-docs, and junior faculty) to attend the IEEE INFOCOM 2002 Conference being
held in New York, NJ on June 23-27, 2002. INFOCOM is a major conference in
networking, which focuses on state-of-the-art research in all areas of computer
networking and data communications.
