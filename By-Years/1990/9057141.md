A Presidential Young Investigator will continue her research on young
children's learning of mathematics in minority settings. The research will
include investigation of small group collaborative learning, small group
interactions, establishment of social norms for cooperation, and affective
issues in learning mathematics. Activities will include classroom teaching
experiments, analysis of data, the development of theoretical perspectives, and
implementation of developed curricula.
