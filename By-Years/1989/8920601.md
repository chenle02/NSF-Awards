The goal of this project is to bring together a significant number of
researchers in the area of material handling for a workshop focusing on the
current state of research and the critical issues to be addressed in the last
decade of the 20th Century. Co-sponsorship for the project is provided by the
Material Handling Industries of America through the College- Industry Council
for Material Handling Education, and the Material Handling Research Center at
Georgia Tech.
