This is support for a symposium entitled "New Service Opportunities for Electric
Utilities: Creating Differentiated Products" to be held at the University of
California - Berkeley on September 12 - 14, 1990. The main topic is product
differentiation in the power industry, an industry which is partially regulated
and partially deregulated. The symposium is also being supported by the Electric
Power Research Institute (EPRI) and the University of California Universitywide
Energy Research Group.
