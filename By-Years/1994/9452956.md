9452956 Spencer The second Gordon Research Conference on "Innovation in the
Teaching of College Chemistry will be held January 8-13, 1995, in Ventura,
California. The conference will provide a unique opportunity for an influential
group of educators to become familiar with rapidly advancing educational
technologies, learn about emerging research in pedagogy and assessment, and
discuss in depth issues and new curricular directions for chemistry education.
Support is being used to insure that representatives of a broad range of
institutions from all sectors of the educational community can participate in
the conference.
