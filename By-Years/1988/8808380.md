Dr. Kenneth Bruce Laughlin has been recommended for a Postdoctoral Research
Fellowship in Chemistry Award. Dr. Laughlin's doctoral degree was from the
University of California at Berkeley under the supervision of Professor Richard
J. Saykally. He intends to continue his research at Massachusetts Institute of
Technology under the sponsorship of Professor Sylvia T. Ceyer. Dr. Laughlins's
area of Postdoctoral Research will be Electronic Processes in Molecule-Surface
Collision Dynamics.
