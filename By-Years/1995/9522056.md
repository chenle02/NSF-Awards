ABSTRACT 9522056 Sinai Princeton University will host a conference "Prospects in
Mathematics" on the occasion of the 250th anniversary of Princeton University.
The goal of the conference will be to discuss the most promising directions for
the future development of Mathematical Science. The areas to be covered include
algebra and number theory; geometry; analysis; mathematical physics; and
probability theory. This award will provide funding attendance for less senior
people and for women and under-represented minorities. Other sources are
available for support of the speakers.
