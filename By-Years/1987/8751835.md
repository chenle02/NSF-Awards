The goal of the project is to help teachers with the planning of programs
designed to increase the participation of Chicanos and Native Americans in the
sciences. The workshop and conference will consist of science symposia on timely
scientific themes, workshops on the improvement of science teaching, and
interactions among SACNAS scientists, teachers and students.
