9419142 Lay This award provides partial funding for a technical support position
in the Institute of Tectonics and the Earth Sciences Board at the University of
California, Santa Cruz. The primary impact of the position (Computer Systems
Manager) will be on computer-intensive geophysics research programs in the
Crustal Imaging Laboratory, the W.M. Keck Seismological Laboratory, and the
Earth Sciences Visualization Laboratory, all of which are maintained by the
Systems Manager. ***
