9627336 Steele This grant provides $46,295 as 20% support of salary, fringe and
associated overhead for a technician at the electron microprobe (EMP) lab at the
University of Chicago for the next two years. This is a Phase II technician
support proposal under the EAR/IF program guidelines. NSF support of this
position will allow this lab to continue to act as a regional facility for major
elemental analysis of igneous, metamorphic and planetary samples. ***
