This is a group travel award to support the participation of engineers and
scientists presenting papers on hydrologic extreme events at the International
Symposium on "Water for the Future", that will take place in Rome, Italy, on
April 6-11, 1987. The Water Resources Center, University of Illinois, at Urbana-
Champaign, is taking the responsibility to select the participants to be funded
as they have done quite successfully, in the past, for other international
conferences.
