A memory upgrade for NCUBE parallel processor will be provided for researchers
at Tufts University for research in the Department of Computer Science. The
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The equipment will be used for
several research projects, including in particular: 1. Parallel Algorithms for
Knowledge Representation 2. Machine Learning: Mapping and Integrating Analogical
Models 3. Support Environment for Parallel Programs 4. VLSI Layout Algorithms 5.
An Expert System that Identifies Damage in Structures
