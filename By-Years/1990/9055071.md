The College of Science and Mathematics at Southwest Missouri State University
will initiate a four.week, residential Young Scholars project in Mathematics for
30 students entering grades 8,9. Participants will design a landfill for a
hypothetical city called Model City, USA. Emphasis will be on problem solving
and mathematical modeling. Trips to Monsanto and McDonnell Douglas will be
conducted to provide participants interaction with scientists and engineers at
work.
