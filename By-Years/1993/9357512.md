9357512 Marand This NSF Young Investigator award deals with research and
teaching in the area of phase transition phenomena in semicrystalline
homopolymers, copolymers, polymer mixtures, and polyrotaxanes. The aim is to
relate chemical composition, crystallization, and morphology to observed bulk
properties. ***
