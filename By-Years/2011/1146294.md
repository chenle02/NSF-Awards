The PIs and Co-PIs of grants supported through the NSF-NIH-BMBF Collaborative
Research in Computational Neuroscience (CRCNS) program meet annually. This will
be the seventh meeting of CRCNS investigators, and the first involving US-German
projects supported by NSF/NIH and BMBF. The meeting brings together a broad
spectrum of computational neuroscience researchers supported by the program, and
includes poster presentations, talks and plenary lectures. The meeting is
scheduled for October 9-11, 2011 and will be held at Princeton University.
