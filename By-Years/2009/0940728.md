The PIs and Co-PIs of grants supported through the NSF-NIH Collaborative
Research in Computational Neuroscience (CRCNS) program meet annually. This will
be the fifth meeting of CRCNS investigators. The meeting brings together a broad
spectrum of computational neuroscience researchers supported by the program, and
includes poster presentations, talks and plenary lectures. The meeting is
scheduled for June 7-9, 2009 and will be held at Carnegie Mellon University and
the University of Pittsburgh.
