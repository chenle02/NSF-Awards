This award is for renewed support for the Committee on Earthquake Engineering.
The committee was established in 1983 as a standing committee by the National
Research Council to provide a national focus for, and continuing assessment of
the nation's earthquake engineering activities under the National Earthquake
Hazards Reduction Program.
