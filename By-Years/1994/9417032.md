ABSTRACT NCR-9417032 PI: Allan Rubens University of Michigan "Implementation of
SNMP-Based Network Management for the MBONE" This is a 15 month project to
develop, implement and evaluate an SNMP-based management capability for the
MBONE, and to develop and distribute some specific SNMP-based MBONE network
management tools.
