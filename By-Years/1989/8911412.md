The 18th International Symposium on Acoustical Imaging will be held, September
18-20, 1989, in Santa Barbara. The professional society involved are: the
Acoustical Society of America; the IEEE Ultrasonics, Ferroelectrics, and
Frequency Control Society; and the International Society for Optical
Engineering. A wide range of topics related to acoustical imaging will be
discussed at this symposium, these include: acoustic holography and tomography,
acoustic microscopy, medical ultrasound, nondestructive evaluation, underwater
acoustical imaging, acoustical signal processing, acoustic parameter estimation,
seismic exploration, tissue characterization, and image reconstruction
algorithms.
