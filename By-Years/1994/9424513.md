ABSTRACT 9424513 Sterling The purpose of this colloquium is to bring together a
select group of academic, industry, and government leaders to discuss issues
related to the future development and maintenance of underground facilities.
This subject area includes the clean up and preservation of the underground
environment as well as the preservation of the surface environment through the
use of underground facilities. The outcome will provide perspective on the
future needs and conflicts for the use of the underground which can help drive
the development of technology and public policy. ***
