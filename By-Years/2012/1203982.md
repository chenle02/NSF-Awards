This award is made as part of the FY 2012 Mathematical Sciences Postdoctoral
Research Fellowships Program. These fellowships support a research and training
plan at a host institution in the mathematical sciences, including applications
to other disciplines. The title of the research fellowship awarded to Ameerah N.
Chowdhury is "Shadows and Intersections." The host institution for the
fellowship is University of California, Los Angeles, and the sponsoring
scientist is Dr. Benjamin Sudakov.
