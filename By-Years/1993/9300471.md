This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduate (REU) Site proposal
to provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in two
interdisciplinary ares:Global Environmental Quality aand the Oceana; and
Estuarine, Coastal and Exclusive Economic Zone Environmental Quality and
Fisheries, to acquaint the students with the excitement and opportunities of
academic research and to encourage them to pursue graduate studies and a career
in ocean sciences.
