ABSTRACT "MOREnet Expansion Phase 7" University of Missouri - Columbia PI:
William Mitchell NCR-9413031 This award adds four higher education institutions
to the MOREnet state network: Avila College Kansas City North Central Missouri
College Trenton St. Louis Community College St. Louis State Fair Community
College Sedalla Faculty and students at the institutions benefit from access to
the resources of the Internet, including libraries and supercomputers. In
addition, they are able to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at other
campuses in pursuit of research and educational projects. The award provides
partial support of the project for two years.
