9731815 Paesler North Carolina State University will continue a Research
Experience for Undergraduates (REU) site in its Physics department. Fifteen
undergraduate students will be recruited with a recruitment focus on minority
and women candidates. Students will participate in materials physics research
that includes semiconductor thin films and interfaces, epitaxial growth of
semiconductor structures, amorphous semiconductors and insulators, and
fullerenes. %%% Undergraduate students will have the opportunity to participate
in state-of-the-art materials physics research and will be utilizing a variety
of modern instruments. ***
