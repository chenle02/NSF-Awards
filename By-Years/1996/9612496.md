This award provided travel support for a workshop on the topic of synergy
between software engineering and programming language research. The workshop was
held June 12- 13 in Boston, MA, in juxtaposition with a Computing Research
Association and ACM symposium on strategic directions in computing, June 14-15,
in the same location. The software engineering and programming language workshop
also received partial funding from ARO. ***
