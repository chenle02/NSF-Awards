Image Processing equipment will be provided for researchers at North Carolina
State University in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer
Science. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
Research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the area(s) (1) Coding
of single frame images for transmission and storage (2) Coding of Sequential
frame imagery for transmission over low bandwidth channels (3) Image restoration
and reconstruction from incomplete and partial information (4) Reconstruction of
several biomedical imaging modalities, and (5) Three-dimensional object
recognition and description from two- dimensional images.
