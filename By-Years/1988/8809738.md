Mr. Steven Antony King has been awarded a Postdoctoral Research Fellowship in
Chemistry. Mr. King's doctoral degree will be from Stanford University under the
supervision of Professor Barry M. Trost. He intends to continue his research at
Princeton University under the supervision of Professor Jeffrey Schwartz. Mr.
King's area of postdoctoral research will be the effect of gross structural
changes on the reactivity of metalloazines and exploration of polyether alkoxide
ligands in transition metal chemistry.
