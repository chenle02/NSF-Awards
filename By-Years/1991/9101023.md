This is an international travel grant to support the Principal Investigator's
visit to University of Canterbury, New Zealand to conduct joint study on
earthquake resistant design and detailing of reinforced concrete structures. The
activity will enable an understanding of the differences in the design
approaches in the two countries be achieved.
