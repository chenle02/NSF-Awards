This is a Research Experiences for Undergraduates project. It will provide
research experiences for eight students working on the University of New
Hampshire's Hubbard Brook Ecosystems study. Students will participate in
projects involving forest ecology, limnology, biogeochemistry, microbial ecology
and atmospheric sciences.
