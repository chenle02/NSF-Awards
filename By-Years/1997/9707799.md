9707799 Boggess The PI will study ultrafast carrier dynamics in the narrow-band-
gap nanostructures typically employed to produce mid-wave infrared radiation.
Characterization activities undertaken will specifically focus upon the impact
of hot carriers on MWIR diode laser performance. ***
