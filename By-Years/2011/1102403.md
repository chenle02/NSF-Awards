This award will provide partial support for the 1000 Islands Energy Research
Forum (TIERF) conference to be held on Nov 12-14, 2010 in Alexandria Bay, NY.
&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Intellectual Merit&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The TIERF meeting
is Canadian-US collaborative activity of the Great Lakes Sustainable Energy
Consortium (GLSEC), a group of academic and industry partners serving to foster
technical research, public policy options, and commercial developments in
sustainable energy strategies on both sides of the border in the Great Lakes
region of Ontario, Canada and New York, USA.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Broader
Impacts&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The TIERF provides a venue for graduate students to
present their research results to their peers and seasoned academic researchers,
to create cross border relations, and to interact with industry representatives.
