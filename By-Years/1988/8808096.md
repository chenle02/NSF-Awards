A Lisp workstation will be provided for researchers at the University of
Arizona for research in the Department of Computer Engineering. This equipment
is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer and
Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which the
equipment is to be used will be in the areas of research combining simulation,
artificial intelligence, and system design.
