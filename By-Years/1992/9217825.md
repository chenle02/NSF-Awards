This award will support to defray some of the travel expenses for selected
participants form the United States in the Third Pan American Congress of
Applied Mechanics (PACAM III), to be held on the campus of Sao Paulo, January
4-8, 1993. The objectives of the PACAM series include promotion of mechanics as
a scientific and technical field in the Americas, to enhance personal
interactions, to expose new research findings to the community of researchers
and engineers.
