This grant will fund 25 travel awards, predominately for women, minorities and
young researchers to attend and participate in the 4th Valencia International
Meeting on Bayesian Statistics to be held in Peniscola, Spain, April 15-20,
1991. This series of meetings, held once every four years, is acknowledged as
the premier international forum for discussion and dissemination of advances in
the rapidly growing area of Bayesian statistics.
