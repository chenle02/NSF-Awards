This award provides funding for a 3-year standard award to support a Research
Experiences for Teachers (RET) Site at Vanderbilt University, entitled,
"Vanderbilt University Biomedical Engineering RET Site Project." The program
will provide a four-week summer research experience for fourteen math and
science teachers from the Nashville, TN, Metropolitan Public and Private Schools
as well as from six neighboring counties.
