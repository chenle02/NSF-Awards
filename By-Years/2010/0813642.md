Computer Science (31)&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This project is conducting a two-day
summer workshop for individuals from colleges and universities who participate
in K-12 computer science outreach activities. The workshop focuses on
coordinating these outreach activities to achieve improvements in their scope
and effectiveness and on facilitating communication and cooperation among the
individuals engaged in these activities.
