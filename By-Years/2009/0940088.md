This award will support a joint US-Japan Workshop on US-Japan Workshop: Life
Cycle Assessment (LCA) of Sustainable Infrastructure Materials to be held in
Sapporo Japan in October 2009. The workshop is being organized by the Center for
Innovative Materials Research (CIMR) of Lawrence Technological University in
conjunction with the Stanford University Department of Civil and Environmental
Engineering. The Workshop will be hosted by the Civil Engineering Department at
Hokkaido University in Sapporo, Hokkaido, Japan. The goal of the workshop is to
convene a meeting of researchers and thinkers mainly from Japan and US, to
confer on the use of LCA for sustainable materials in bridge infrastructure.
