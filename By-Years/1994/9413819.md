9413819 Karniadakis A research group at Brown University, working on aspects of
high performance architectures for computational science, will use ARI funds to
purchase a state-of-the-art parallel computer for researches involved in: (1)
Parallel Algorithims Paradigms; (2) Parallel Methods for Hyperbolic Problems;
(3) Parallel Methods for Incompressible Flows.
