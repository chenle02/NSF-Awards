The Department of Mathematics and the Center for Applied Mathematics and
Statistics at the New Jersey Institute of Technology will purchase color
monitors, tape drive, disk, compiler and statistical packages: S-Plus, IMSL
Fortran and SYSTAT which will be dedicated to support faculty and students
research in the mathematical sciences. The equipment will be used in support of
several projects, including in particular: .Evaluation of Multitype Repair
Strategies by M.C. Bhattacharjee. . A Minimum Distance Estimation Procedure and
Bivariate Geometric Models by Sunil K. Dhar. . Biomedical Studies in Cardiology,
Gynecology/Oncology, and Neurology by R. Dios. . Multivariate Long Memory
Processed by Bonnie K.Ray.
