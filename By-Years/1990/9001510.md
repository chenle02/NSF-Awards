To facilitate the exchange of ideas among workers interested in computer
applications in biology, an international meeting will be held from 8-11 July
1990, at George Mason University. The meeting will involve computer workshops,
poster sessions, and plenary sessions with invited talks. The plenary sessions
will treat the following specific areas: - The BioMATRIX Concept - Phylogeny and
Systematics - Enabling Technologies for BioInformatics - Artificial Intelligence
and Philosophical Foundations - Directories and Data Integration Projects -
BioMATRIX of the Biosequences - Modeling and Simulation - Electronic Facilities
for Scientific Communication The meeting will also involve organizational
sessions that will, among other things, explore the formation of a BioMATRIX
society to provide a vehicle for further scientific exchange.
