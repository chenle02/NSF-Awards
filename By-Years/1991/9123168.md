This award will support an astronomical symposium entitled: "Massive Stars:
Their Lives in the Interstellar Medium," to be held at the University of
Wisconsin on June 23-25, 1991. These funds will be used to defray travel costs
for 16 young astronomers or invited speakers. This symposium will focus on the
star/ interstellar medium interactions for massive stars and call attention to
areas needing additional research.
