A shared - memory multiprocessor will be provided for researchers at Kent State
University for research in the Department of Mathematical Sciences. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which the
equipment is to be used will be in the areas of: Parallel Algorithms and
Implementations for Symbolic Computations Automatic Parallel Code Generation
Parallel Algorithms for the Numerical Solution of Linear Systems Parallel
Computing for Engineering Applications Multiprocessing Languages and Operating
Environments
