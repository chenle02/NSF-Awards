This award provides technical support, in the form of the partial salary costs
of a technician, to operate, maintain, and give instructions on the principles
and operations of the electron microprobe laboratory in the Department of
Geological Sciences at the University of Chicago. The electron microprobe is a
heavily used research and teaching instrument at Chicago, used primarily for the
quantitative microanalysis (chemical composition) of mineral and geological
samples.
