A multicomputer system for parallel processing will be provided for researchers
at the University of Michigan for research in the Department of Electrical
Engineering and Computer Science. This equipment is provided under the
Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer and Information Science and
Engineering program. The research for which the equipment is to be used will be
in the area of parallel database processing.
