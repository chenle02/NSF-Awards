This award funds a Research Fellowship in Plant Biology for twoyears. The
project is entitled "Molecular Analysis of Disease Resistance in Arabadopsis
thaliana". The Research Fellowships in Plant Biology are granted to applicants
trained in traditional areas of plant biology who wish to extend their training
in new techniques and to those trainedin molecular or cellular techniques who
wish to gain experience working with plants. These fellowships are designed to
increasethe number of outstanding young scientists in plant biology.
