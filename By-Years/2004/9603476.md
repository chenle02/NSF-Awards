Abstract Solomonson 9603476 This is an award to support attendance of young
investigators at a meeting to be held May 4-9, 1997 on the campus of the
University of South Florida in Tampa. The topic of the meeting is "Nitrogen
Assimilation: Molecular and Genetic Aspects". This meeting will bring together
microbiologists and plant biologists to discuss a variety of topics relating to
nitrogen fixation and nitrate assimilation. The program includes topics from
chemical to ecological. Participants will have the opportunity to meet
informally to discuss their work as well as attend formal lectures.
