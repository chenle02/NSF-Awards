9610292 Goad This award to University of Michigan provides shipboard technical
support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument calibration
for researchers using R/V Laurentian, a research vessel operated by the Center
for Great Lakes and Aquatic Sciences as part of the University National
Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet. The technical support awarded
here will assist NSF-funded researchers conduct studies in the Great Lakes in
1997. ***
