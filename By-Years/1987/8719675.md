The Center for Interactive Computer Graphics is an Industry/University
Cooperative Research Center that is "self- sufficient." A new program now allows
the NSF to fund the evaluation activities of "self-sufficient"
Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers. This project funds the
evaluation studies for the Center for Interactive Computer Graphics.
