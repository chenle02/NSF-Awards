This proposal is for student travel support to attend the IEEE EnergyTech 2013
conference which surveys promising technologies in Power and Energy to address
the growing demand for sustainable energy. This conference will bring together
experts in diverse areas from academia, government and industry to review and
explore technologies that promise significant contributions towards addressing
the global Energy Sustainability challenge. The workshop is chaired by Prof.
Wyatt Newman and will be held at Case Western Reserve University on May 21-23,
2013.
