Funds are requested for a transmission electron microscope. This microscope will
be placed in a central training and research facility at the University of
Florida and will be available to university community. The main users will be
six major and at least five minor users. Research projects will be funded in the
following areas: bacterial ultrastructure, fungal taxonomy, orchid scent
secretion, mitochondrial ribosome substructure, glycoprotein secretion, and
lobster chemoreception.
