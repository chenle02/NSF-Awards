This award provides support for student attendance at the 2001 Workshop on Non-
neutral Plasmas. The workshop is being held at the University of California, San
Diego, from 30 July to 3 August, 2001, and brings together about 120 plasma and
atomic physicists working on diverse aspects of non-neutral plasmas and beams.
