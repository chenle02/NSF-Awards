This project will organize a virtual PI meeting for the NeTS community, with a
broad invitation provided to current PIs, eminent researchers in academia,
aspiring PIs, early-career faculty and researchers from diverse backgrounds. The
PI meeting will include sessions on future networking research challenges, early
career advice and NSF programs.

This award reflects NSF's statutory mission and has been deemed worthy of
support through evaluation using the Foundation's intellectual merit and broader
impacts review criteria.
