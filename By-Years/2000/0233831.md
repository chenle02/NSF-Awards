Professor Jeanne Pemberton of the University of Arizona is supported by the
Analytical and Surface Chemistry Program and the MPS Office of Multidisciplinary
Activities for a symposium entitled "Celebrating Women in Analytical Chemistry"
to be held in conjunction with the American Chemical Society National Meeting in
Boston MA August 2002. The meeting will present outstanding scientific
achievements by women in analytical chemistry from academic, industrial and
government laboratories. &lt;br/&gt;
