This award will support travel of approximately 15-20 U.S. students to the
annual IEEE symposium on Security and Privacy, held this year on May 22-25,
2011. This is one of the primary research conferences in this field. Attending
this particular symposium can be of significant value in starting students on
effective careers in this field, which is of vital national interest.
