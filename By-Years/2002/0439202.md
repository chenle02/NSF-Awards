This Small Grant for Exploratory Research (SGER) funds a twelve-month pilot
effort for site-visit support for emerging research and education communities'
adaptation and deployment of technology, in particular research and education
communities at minority-serving institutions. This pilot effort is in
collaboration with the Minority Serving Institution Consortium (MSIC), led by
Stephanie A. Mclean of the National Center for Supercomputer Applications
(NCSA).
