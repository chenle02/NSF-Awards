9528352 Hopking St. Thomas University requests support from NSF for connection
to Digital Business Systems (DBS). DBS, a midlevel network in the state of
Florida, will provide operations management an information services and it will
give the campus access to the Internet, a collection of interoperable networks
that connect most research and education organizations in the United States. The
campus needs the Internet connections to be able to access information resources
throughout the state, the nation and the world. The connection will benefit the
faculty, staff students of the St. Thomas University campus as well as the
community in general.
