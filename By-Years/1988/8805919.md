A set of networked workstations will be provided for researchers at the
University of California for research in the Department of Electrical
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of 1)
Automatically recoverable distributed computer systems for real- time
applications. 2) Packet communications protocol. 3) Methodology for synthesis of
VLSI systems. 4) Architecture concepts for high-speed pattern recognition.
