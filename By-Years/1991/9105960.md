Five undergraduate students will be selected to participate in the Convection
and Precipitation/Electrification Experiment (CaPE), being conducted in east
central Florida (8 July - 18 Aug 1991). The students will assist various
scientists in ways designed to promote their interest in science as intended
under the REU program.
