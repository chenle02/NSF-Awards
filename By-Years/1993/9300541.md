This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduate (REU) Site proposal
to provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in studies related
to Physical, Chemical and Biological Oceanography, to acquaint the students with
the excitement and opportunities of academic research and to encourage them to
pursue graduate studies and a career in ocean sciences.
