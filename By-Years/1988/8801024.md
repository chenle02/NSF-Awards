This award provides funds to support, in part, the travel expenses for U.S.
participants in the 1988 IEEE International Symposium on Information Theory,
held in Kobe Japan, June 19-24, 1988 and in the 1988 Workshop on Information
Theory co-sponsored by the Chinese Institute of Electronics Information Theory
Society, held in Beijing, China, July 3-6, 1988.
