9417576 England This project connects three higher education institutions to
NSFNET through the CERFnet mid-level network. They are: Woodbury University
Burbank Webster University Irvine California School of San Diego of Professional
Psychology Faculty and students at the schools benefit from access to the
resources of the Internet, including libraries and supercomputers. In addition,
they are able to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at other
institutions in pursuit of research and educational projects. The award provides
partial support of the project for two years.
