9322137 Makaroff Miami University @ Oxford Dr. Chris A. Makaroff and other
members of the Chemistry Department of Miami University at Oxford are being
funded for a Research Experiences for Undergraduate students site in Chemistry.
For the period 1994-6, nine undergraduate students will spend twelve weeks each
summer actively engaged in a variety of research projects. Projects cover the
areas of analytical, biochemistry and organic. The students will be drawn
nationally.
