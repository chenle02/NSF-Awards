Geological Sample Storage facilitites provide important and essential services
to the geological research community. The core repository facility at Scripps
Institution of Oceanography is being funded to archive cores and other
geological rock samples from the seafloor and provide the important services of
sample distribution and related data dissemination to the marine geological,
sedimentological and paleoceanographic communities.
