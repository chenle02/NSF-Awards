The Conference on Ramsey Theory and Topological Algebra is a conference designed
to disseminate new findings in the areas and to honor Neil Hindman on his 65th
birthday, who is one of the top experts in the fields. Hindman is also known for
directing more doctorate degrees given to African Americans than anyone else in
modern history.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The invited speakers
are.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; Vitaly Bergelson, The Ohio State
University&lt;br/&gt; Hillel Furstenberg, Hebrew University&lt;br/&gt; Imre
Leader, Trinity College, Cambridge&lt;br/&gt; Randall McCutcheon, The University
of Memphis&lt;br/&gt; Dona Strauss, The University of
Hull&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The conference expects to have approximately 100
mathematicians and 20 graduate student participants. The conference will be held
at Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. Contributed talks will also be given. The
homepage for the conference is http://www.units.muohio.edu/sumsri/hindman2.html
