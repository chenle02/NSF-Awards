This award is for partial support of an "International Symposium on 30 years of
Neutral Currents" to be held on the UCLA campus. Participation is international
in scope. About 65 - 80 scientists are expected to attend. The subject matter is
a mix of historical reminiscence, review of current status and prospects for
future developments in this sector of electroweak physics.
