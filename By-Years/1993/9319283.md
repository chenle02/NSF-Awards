9319283 Draine Studies of Interstellar Processes. Dr. Draine will use this award
to support several research projects in the general area of theoretical
astrophysics of diffuse interstellar matter. The studies include: (A) optics of
interstellar dust grains; (B) photodissociation fronts; and (C) interstellar
magnetohydrodynamic shock waves.
