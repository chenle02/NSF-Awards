9452957 Dubinsky A special issue of the journal Undergraduate Mathematics
Education Trends is being developed to inform the mathematics community about
the first decade of Calculus Reform. The issue is being disseminated to more
than 70,000 individuals. The articles for the special issue will examine key
issues of the Calculus Reform movement and will range over its extent and
effects, measures of success, and basic questions of content, pedagogy, and
technology.
