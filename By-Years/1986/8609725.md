* Improved Model of Combined Cutting Mechanics, Heat Transfer and Wear of PDC Cutters in Cutting Rock

This project will study the effects of heat transfer and cutter temperature on
the performance and life of drill bits which utilize synthetic diamond compact
cutters. The end results of this work are expected to enable drilling for oil to
be performed at greater depths than at present.
