Dr. William H. Green has been recommended for a Postdoctoral Research Fellowship
in Chemistry Award. Dr. Green's doctoral degree was from the University of
California at Berkeley under the supervision of Professor C. Bradley Moore. He
intends to continue his research at Cambridge University under the sponsorship
of Professor Nicholas C. Handy. Dr. Green's area of Postdoctoral Research is in
the area of Ab initio studies of highly vibrationally excited polyatomic
molecules.
