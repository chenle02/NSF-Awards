Scholarships are being awarded to academically talented low-income students
majoring in mathematics, computer science or engineering. The selection process
includes academic merit, science and mathematics background, and motivation. A
special effort is being be made to attract applications from among qualified
graduates of Arkansas community colleges. The scholarship recipients are being
nurtured through proactive advising, community-building activities,
presentations on career opportunities by industry representatives, and access to
research opportunities. &lt;br/&gt;
