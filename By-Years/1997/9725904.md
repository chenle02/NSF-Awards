ABSTRACT Crumpton EEC-9725904 Mississippi State This award provides support for
a two-year, Research Opportunity Award to Mississippi State University. The
overall goal of this research project is to evaluate the effects of the total
body fatigue on human reliability. The research work will be performed at the
I/UCRC on Ergonomics at the Texas A&amp;M University, by an Assistant Professor
from the Department of Industrial Engineering at Mississippi State University.
