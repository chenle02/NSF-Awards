Seattle University in conjunction with Seattle Central Community College and the
Washington Center for Improving Undergraduate Education is conducting a project
on precalculus revitalization. A 1992 summer workshop will train twenty core
faculty in new topics and approaches for teaching precalculus mathematics. The
participant pairs from 10 institutions must agree to teach at least one course
the following year using materials and ideas generated in the workshop and
