Detectors of infra-red radiation use crystals of HgCdTe. These crystals in turn
are grown on substrates formed by CdTeSe crystals. The performance of the HgCdTe
crystals will be better if grown on better substrates. A better method of
growing CdTeSe crystals is proposed.
