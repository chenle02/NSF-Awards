Joint support with the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the Army
Construction Engineering Research Laboratory is provided for a conference,
symposium, and workshop on innovative concepts for biological treatment of
wastewaters that may be of toxic significance to the environment. The conference
is planned for July of 1986 in Washington, D.C.
