9452701 Duggan Northeastern University will initiate a six-week, commuter, Young
Scholars Project in engineering for 26 students entering grades 11 and 12. The
program provides classroom instructions, field trips, hands-on laboratory
activities, and an opportunity for students to have significant laboratory
research experiences by engaging in the state-of-the-art research projects under
the supervision of scientists and engineers in research laboratories in the
College of Engineering at Northeastern University.
