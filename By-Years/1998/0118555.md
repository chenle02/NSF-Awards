This award provides support for student attendance at the annual meeting of the
Division of Atomic, Molecular, and Optical Physics (DAMOP) of the American
Physical Society. The meeting will be held jointly with the Division of Atomic
and Molecular Physics of the Canadian Physical Society in London, Ontario, 16-19
May, 2001. This is the principal meeting covering principles of AMO physics held
each year, and this award gives students the opportunity to participate in DAMOP
sponsored events.
