Through this planning effort, Knoxville College will develop comprehensive
approaches to increase the number of undergraduate students graduating in good
standing in science, mathematics, and technology (SMT), as well as the number of
non-SMT majors who develop basic science literacy as undergraduates. Specific
objectives for this planning initiative include the development: o of strategies
to improve the Knoxville College undergraduate SMT curriculum and its delivery
including content, faculty development, equipment and facilities, and
technology; o of a strategy to provide students with meaningful internship
opportunities that complement their undergraduate SMT experience; o of
entrepreneurial science and technology partnerships with industry; and o a
comprehensive multi-year implementation plan outlining a vision, needs,
resources, and outcomes for the objectives above.

This initiative addresses the Nation's need of to broaden participation in
science and technology, and to expand the scientific literacy of all citizens.
