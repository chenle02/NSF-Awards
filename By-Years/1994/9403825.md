9403825 Kleinfeld "Northern Women, Northern Lives", an International Conference
on Women in the Circumpolar North, will be a forum to gain recognition for the
perspectives and roles of women in the Arctic and Subarctic region. Topics of
discussion will include family, worklife, health, leadership, public policy,
economic development, environment, culture and education. Presentations will
focus on northern research and tools that are relevant to address contemporary
concerns. The objectives also include the inauguration of an International
Arctic Women's Research Network. ***
