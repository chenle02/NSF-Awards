This action is for contunued partial support of the NAS/NRC Committee on
Seismology. The activities supported include: (1) the operations of a Working
Group on Seismic Networks, (2) study and reporting on seismic hazard analysis,
(3) the conduct of meetings of the Committee and its subgroups, (4) initiation
of additional studies, as appropriate, and (5) aiding in the establishment of
global seismological networks and data analysis facilities.
