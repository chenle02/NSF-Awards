This project will support the Prinicpal Investigator to spend one-half year of
his sabbatical leave at the University of Wales, Cardiff, United Kingdom. The
Principal Investigator will participate in joint research projects in the area
of creep and loss of prestress in masonry with Prof. David Lenczner of the
University of Wales, who is a world authority in these areas.
