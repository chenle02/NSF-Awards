Image processing is an important activity which impacts numerous research areas
in engineering, biology, physics and medicine. This equipment grant will upgrade
the facilities at the Image and Pattern Analysis Laboratory at the University of
Tennessee, which is actively involved in research dealing with image processing,
pattern recognition and robotics.
