An interconnection network of microprocessors will be provided for researchers
at the University of Pennsylvania for research in the Department of Electrical
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the study of routing
strategies for broadband networks.
