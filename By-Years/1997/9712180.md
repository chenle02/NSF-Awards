ECS-9712180 Fatehi The goal of this proposed project is to complete the
investigations required to write a full research proposal to develop a new
technique for U.S. power industry in order to improve the stability of power
system and to increase power transfer capacity of the existing transmission
lines. The result of this research will provide more economic and secure
electricity in the deregulated environment.
