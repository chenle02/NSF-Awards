ABSTRACT NCR-9416667 PI: Timothy Howes University of Michigan "Workable Internet
x.500 Directory" This is a two year project to develop a stand-alone Lightweight
Directory Access Protocol server. It uses a centroids-based distributed indexing
service that works within the X.500 framework. Its design is consistent with the
white pages directory service concept described in RFC 1588 which resulted from
the November 1993 IETF meeting sponsored by NSF.
