This award provides partial support for the third annual SUNYA Topology
conference held over the Columbus Day weekend, 1991. The conference focusses on
combinatorial group theory and low dimensional topology. The invited speakers
are Steve Gersten, Elia Rips, Joan Birman, Thomas Farrell, Allen Hatcher and
James McCool.
