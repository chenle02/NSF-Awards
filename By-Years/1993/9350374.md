9350374 Laurent Dr. Laurent's goal is to develop an interdisciplinary research
program to study information processing and circuit design in the brain, and
understand fundamental aspects of neural computation. Dr. Laurent's teaching
plans are to broaden the scope of Neurobiology teaching in the Caltech
Computation and Neural Systems graduate program, and to create a new
interdisciplinary course on Motor Systems.
