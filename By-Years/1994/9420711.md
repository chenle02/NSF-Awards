9420711 Niebaum This award adds sever higher education institutions to the
KANREN state network: Community Colleges: Fort Scott Community College
Hutchinson Community College Kansas City Kansas Community College Other Colleges
and Universities: Central College Donnely College Friends University Hesston
College Faculty and students at the new institutions join the existing KANREN
institutions in benefitting from access to the resources of the Internet,
including libraries and supercompuers. In addition, they are able to communicate
and collaborate with colleagues at other campuses in pursuit of research and
educational projects. The award provides partial support of the project for two
years.
