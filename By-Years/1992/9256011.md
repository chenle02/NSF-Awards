Concordia College seeks to initiate a four-week, residential, summer program
with follow-up sessions during the academic year in interdisciplinary science
for 24 students entering the eleventh and twelfth grades. The project provides
an opportunity for students to participate in research activities in biology,
chemistry, and physics under the guidance of research scientists at Concordia
College.
