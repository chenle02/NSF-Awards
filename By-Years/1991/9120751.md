Hood College is establishing a connection to NSFNET via a 56,000 bits per second
line through the SURANET mid-level network. The connection enhances
collaborations of campus scientists with colleagues in fields including biology,
chemistry, physics and astronomy. This award funds part of the costs for two
years.
