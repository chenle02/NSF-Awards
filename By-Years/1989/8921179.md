A 4096-Processor Connection Machine will be provided for researchers at the
University of Massachusetts Amherst for research in the Department of Computer
and Information Science. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation
Grants for Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program.
The research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of image
understanding, neural computation and learning, programming environments for
massively parallel computation, theoretical aspects of parallel architectures.
