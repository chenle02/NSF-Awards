9308053 Mezzacappa This three year plan of postdoctoral study will allow Ms.
Mezzacappa, through extensive training in psychophysiological measurement, the
opportunity to develop an important research program toward understanding the
influence of group cohesiveness and threats of punishment for non-conformity on
likelihood of conformity and stress responses under a group pressure situation.
