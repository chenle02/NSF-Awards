This award is for the support of the U.S. Panel on Wind and Seismic Effects. The
Panel is comprised of 15 agencies participating in various tasks committees
which focus on specific issues such as earthquake hazard reduction. Annual
meetings alternate between Japan and the U.S. The National Bureau of Standards,
Center for Building Technology, provides the Secretatiat of the U.S. Panel.
