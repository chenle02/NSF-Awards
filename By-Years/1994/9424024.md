9424024 Heller A new "Research Experiences for Undergraduates" Site will be
initiated in the Physics Department of the University of Minnesota. Recruitment
will be conducted nationally, with a special emphasis on women and minority
students. The Site will provide consequential involvement in research in a
number of areas of physics. There will be a number of professional events
organized for the participants. ***
