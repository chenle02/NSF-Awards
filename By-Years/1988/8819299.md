This grant provides a state-of-the-art MicroVAX 3500 computer system for use by
the Kent State University Intermediate Energy Nuclear Physics faculty and
students involved in NSF sponsored research. Essential features of the system
include: fast processing time for large scale data replay, line-shape fitting,
and analysis codes; large memory; high speed communications; and multi-user
environment.
