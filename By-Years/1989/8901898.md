It is proposed to hold the Fifth Interdisciplinary Laser Science Conference in
Stanford, California August 27-31, 1989. Topics to be covered include (1) lasers
and coherent sources, (2) nonlinear optical phenomena and applications, (3)
atomic, molecular, and ionic spectroscopy, (4) condensed matter, surface, and
particle spectroscopy, (5) laser photophysics, photochemistry and photobiology,
and (6) diagnostic and analytical applications of lasers.
