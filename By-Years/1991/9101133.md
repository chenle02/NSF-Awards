This award provides travel grants for Americans to attend a workshop on
Operating Systems for the 90's and Beyond to be held in Germany. An organizing
group from the U.S. and Europe has set a list of topics, selected key
presenters, arranged funding for travel and lodging in Europe, and arranged for
sponsorship by several professional societies and industrial concerns. The
Proceedings will be published.
