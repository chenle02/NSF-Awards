This workshop is the annual IDM (Information and Data Management) Program's
Principal Investigator meeting. The IDM principal investigators as well as
invited visitors from academia, industry and government attend this workshop.
The purpose of this workshop is to review the progress made during the past year
and formulate strategy for IDM research. The abstracts of projects funded by IDM
are compiled and distributed at the workshop. The workshop consists of invited
presentations, panels and breakout sessions. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
