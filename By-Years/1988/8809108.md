Dr. Tarek Sammakia has been recommended for a Postdoctoral Research Fellowship
in Chemistry Award. Dr. Sammakia received his doctoral degree from Yale
University under the supervision of Professor Stuart L. Schreiber. He intends to
continue his research at Harvard University under the sponsorship of Professor
David A. Evans. Dr. Sammakia's area of Postdoctoral Research is the recognition
of prochiral organic molecules by chiral organometallic complexes; applications
to catalytic asymmetric synthesis.
