9320735 Buizer U.S. preparations for and participation in international
initiatives related directly to the U.S. Global Change Research Program (USGCRP)
will be supported. Specific activities to be undertaken include: operations of
the Program Office for the USGCRP; preparation, translation and distribution of
documentation for the USGCRP; and travel -- all of these associated with the
implementation of the USGCRP's international programmatic and research-policy-
related initiatives. These initiatives include participation and providing
staffing for the International Group of Funding Agencies for Global Change
Research (IGFA) which seeks to improve and expand national participation in and
support of international global change research programs.
