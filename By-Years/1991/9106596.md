This grant to Drexel University will be used to support travel for
international guests at a symposium on nuclear superdeformation. The symposium
is scheduled to coincide with the awarding of the John Price Wetherill Medal of
the Franklin Institute to Professor Peter Twin for his leadership in the
discovery of nuclear superdeformation.
