This is a Research Experiences for Undergraduates project. It will provide
research experiences for eight students at the University of Florida Whitney
Laboratories. Students will participate in projects involving: localization and
quantitation of the enzyme glutamine synthetase in lower chordates and related
invertebrates and oocyte growth in nonmammaliam vertebrates.
