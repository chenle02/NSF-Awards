This Special Minority Award will give the graduate student additional
flexibility in pursuing her graduate studies and research initiation in Physics.
Her research interests include during the first year core physics in Classical
Mechanics, Electricity and Magnetism, and Mathematical Methods, fields which are
strong at the University of Minnesota. This award will strengthen minority
research participation in Physics.
