Abstract Arctic Research Commission OPP-9714570 This is the Interagency
Agreement between the National Science Foundation and the Arctic Research
Commission and provides for its continuing operations. The Arctic Research
Commission was established under the Arctic Research Policy Act of 1984 (Public
Law 98-373) and Executive Order 12501. The General Services Administration will
provide administrative services. This action fulfills the intent of Congress to
provide funding for the Commission through the NSF.
