This award is for student travel support for attending the 36th International
Symposium on Distributed Computing (DISC) in Augusta, Georgia, October 25-27,
2022. DISC is a premier conference on distributed computing with an emphasis on
theoretical foundations, modeling, and analysis of distributed systems. This
grant will help to broaden the student participation in the conference and
expose the students to state-of-the-art research on distributed computing.
Emphasis is given to students from underrepresented population groups. The grant
aims to support 20 students.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This award reflects NSF's
statutory mission and has been deemed worthy of support through evaluation using
the Foundation's intellectual merit and broader impacts review criteria.
