Students are gaining a better understanding of calculus from practical classroom
demonstrations and from problem-solving experience gained in a laboratory
setting. They are accomplishing this using a computer laboratory with networked
IBM PS/2 computers running the computer algebra system MAPLE. The institution
will provide funds to match this grant.
