This project will provide travel support for a U.S. Delegation of highly
qualified earthquake engineering researchers to attend and participate in the
10th World Conference on Earthquake Engineering (10WCEE), July 19-24. 1992, in
Madrid, Spain. As recognized leaders in the field of earthquake engineering,
U.S. investigators supported by this project will present results of their
current research work at this important international forum.
