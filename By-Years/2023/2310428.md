This award supports the 2023 Fundamentals of Quantum Materials (FQM) Winter
School and Workshop at the University of Maryland. This meeting is dedicated
specifically to the synthesis, characterization and electronic modelling of
quantum materials.

This award reflects NSF's statutory mission and has been deemed worthy of
support through evaluation using the Foundation's intellectual merit and broader
impacts review criteria.
