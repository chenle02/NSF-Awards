The PI requests funds for a CUEBS (Commission on Undergraduate Education in the
Biological Sciences) Panel to be held at the AIBS annual meeting in San Antonio,
Texas, August 4-8, 1991. This panel will be composed of individuals who were
members of the original commission, participated in CUEBS-sponsored workshops
and/or were authors of various CUEBS publications. The topics to be addressed
are: Biology in a liberal education, Biology for non-majors, the role of modules
and technology in biological education, the laboratory as a place to
investigate, and preparing scientists to become educators.
