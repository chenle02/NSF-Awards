This award is to support travel to Taipei, Taiwan for Armando de la Cruz,
Mississippi State University to develop a research program with Taiwan on
allelopathy in mangrove plants. The project is supported under the cooperative
science program between the American Institute in Taiwan (AIT) and the
Coordination Council for North American Affairs (CCNAA). The National Science
Foundation manages the program on behalf of AIT.
