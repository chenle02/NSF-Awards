This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduate site proposal to
provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in the marine sciences,
in order to acquaint them with the excitement and opportunities of academic
research and to encourage them to contemplate going on to graduate studies and
eventually careers in science research.
