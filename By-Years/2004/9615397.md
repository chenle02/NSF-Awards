ABSTRACT CTS-9615397 Award of $752 to cover round-trip airfare between Kansas
City and Zurich for Dr. Bala Subramaniam is recommended. Dr. Subramaniam will be
presenting a paper on catalysis in supercritical media at the Third
International Symposium on High Pressure Chemical Engineering on October 7. This
paper is based on work initiated in this program and later supported by EPSCoR.
This effort is now being supported in the joint NSF/EPA program on Technology
for a Sustainable Environment.
