This award provides funding for a three year continuing award to Polytechnic
University of New York for the support of a Research Experiences for Teachers
Site in Mechatronics under the direction of Dr. Vikram Kapila. This program will
provide ten pre-college teachers from New York City school districts with the
opportunity to promote and enhance their technical competency and professional
development in the areas of science, technology, engineering and mathematics,
specifically in the exciting field of mechatronics.
