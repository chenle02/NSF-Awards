* Condition Evaluation of Concrete Structures Using           High-Frequency Ultrasound

This project will investigate the feasibility of using high frequency ultra-
sound techniques to determine flaws, rebars, and strength of concrete. The
results and techniques will be useful to determine the condition of existing
concrete structures.
