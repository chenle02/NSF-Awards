The ultimate goals of the project are: cooperative course development activities
by at least 144 faculty in mathematics and non-mathematics departments in at
least eight colleges and universities in Nebraska and Oklahoma. these activities
will bring together the best features of the mathematics reform and the
multimedia activities in all of the consortium colleges. these activities will
provide both mathematics and non-mathematics faculty with| new math
reform/multimedia lessons, curricula and courses that can be offered across the
curriculum and across the nation.
