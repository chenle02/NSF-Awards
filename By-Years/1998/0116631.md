This project proposes to develop an application delivery system, in
collaboration with the Universidad Nacional Autonoma de Mexico (UNAM), which
will provide real-time distance learning opportunities. This system will be
created by:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(1) Establishing a high-speed, high-bandwidth
Internet2 gateway at the Texas A&amp;M University (TAMU) Center at
UNAM&lt;br/&gt;(2) Connecting the Texas A&amp;M University Virtual Networking
Lab to the gateway&lt;br/&gt;(3) Linking distance learning studios at Texas
A&amp;M University and UNAM&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The user population for the
project is as diverse as the institution's populations and
includes:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;(1) Texas A&amp;M University Educational
Broadcasting Services&lt;br/&gt;(2) Texas A&amp;M University Architecture Study
Abroad students and faculty&lt;br/&gt;(3) Texas A&amp;M University Latin
American Study Program&lt;br/&gt;(4) Texas A&amp;M University Chemistry
Department&lt;br/&gt;(5) DGSCA-UNAM&lt;br/&gt;(6) Mexican students in U.S.
continuing education programs&lt;br/&gt;(7) Mexican networking students and
personnel needing hands-on continuing networking education.&lt;br/&gt;
