This award is made as part of the FY 2012 Mathematical Sciences Postdoctoral
Research Fellowships Program. These fellowships support a research and training
plan at a host institution in the mathematical sciences, including applications
to other disciplines. The title of the research and training plan for this
fellowship to Alexander M. Zupan is "Bridge splitting of knots and hyperbolic
geometry." The host institution for the fellowship is University of Texas, and
the sponsoring scientist is Dr. Cameron Gordon.
