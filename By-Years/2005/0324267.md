This award provides financial support for twenty travel grants directed at
scientists fro lesser developed countries from former Soviet Union and Eastern
Europe to attend the XXII General Assembly of the IAPSO/IUGG in Sapporo, Japan,
June 30-July 11, 2003. The scientific topics o be presented and discussed
include: "State of the planet: Frontiers and Challenges" and "Earth Systems and
Global Change". The Union lecture will be delivered by Nobel Laureate Professor
Mario Molina on the "The Impact of Human Activities on the Chemistry of the
Atmosphere."
