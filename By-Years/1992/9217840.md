This award provides travel support for three invited speakers for the Yale Mintz
Memorial Symposium on Climate and Climate Change which will be held in
Jerusalem, December 28-31, 1992. The topics for the symposium spans a broad
spectrum of climate research areas, in keeping with the scientific interests and
breadth of Dr. Mintz.
