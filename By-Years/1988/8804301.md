This project focuses on small business distruption resulting from the October
1, 1987 Whittier Narrows California earthquake and the initial recovery plans of
a sample of affected businesses. The objectives of the project are to: (1)
identify the significant components of earthquake-related business disruption
and (2) develop a model identifying factors that impede or facilitate the
recovery process for businesses.
