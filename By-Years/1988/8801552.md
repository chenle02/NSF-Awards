"The Conference on Mechanisms of Gene Regulation and Development" will be held
in Arolla, Switzerland, August 14 through August 21, 1988. This meeting will
bring together scientists working with different approaches in an environment
conducive to discussions between scientists at all levels of their professional
careers.
