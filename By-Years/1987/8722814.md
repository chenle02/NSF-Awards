Columbia University holds two theory days each year, one in the spring and one
in the fall. Each theory day consists of four invited talks, an hour each in
duration. Approximately 125 people attend each theory day. Most of the
participants are from the New York City area. Current reserach results are given
rapid distribution. There is ample time for discussion. Research liasons are
forged and renewed.
