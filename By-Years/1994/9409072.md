9409072 Mook This grant provides partial support for the travel of selected US
participants to attend the FOURTH PAN AMERICAN CONGRESS OF APPLIED MECHANICS
that will be held at the Universidad del Salvador in Buenos Aires, Argentina,
January 3-6, 1995. Participants will be selected based on abstracts in all areas
of applied mechanics. The CONGRESSES contribute to the interactions among the
peoples in the Americas. ***
