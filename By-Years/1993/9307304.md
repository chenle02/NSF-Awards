Support is provided for the U. S. delegation to the Sixth Trilateral Symposium
on Catalysis to be held in Beijing on June 8- 11, 1993. This joint meeting,
which rotates among the U. S., China, and Japan, is acquiring increasing
importance in technological exchanges in the Pacific Rim.
