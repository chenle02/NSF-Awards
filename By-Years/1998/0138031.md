The proposal requests funding for partial support for the 2002 Meeting of the
Division of Particles and Fields (DPF2002) of the American Physical Society on
May 24-28, 2002 at the College of William and Mary, Williamsburg, VA. The funds
will be used to support graduate students and postdoctoral research associates
who might otherwise not be able to afford to attend the meeting.
