A high-speed, multichannel data acquisition and computation system is purchased.
The equipment is used to assist in research in the fields of noise control in
machines, propagation of sound across open terrain and through vegetation, and
the use of high intensity sound to collect submicron particulates from coal
combustion and in dust control of industrial processes.
