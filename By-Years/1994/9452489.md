9452489 Mansfield A computer laboratory for instruction in advanced mathematics
is being developed to be used for classroom instruction, homework assignments,
individually assigned projects, and supervised independent study. The laboratory
runs on Mathematica on Macintosh computers and is equipped with an overhead LED
projection system. Students working in the lab experiment and discover
mathematics for themselves. Courses are being redesigned to promote this
experimental side of mathematics.
