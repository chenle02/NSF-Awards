9320887 Ogden This award assists the regional network, MICHNet, in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks in the United States and to the rest of
the Internet. The award made under the guidelines published in the NSF Program
Solicitation 93-52, partially funds one T-3 connection over an anticipated
duration of 4 years.
