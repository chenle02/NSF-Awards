This proposal covers the administrative support for the 10 person Soviet
delegation's visit to Washington, DC in May 1988 for negotiation of the
intergovernmental U.S.- U.S.S.R. Agreement on Cooperation in the Field of Basic
Scientific Research. This project fulfills the program objective of advancing
scientific knowledge by enabling leading researchers in the US and the USSR to
combine complementary efforts and capabilities in areas of strong mutual
interest and competence in the field of basic scientific research on the basis
of equality, reciprocity, and mutuality of benefit. **//
