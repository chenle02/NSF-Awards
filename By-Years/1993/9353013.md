9353013 Opliger The Ohio State University Agricultural Technical Institute will
initiate a three week, residential Young Scholars project in agricultural
sciences for 36 students entering the 8th and 9th grades. This highly
interactive program stresses group work and collaboration in math and science
under the general focus of agricultural science. Project activities include
laboratory and field experiences with practicing scientists serving as mentors.
Career exploration and ethics of science activities are also provided. High
school teachers serve as mentors for follow up science projects. ***
