The purpose of this conference is to promote communications between
microelectronics educators, industry personnel, and government who are
interested in improving microelectronics education. Particular emphasis is given
to upgrading undergraduate microelectronic systems education in primarily
undergraduate institutions. This is the third annual conference with expanded
scope in topics, participants and activities.
