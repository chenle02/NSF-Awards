A hypercube-computer memory upgrade will be provided for researchers at Oklahoma
State University for research in the School of Electrical Computer Engineering.
This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in
Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which
the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of: Parallel Lp Algorithms for
Beam Forming Parallel Superquadric Description for Machine Vision Hypercube Ray
Tracer for Computer Graphics Hypercube Image Processor (HIP)
