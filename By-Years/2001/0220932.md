SIOSEIS is a software package used for enhancing and manipulating marine seismic
reflection and refraction data on UNIX computers. As with all software, SIOSEIS
must be enhanced and maintained as advances in computer technology and seismic
imaging are made.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
