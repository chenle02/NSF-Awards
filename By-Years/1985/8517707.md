* Curing Reaction-Processing-Structure Relationships of Thermosetting Polyester Resins

This research deals with the curing reaction-processing-structure relationship
of thermosetting unsaturated polyester resin and its composites. The project
will develop mathematical models for simulating such processing operations as
pultrusion and compression molding.
