This project to The Masonry Society and the Construction Research Center at the
University of Texas at Arlington will coordinate and conduct a series of
international seminars on unreinforced and reinforced masonry construction as
practiced in the U.S. and abroad. Foreign countries participating are
Yugoslavia, Czechoslovakia and Italy. Seminars will be conducted in Boston,
Washington, D.C., Chicago and Los Angeles.
