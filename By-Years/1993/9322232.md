9322232 Winter This award provides funds to Thomas Jefferson University to
establish an REU Site that will enable talented undergraduate students between
their junior and senior years to participate in biochemical/molecular biology
research and in a series of classroom experiences. The overall goal is to foster
an understanding and appreciation of what biochemists and molecular biologists
do. The result will be an increase in the pool of talented and motivated
students who intend to pursue careers in biological research. ***
