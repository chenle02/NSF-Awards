Molecular graphics provides state of the art training to undergraduates in
biochemistry, plant science, genetics, chemistry, animal science and nutrition.
Students' ability to understand the chemistry and function of molecules is
enriched by experiments which combine laboratory experiments with graphics
exploration of molecular interactions.
