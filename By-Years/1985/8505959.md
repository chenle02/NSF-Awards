Millimeter-wave instrumentation is acquired. This equipment has frequency
coverage in the 50-75 ghz range or 5 mm wave band at center frequency and is an
instrumentation system used to conduct experiments for open-boundary dielectric
systems now being analyzed theoretically by the researchers.
