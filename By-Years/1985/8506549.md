Advanced computer equipment is acquired in support of the current and future
research in earthquake engineering concerning structural safety at the Virginia
Polytechnic Institute (VPI). The computer equipment acquired consists of
important peripherals with features such as a 512 KB memory, 1.2 MB floppy disk
drives, 20 MB hard disk drive, color graphics and display, printer, FORTRAN
compiler, VM PC, word processor, and a math co-processor.
