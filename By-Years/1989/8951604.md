Acquisition of this computer workstation enhances the Computer Graphics
education at Potsdam College of the State University of New York. It also allows
computer graphics based techniques to significant role such as logic design and
simulation. With this capability the Computer Science Department added a new
course in computer animation which is designed to teach seniors the principles
of computer assisted 2-D animation. This award is being matched by an equal
amount from the principal investigator's institution.
