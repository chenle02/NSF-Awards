This award provides funds for international travel for Professor Orville C.
Sandall to attend the "International Symposium on Gas Separation Technology" in
Antwerp, Belgium (September 10-15, 1989). The awardee will also visit the
research laboratories of Professor Blauwhoff at the University of Twente and
Royal Dutch Shell in Amsterdam (Netherlands). Researchers from these
laboratories have worked in the University of California, Santa Barbara
laboratory of Professor Sandall.
