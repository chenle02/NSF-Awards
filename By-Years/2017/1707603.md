This award provides funding for the conference "Midwest Several Complex
Variables Conference 2017" that will be held at Brown University during April
7-9, 2017.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The conference focuses on recent developments in
Analysis, especially in the fields of Several Complex Variables and Operator
Theory. A number of distinguished mathematicians have agreed to attend and speak
at this conference. This award gives early career researchers, members of
underrepresented groups, researchers not funded by NSF and the like an
opportunity to attend and participate in this conference. The organizing
committee will strive to make this funding opportunity known to target groups
through a number of different activities. More information will be made
available at: https://sites.google.com/a/umich.edu/mw-scv-17/
