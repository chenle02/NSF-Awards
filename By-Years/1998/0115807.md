After nine years of this alliance, we have learned what the needs of our
students are, and acted to produced desired results. We will continue many of
the strategies put in place over the past nine years, since they are producing
excellent results!&lt;br/&gt;GOALS: The aims of the Mississippi Alliance in
Phase III will be to:&lt;br/&gt;1. Increase the number of SMET graduates from an
estimated 661 to 825 by 2007;&lt;br/&gt;2. Increase the number and percentage of
minority students enrolled in SMET;&lt;br/&gt;3. Increase retention rates of
minority students in SMET;&lt;br/&gt;4. Increase the annual graduation rate of
enrolled seniors;&lt;br/&gt;5. Track progression of baccalaureates into graduate
and professional programs and careers;&lt;br/&gt;6. Double the number of
baccalaureates enrolling into graduate programs in SMET by 2007;&lt;br/&gt;7.
Double the number of minority students who earn Ph.D. degrees in SMET by
2007;&lt;br/&gt;8. Develop institutional cultures that value
diversity;&lt;br/&gt;9. Forge partnerships with business and government across
the state and region;&lt;br/&gt;10. Leverage the federal dollars into
institutionalized programs;&lt;br/&gt;We will continue to emphasize
undergraduate education but will develop productive bridges to graduated
education.&lt;br/&gt;
