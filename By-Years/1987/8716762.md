This International Conference on Contemporary Topics in Nuclear Structures to be
held in Cocoyoc, Mexico, 9-14 June 1988 will bring together active researchers
to discuss current developments, to identify outstanding problems and explore
new methods of attack, and to assess future directions for research. The
conference is being organized to attract a large number of young scientists and
a significant representation from Latin American scientists.
