This award is to provide limited travel and subsistence for students from U. S.
universities to attend a NATO-sponsored Advanced Study Institute on "Dynamic
Modeling and Flow in the Earth and Planets". It will be held in Fairbanks,
Alaska, June 17-28, 1991
