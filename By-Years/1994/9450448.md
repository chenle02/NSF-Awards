9450448 The proposed research would concentrate on two main areas of
computational mathematics: ( 1.) Design and Implementation of Crystallographic
Fast Fourier Transforms ( 2.) Structure of Euclidean Distance Matrices and
Optimization Problems. The proposed research would strengthen the research
capabilities of the University of Puerto Rico at Mayaguez in the area of
computational mathematics.
