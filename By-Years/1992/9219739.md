This action is an accomplishment based renewal of EAR 90-18606 Jurassic
Extensional Arc-Continental Rift transition, Mojave-Sonoran Deserts. The work
involves study along the Sawmill Canyon fault zone in southern Arizona, that
shows the interrelations of grabens, calderas and basement structures in a
transtensional arc setting.
