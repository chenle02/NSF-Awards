This award supports travel by an investigator to conferences on river basin
management and water pollution research and control. At the conference on river
basin management, he will present a paper on water quality issues in natural
streams that are important to providing a suitable habitat for life-forms in the
stream. At the International Association on Water Pollution Research and
Control, he will initiate a task group activity relating to management of
contaminants in aquatic systems.
