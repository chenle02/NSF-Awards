9633005 Suarez This award is for the partial support of the Fifth Pan American
Congress of Applied Mechanics to be held at the University of Puerto Rico at
Mayaguez on January 2-4, 1997. Previous Congresses have been held in South
American and had outstanding success. Most of the funds will l be used to
support travel of the South American researchers to the Congress and for the
proceedings. All areas of applied mechanics will be covered at the meeting. ***
