Travel assistance is provided for 10 foreign experts and 10 U.S. graduate
students to attend an International Conference on Polymer Colloids to be held in
Racine, Wisconsin. The objectives of the conference are to discuss recent
advances in polymer colloid research and to formulate recommendations for future
research.
