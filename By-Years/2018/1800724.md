This award provides funding for US participation in the conference "Arizona
School of Analysis and Mathematical Physics" that will be held March 5-9, 2018
at the University of Arizona&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The conference focuses on
recent developments in Analysis, especially in the fields of mathematical
physics and partial differential equations. A number of distinguished
mathematicians have agreed to attend and speak at this conference. This award
gives early career researchers, members of underrepresented groups, researchers
not funded by NSF and the like an opportunity to attend and participate in this
conference. The organizing committee will strive to make this funding
opportunity known to target groups through a number of different activities.
More information will be made available
