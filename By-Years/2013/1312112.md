This project funds travel for graduate students to participate in PerCom 2013 to
be held San Diego, Californiain March 2013. PerCom is the flagship international
conference in pervasive computing. Students will participate in an organized PhD
Forum, both before and during the conference, demonstrate their work in a Poster
Session, give presentations about their research, and be mentored by senior
researchers.
