The meeting on "Regulation of Eukaryotic mRNA Transcription" will be held at
Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory in Cold Spring Harbor, New York. September 6-10,
1989. This meeting will ensure that the most recent results and methodologies in
this rapidly moving field are discussed in an atmosphere which encourages
interactions between established scientists and young students.
