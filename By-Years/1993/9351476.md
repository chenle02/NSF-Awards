This project will enhance UAB's undergraduate program in computerscience by
creating a new telecommunications laboratory to support a new specialization in
telecommunications. The term"telecommunications" is used in the broad sense to
include currentconcepts of information networking and distributed
computing,especially client-server heterogeneous applications.
