This Special Minority Award will give the graduate student additional
flexibility in pursuing his graduate studies and research initiation in Physics.
His research interests include Classical Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Quantum
Mechanics, and Relativity, leading to a career in Theoretical Physics--an area
in which the University of California, Berkeley, is strong. This award will
strengthen minority research participation in physics.
