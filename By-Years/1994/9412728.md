9412728 Gallagher This program supports graduate student attendance at the 1994
annual meeting of the Division of Atomic, Molecular and Optical Physics of the
American Physical Society, held in Crystal City, Virginia. Over 450 abstracts
have been recieved for the meeting and it represents the principal annual
gathering of physicists in the field of Atomic, Molecular and Optical Physics.
Attendance at the meeting is valuable to students and contributes to the
vitality of the meeting. ***
