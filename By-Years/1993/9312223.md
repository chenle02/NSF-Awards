9312223 Clowers This award connects Shoreline Community College to NSFNET
through a Washington state community college computer consortium. Faculty and
students at the institution will benefit from access to the resources of the
Internet, including libraries and supercomputers. In addition, they will be able
to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at other campuses in pursuit of
research and educational projects. The award provides partial support of the
project for two years. ***
