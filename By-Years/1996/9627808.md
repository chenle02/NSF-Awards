McElhinney 9627808 The PIs propose to construct a Global magnetostratigraphy
database as recommended by the International Association of Geomagnetism and
Aeronomy (IAGA). The PIs will incorporate data collected worldwide including the
former Soviet Union through a collaboration with Russian scientists.
