This project is an NYI award and is recommended with the challenge of improving
the nation's public works and infrastructure systems. Research will include the
investigation of new materials, such as fiber composites of all types, epoxy
bonded laminates to concrete surfaces, and their mechanical properties for
potential use in constructed facilities.
