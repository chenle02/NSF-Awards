9353853 Gillman Valparaiso University will initiate a four-week, residential
Young Scholars program in mathematics for students entering grades 9 and 10.
Students will explore group theory and combinatorics. Emphasis will be given to
hands-on exploration and use of concrete examples. Career activities and ethics
of science discussions are also featured. Field trips to local museums,
laboratories and industrial sites and four follow-up meetings complete the
program activities. ***
