This action provides round-trip air fare for an investigator to travel to
Sendai, Japan, to participate in and present a paper at the International
Symposium on Cavitation, April 16-19, 1986. His work on marine propeller noise
is recognized, and the prospect of gaining current international perspective at
first hand in the field of marine noise and cavitation is of national
importance.
