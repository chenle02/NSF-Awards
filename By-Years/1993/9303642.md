9303642 Kitten This is an individual award for the Postdoctoral Research
Fellowship in Plant Biology. The applicant received his Ph.D. from University of
Texas Health Science Center. His Ph.D. research was in the area of infectious
diseases in animal systems. He plans to carry out his postdoctoral fellowship
research in the laboratory of Dr. D. Kyle Willis at University of Wisconsin. The
proposed research entitled "Overproduction, isolation, and functional analysis
of the LemA protein in Pseudomonas syringae" will broaden the training of the
Fellow into plant biology in general and plant pathology in particular. ***
