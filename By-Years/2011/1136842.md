The Polar Research Board (PRB) within the National Academies serves as the US
Committee to the Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research (SCAR) and the
International Arctic Science Committee (IASC). This award covers the dues for
the US participation in the SCAR and the IASC. US participation in SCAR and IASC
gives the US a voice in international research and policies concerning the polar
regions.
