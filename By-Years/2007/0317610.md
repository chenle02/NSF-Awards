This proposal seeks funding to build on the strengths and successes of the
Unidata Program Center with an enhanced focus on the application of cutting-edge
technologies and tools for a broad community of scientists and educators, with
the overarching goal of transforming geoscience's education and research. The
proposed endeavors emphasize providing data and a rich set of tools, services,
and collaborative activities that will enable the community to advance
scientific exploration and learning. The goals, objectives, and activities in
this proposal are based on national science, technology, and education trends,
as well as community concerns and needs.
