This award seeks to partially support the registration of 18 undergraduate
students, graduate students, post-docs, and under-represented, female, junior
and retired faculty to attend and present at the symposium on Novel Approaches
to Manipulate and Detect 2D Magnetism in van der Waals Quantum and Topological
Materials at the Spring Materials Research Society (MRS) national meeting in San
Francisco, California from April 10th to 14th (in-person) and April 25th to
27th, 2023 (virtual).

This award reflects NSF's statutory mission and has been deemed worthy of
support through evaluation using the Foundation's intellectual merit and broader
impacts review criteria.
