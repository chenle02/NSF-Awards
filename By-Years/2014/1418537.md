Abstract&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;1418537&lt;br/&gt;Jane P.
Chang&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The proposed grant is for partially offsetting costs
for graduate students and post-docs to attend the Gordon Research Conference on
Plasma Processing Science (GRC-PPS, to be held July 27-August 1, 2014 at Bryant
University, Smithville, RI. The conference will bring together leaders in the
field with junior investigators and graduate students. The special format of the
Gordon Conferences, with programmed discussion sessions and ample time for
informal gatherings in the afternoons and evenings, will provide&lt;br/&gt;for a
fertile atmosphere of brainstorming and creative thinking among the attendees.
