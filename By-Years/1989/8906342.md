Funds are provided for the acquisition of a modern computer- controlled x-ray
diffractometer with vacuum hot stage. The equipment will be used in research
projects on texture and residual stress in thin films, on long range order in
intermetallic compounds and on phase relationships and microstructures in
advanced ceramics.
