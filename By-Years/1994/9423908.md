CUHEL 9423908 This project is a renewal of an on-going REU SITE in environmental
aquatic science research. The research opportunities include studies in the
chemistry, geology and/or biology of lakes and oceans. The combined activities
provide the students with an appreciation of many of the elements encountered in
a career in aquatic sciences.
