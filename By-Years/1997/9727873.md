This award is made under the innovative technology connections portion of NCRI's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64, which covers K-12
education institutions (including vocational technical schools), public
libraries and museums. It provides partial support for 24 months to connect an
elementary school and a middle school to the Internet using microwave
technology. The wireless link will connect to a high school, which has a fiber
connection to the Internet.
