This workshop at the Institute for Computational and Experimental Research in
Mathematics (ICERM) at Brown University brings together researchers from several
disciplines, who are interested in a encompassing mathematical foundation for
cyber security. The results of the workshop will be made widely available
through archival publications and online at ICERM.
