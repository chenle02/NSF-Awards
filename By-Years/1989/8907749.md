A "Supercomputer Prototype System" will be provided for researchers at the
University of Colorado for research in the Department of Electrical and Computer
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering Program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the area of state-of-
the-art logic synthesis applications
