This award supports the 16th Annual NSF Grantee's Conference on Manufacturing
Systems Research at the Arizona State University College of Engineering and
Applied Sciences. The five day conference will provide an opportunity for
grantees and other invited guests to exchange ideas and research results with
the aim of enhancing the overall research effort, reducing duplication and
encouraging cooperation among the participants. The conference dates are January
8-12, 1990.//
