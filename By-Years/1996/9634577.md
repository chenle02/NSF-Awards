ABSTRACT Proposal Number: CTS 9634577 PI: Dudukovic This is a grant for partial
support of the Engineering Foundation Conference on Computational Fluid Dynamics
in Chemical Reaction Engineering to be held in San Diego, CA on October 13-18.
It is anticipated that the conference will be significant examination of the
role of CFD chemical reaction engineering. The focus of the conference is on:
Frontier orientation State of the art with emphasis on future trends Interface
between academic discoveries and industrial practice
