A supermini computer will be acquired, along with increased networking capacity,
and a molecular graphics workstation, for use in structural biology. A wide
variety of projects in virus structure and other problems in contemporary
biotechnology research will be enhanced by the additonal capacity for analysis
of supramolecular structures at atomic resolution.
