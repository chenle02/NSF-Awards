9418364 Kroenke This project provides funding for an evaluator to study the
industry/university interactions occurring in the University of New Mexico's
Industry/University Cooperative Research Center for Micro- Engineered Ceramics.
The Center continues to meet the renewal criteria to remain in the
Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers Program. The evaluator has the
experience necessary to perform the study. The Program Manager recommends Dr.
William Kroenke be awarded $24,000 for three years for this evaluation study.
