9406193 Rocke The Graduate School of Management at the University of California,
Davis will purchase two workstations that will be dedicated to the support of
research in the mathematical sciences. The equipment will be used for several
projects, including in particular: 1.) Detection of Multivariate Outliers; 2.)
Robust Model Selection; and 3.) Tabu Search and Chunking.
