This fellowship will support significant expansion of and innovation in a
research, teaching, and service program at Mount Holyoke College. The Fellow
will synthesize teaching and research activities in three key areas, tropical
marine ecology, a multidisciplinary program in environmental studies, and
integration of computer technologies into existing biology and environmental
studies curricula.
