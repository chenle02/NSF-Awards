The proposal is for theoretical studies of heavy ion collisions for energies
from medium to ultrarelativistic. The primary goal is to investigate hadronic
matter as a function of excitation energy and density. Non-equilibrium processes
are discussed in the frame work of the Fokker-Planck equation.
