A 16-node hypercube computer will be provided for researchers at Illinois
Institute of Technology for research in the Department of Computer Science. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The equipment will be used for
several research projects, including in particular: Message Driven Computing
Natural Robotics Distributed Algorithms
