This award supports travel for participants from the US to China in the
conference "Second International Conference on Random Dynamical Systems", held
20-22 June 2011 at Nanjing Normal University, Nanjing, China. The conference
will bring together leading experts in Stochastic Partial Differential equations
and Random Dynamical Systems. The conference will enhance communication among
junior and senior researchers in these two fields. The conference encourages and
supports participation by graduate students, junior researchers and members of
under-represented groups. Conference web site:
http://math.njnu.edu.cn/web/home.html
