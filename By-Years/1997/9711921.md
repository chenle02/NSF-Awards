This award is for travel support for the April 7-11,1997 IEEE INFOCOM
International Conference on Computer Communications held in Kobe, Japan. This
annual international conference is jointly sponsored by IEEE Computer and
Communications Societies. INFOCOM has become a premier meeting for examining
research advances made in the state-of-the-art computer communications
technology. INFOCOM addresses research in multimedia, networking architectures
and protocols; network design, planning and management; photonic switching and
optical networks; protocol design, analysis, and specification; congestion and
flow control; network security and privacy; distributed systems and network
algorithms; internetworking and interoperability; and high speed networks.
