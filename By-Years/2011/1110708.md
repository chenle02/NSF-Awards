This award assists approximately 20 US-based graduate students to travel and
attend the Eighth Symposium on Networked Systems Design and Implementation
(NSDI-2011), which is to be held in Boston, MA on March 30, 2011. This support
enables the participation of students who would otherwise be unable to attend
NSDI-2011.
