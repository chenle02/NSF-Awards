This award provides partial support for the fourth annual SUNYA Topology and
Group Theory conference, October 16-18, 1992. The conference concentrates on
combinatorial group theory and low dimensional topology. The invited speakers
are Martin Bridson, Andrew Casson, Marshall Cohen, Martin Dunwoody, Louis
Kauffman, Steven Pride, and Richard Skora.
