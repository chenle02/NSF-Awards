9703802 Greene This award to San Jose State University Foundation provides
shipboard technical support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and
instrument calibration for researchers using R/V Point Sur, a research vessel
operated by the Moss Landing Marine Laboratories as part of the University
National Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet. The technical support
awarded here will assist NSF-funded ocean researchers conduct studies in the
eastern North Pacific Ocean in 1997. ***
