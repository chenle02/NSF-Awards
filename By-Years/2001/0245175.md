This proposal is a request for support to help cover the costs of organizing the
conference "Quantum Field Theory Under the Influence of External Conditions". It
will take place from September 15-19, 2003 in Norman Oklahoma on the University
of Oklahoma Campus. The funds will be used entirely to support graduate
students, Post-docs and junior faculty who otherwise could not afford to attend
the meeting.
