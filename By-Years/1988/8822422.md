This is the Eighth Dissertations Symposium on Chemical Oceanography to be held
in November, 1988. Twenty-six chemical oceanographers who have recently or have
nearly received their PhD degree present their theses dissertations, contribute
to discussions, participate in working groups and meet with other attendees and
sponsors to discuss their work and future activities. The symposium is sponsored
by ONR, NSF and NOAA.
