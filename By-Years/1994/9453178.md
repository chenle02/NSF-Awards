9453178 Royster Kentucky Science and Technology Council will host a one-day
regional conference focused on educational policy issues critical to systemic
reform in rural, poor school districts. The participants will feature state
superintendents of education and other policy-makers in the six states involved
with the Appalachia Rural Systemic Initiative. Expected outcomes are an
increased understanding of systemic reform in isolated schools; and educational
policy commitments by the participating states. ***
