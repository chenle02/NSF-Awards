A multiprocessing system will be provided for researchers at the University of
Missouri - Rolla for research in the Department of Computer Science. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which the
equipment is to be used will be in the areas of, 1) The Application Oriented
Fault-Tolerance Paradigm, 2) Application of Parallel Automated Reasoning to
Diagnosis, 3) An Intelligent Example-Generator for Graph Theory, 4) Development
of a Versatile Communication Mechanism and its Language Support, and 5) Parallel
Computer Operating System Development.
