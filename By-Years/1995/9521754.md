9521754 Stone The Woods Hole Research Center requests support from NSF for a
connection to the Internet through the WHOInet network. WHOInet is the network
located in the state of Massachusetts that will provide operations and network
information services. It will provide the Woods Hole Research Center with a
connection to the Internet, a collection of interoperable networks that connect
most research and education organizations in the United States The center needs
the Internet connection to be able to access information resources throughout
the state, the nation and the world. The connection will benefit the staff of
the Woods Hole Research Center.
