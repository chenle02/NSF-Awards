Partial support is provided for conducting the "Fourth International Conference
on the Manufacturing Science and Technology of the Future" to be held at MIT on
June 22-25, 1987. The purpose of this conference is to serve as a forum for
leading researchers to pool their knowledge of advanced manufacturing
technologies and to determine the future direction of research and development.
To achieve this goal, outstanding research leaders from key industrial nationas
will be invited to be speakers and session chairmen at the conference.
