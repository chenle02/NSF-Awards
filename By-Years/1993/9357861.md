9357861 Zank This new National Science Foundation Young investigator will
develop a basic understanding of the complex, non-linear interactions between
fully developed small-scale turbulence and large scale compressible and
apparently "laminar" phenomena by investigating a series of relevant, well-posed
and important problems in solar, space, and astrophysics. ***
