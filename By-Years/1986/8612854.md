This project is designed to introduce undergraduate students to the problems of
practical engineering and the interface of engineering with social and economic
considerations. The project is a unique feature of the eductional curriculum at
Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI). The project will investigate the social,
economic, and engineering management considerations concerning the possible sale
of the Federal Airports at National and Dulles to a private organization. The
study will provide a basis for research activities involving public works.
