The Department of Mathematics at the University of Maryland, College Park, will
purchase equipment and personnel to support research in the mathematical
sciences. The equipment will be used to study: (i) Systems of Conservation Laws,
Computational Fluid Dynamics and Turbulence, (ii) Polyhedra in non- Euclidean
geometries.
