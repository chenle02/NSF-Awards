This award provides support for participants in the Grid Summer Workshop to be
hosted by the University of Texas at Brownsville on South Padre Island (TX) from
June 21 to June 25, 2004. The goal of this summer school is to provide intensive
immersion in Grid computing and its application in scientific data analysis. The
Workshop is open to graduate and undergraduate students in Computer Science,
Physics, and Mathematics.
