This grant provides partial support for the participation of advanced graduate
students and postdocs in the 6th International Conference on Nucleus-Nucleus
Collisions, to be held in Gatlinburg, Tennessee, June 2-6, 1997. The Conference
is the major IUPAP-sponsored topical conference on nucleus-nucleus collisions
covering the entire available energy domain from close to the Coulomb barrier to
ultra- relativistic energies.
