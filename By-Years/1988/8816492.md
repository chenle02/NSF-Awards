This award will enable the 1988 Presidential Young Investigators (PYI's) in
Chemistry to meet for a workshop at the annual meeting of the Council for
Chemical Research (CCR) to be held in St. Louis, Mo. October 9-11,1988. The
workshop is specifically designed to aid the awardees' quest for matching funds
from industry. Additionally,the PYI's will have the opportunity to interact with
each other and to publicize their research programs. The award will provide for
lodging, meals and travel. Overhead and any additional costs in excess of the
award will be borne by CCR.
