The Conference on Strategic Directions in Computing Research is timely and
important for computing related disciplines. The results of the conference will
assist academic, industrial, and governmental organizations in making the
difficult decisions of which research directions to pursue in the next few
years. As a result, the conference should have a positive impact on a wide range
of computer- oriented research at a variety of levels.
