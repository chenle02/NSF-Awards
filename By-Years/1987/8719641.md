This travel grant provides for Dr. David Fowler, of the University of Texas,
Austin, to present a paper, "Polymer Concrete for Construction and Repair of
Bridges", representing the American Concrete Institute Committee 548 at the
International Association for Bridge and Structural Engineering (IABSE) at the
Paris, France, meeting on September 2-4, 1987. Dr. Fowler will also present
papers in Brighton, England, on September 21-24, 1987, at the International
Congress on Polymers in Concrete, of which he is currently president.
