This is a workshop to bring together researchers and designers from academia and
industry to discuss possible directions in the development of high-performance,
parallel, distributed, and multimedia processors and systems. The topics center
around systems on a chip and their utilization in building larger systems. The
participants include designers of processors, compilers and operating systems,
together with application experts. The wide representation will allow rich
interaction concerning ideas and emphasis in the field.
