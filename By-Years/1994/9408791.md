9408791 Weingarten The Computing Research Association requests partial support
for a joint industry, academic, and government technical symposium to explore
and plan the R&amp;D agenda for the National Information Infrastructure (NII).
The symposium is a joint project of several industry and academic groups and is
undertaken in cooperation with the National Institute of Standards and
Technology and the Committee on Technology of the Applications Committee of the
government's Information Infrastructure Task Force. Funds will be used to
partially defray travel costs of the academic attendees and to pay for post
forum editing and costs for disseminating the resulting report.
