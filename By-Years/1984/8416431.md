Support will be provided to the International Symposium/Workshop on Northern
Basin Hydrology, entitled 'Field Measurements under Winter Conditions,' which
will be held in February 1986 at the Michigan Technological University.
Approximately half of the sessions will deal with winter discharge measurements,
while the remainder will cover snow, soil and related hydrometeorological
parameters. The symposium will bring together scientists from across the world.
