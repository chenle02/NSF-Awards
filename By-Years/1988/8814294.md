It is the purpose of this contract to procure, deploy and maintain Automatic
Geophysical Stations for research use in Antarctica. The contract is for two
totally self-contained, autonomous, unmanned stations, complete with data
recording systems and satellite data links. The contract allows for additional
priced options for the procurement of up to a total of six such stations.
