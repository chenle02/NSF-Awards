9531599 MACKIERNAN This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduate
(REU) SITE to provide research experiences for summer fellows to participate in
interdisciplinary research programs which examine the physical and biological
functioning of estuarine ecosystems. It will acquaint the students with the
excitement and opportunities of academic research, and encourage them to pursue
graduate studies and a career in ocean sciences ***
