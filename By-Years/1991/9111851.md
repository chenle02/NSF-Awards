The objective of this conference is to provide a forum for food engineers in
academia and industry to discuss present developments and future directions,
exchange ideas and experiences, demonstrate gaps, and recognize challenges. The
key features of this conference are: plenary lectures, symposia, interactive
poster sessions, and workshops. Reports from individual sessions will be
combined into a final report of the conference.
