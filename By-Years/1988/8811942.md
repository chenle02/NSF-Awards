A file server system for an Information Systems Laboratory will be provided for
researchers at Stanford University in the Department of Electrical Engineering.
This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in
Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which
the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of optical processing,
communication channels, signal quantization and compression, and related areas.
