This research equipment award will be used to purchase a Resonant Column
Device, a soil testing device. The dynamic response of soils will be studied.
Application include earthquakes, vibrating machinery foundations, blast effects
on foundations, grout distribution, and in general soils and foundations
subjected to dynamic loads.
