This statement of work (SOW) is in support of the Four-Dimensional Weather
(4DWX) System, a weather-modeling and data-processing system that ATEC runs for
operational support of DOD testing. This document describes the research and
development (R&amp;D), technology transfer, and operations and maintenance
(O&amp;M) that the U.S. Army Test and Evaluation Command (ATEC or "government")
requires from the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) in fiscal year
(FY) 2019.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This award reflects NSF's statutory mission and
has been deemed worthy of support through evaluation using the Foundation's
intellectual merit and broader impacts review criteria.
