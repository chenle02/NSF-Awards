This award to Dusquene University Center for Pharmaceutical Technology (DCPT) is
to plan to join the Industry/University Cooperative Research Center (I/UCRC) for
Pharmaceutical Processing Research (CPPR), a multi-university center that
includes Purdue University, the University of Connecticut, the University of
Puerto Rico, and the University of Minnesota.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The purposes
of the Center are to explore and develop new technology for pharmaceutical
processing, to foster collaborative research projects between academic and
industrial scientists, and to promote an interdisciplinary approach to training
students in pharmaceutical processing research and
development.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
