This award funds the first year of a three-year continuing award to study the
university/industry interactions occurring in the University of Iowa's
Industry/University Cooperative Research Center for Simulation and Design
Optimization of Mechanical Systems. The evaluator performing this study has the
background and experience to perform the necessary activities. The Program
Manager recommends the University of Iowa be awarded $8,000 for the first year
of a three-year continuing award.
