Biometrics is an emerging critical area for homeland security. The proposed
effort will conduct a study of biometrics. In particular, the following
activities will be carried out:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Survey the state-of-the-art
in Biometrics&lt;br/&gt;Examine the current biometrics technologies and
determine areas for future work&lt;br/&gt;Identify the needs of the government
in biometrics&lt;br/&gt;Produce a report that summarizes the findings.
&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The participants of this study will be from academia,
industry and government who are experts in the field.
