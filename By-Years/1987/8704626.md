The purpose of the research is to investigate the relationship between surface
roughness and wear of a magnetic medium in contact with a recording head, in a
magnetic recording device. The funds will be used towards the purchase of a
stylus profilometer for surface roughness evaluation. The equipment is necessary
to complete the objectives of the research program.
