This award provides for the purchase of a NMR spectrometer for the use of
members of the Department of Chemistry. It will be used in their research into
the chemistry of naturally occurring compounds, coal liquefication, and
biochemical reactions. The instrument will broaden the educational opportunities
for undergraduate students.
