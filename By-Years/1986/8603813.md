This group travel award provides support for U.S. participation in the Seventh
World Clean Air Congress in Sydney, Australia from August 25-29, 1986. Persons
who will be supported by this Group Travel award will include the President and
the Executive Vice-President of the Air Pollution Control Association, and the
U.S. Program Chairman for the Seventh Congress.
