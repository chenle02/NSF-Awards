9712348 Ruscher This grant will establish a permanent Internet educational site
devoted to fostering a better understanding of available data resources
collected operationally in the atmospheric sciences. This site will be
established as a permanent, unrestricted world-wide web (WWW) location open to
all with an interest or need for information on upper air meteorological
observations.
