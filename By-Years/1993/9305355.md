The department of mathematical sciences at Worcester Polytechnic Institute will
purchase computer equipment which will be dedicated to support research in the
mathematical sciences. The equipment will be used for several research projects.
In particular: 1) CFD and turbulence. 2) Clear Air turbulence. 3) The design of
high performance parachutes. 4) Multicriteria Optimal Shape Design.
