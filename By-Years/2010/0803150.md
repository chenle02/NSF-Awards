This Communicating Research to Public Audiences (NSF 03-509) project in
partnership with the American Museum of Natural History seeks to increase public
understanding of research being conducted at the Pierre Auger Observatory that
will be shown in 22 science centers and available on the web, as well as live
interactive presentations by the lead researchers.
