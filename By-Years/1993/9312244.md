9312244 Teague This award is for continued support of the U.S. Solar Terrestrial
Energy Program (STEP) Coordination Office (USSCO). The USSCO interacts with the
international space science community for the purpose of (1) collecting and
disseminating STEP-related information and data, (2) coordinating joint
campaigns for data acquisition and analysis, and (3) organizing professional
society meetings and special sessions. USSCO activities are crucial for
coordinating the various federal agency activities related to STEP with the
international scientific community. It also facilitates communication of the
STEP activities to the U.S. community. ***
