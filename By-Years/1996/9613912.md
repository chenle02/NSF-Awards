This award is made under the innovative technology connections portion of
NCRI's "Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64, which covers K-12
education institutions (including vocational technical schools), public
libraries and museums. It provides partial support for one year to complete a
cable television-based Internet access system for six inner-city schools.
