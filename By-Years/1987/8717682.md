This project involves travel support for three U.S. computer science professors
to attend the IEEE Eleventh Annual International Computer Software & Application
Conference (COMPSAC) to be held in Tokyo, Japan on October 7-9, 1987. The
objectives of the trip to attend COMPSAC 87 in Japan are: (a) to present papers
and chair session at the conference. (b) to interact with computer scientists
outside USA, especially those in Japan. (c) to increase the understanding of
computer education and technology in Japan. (d) to seek joint research between
computer scientists in USA and Japan.
