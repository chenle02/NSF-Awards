This grant is for support of students to attend the 1996 Gaseous Electronics
Conference at Argonne National Laboratory, October 20-23, 1996. This conference
is the major source of cross fertilization between the atomic, molecular, and
optical physics community, the low-energy plasma physics community and industry.
