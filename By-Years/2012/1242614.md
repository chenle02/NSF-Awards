The PIs and Co-PIs of grants supported through the NSF-NIH-BMBF Collaborative
Research in Computational Neuroscience (CRCNS) program meet annually. This
eighth meeting of CRCNS investigators brings together a broad spectrum of
computational neuroscience researchers supported by the program, and includes
poster presentations, talks, plenary lectures, and discussions. The meeting is
scheduled for June 3-5, 2012 and is hosted by Washington University in Saint
Louis.
