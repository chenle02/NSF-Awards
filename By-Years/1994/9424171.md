9424171 Winful The REU Site at the Center for Ultrafast Optical Science proposes
to involve six new students per year in research in our laboratories. Program
Participants will collaborate on project that apply the unique capabilities of
intense, ultrashort optical pulses in the field of engineering, applied physics,
and chemistry. ***
