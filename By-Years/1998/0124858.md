The proposal plans to connect Marist to NYSERNet and then to the vBNS+ Network
for the purpose of enabling the following research: Remote Microscopic
Visualization in Support of Polar Microbial Research; Advanced Brain Imaging;
Functional Imaging Archive; MRI Vertebrate Development Analysis; Addictions
Research; Computational Study of Chemical Bonding Across the Entire Periodic
Table; Finite and Infinite Group Theory; Pedagogical Research in Mathematics and
Writing.
