9705446 McBride The Maine Mathematics and Science Alliance is conducting a
12-month planning project in preparation for a Parental Involvement project. The
planning activities include conducting a needs assessment, reviewing literature,
refining goals, writing measurable objectives, identifying and piloting
strategies, designing plans for evaluation, implementation, and dissemination,
and overseeing the development of a full proposal. A broad-based planning team
is convened to accomplish the planning activities.
