9315834 FINERTY This project is to up date the publication "University
Curricula in Oceanography and Related Fields." The publication serves as a guide
for men and women interested in the educational opportunities in ocean sciences.
This will be the twelfth update of this publication, included in the changes
will be an advance the state of the data base that outlines marine engineering
and science education facilities and an index of faculty. The new title will be
"Education and Training Programs in Oceanography and Related Fields."
