The Indianapolis Zoo, in collaboration with the Metro Toronto Zoo, the Milwaukee
Public Zoo, the Phoenix Zoo, the Oregon Zoo and the Fort Worth Zoo will engage
in a planning effort to enable the group of zoos to form the Zoo Exhibit
Collaborative. The Collaborative will develop a series of traveling exhibitions
and collateral materials at member zoos and for the broader zoo and acquarium
industry.
