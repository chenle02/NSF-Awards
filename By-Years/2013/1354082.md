Under the supervision of historian, Graham Burnett, the Ph.D. student Anthony R
Acciavatti will produce a history of multi-disciplinary, trans-national
scientific cooperation. The student will document innovative ways scientists,
engineers, and economists worked with bureaucrats to bring about liberal
economic integration. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;In addition to a dissertation, this
project will produce a traveling multi-media exhibition.
