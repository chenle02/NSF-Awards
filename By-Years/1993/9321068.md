9321068 Bajzek This award assists the regional network, PREPnet, in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks in the United States and to the rest of
the Internet. The award made under the guidelines published in the NSF Program
Solicitation 93-52, partially funds two connections each providing four million
bits per second service over an anticipated duration of 4 years.
