High-speed disk storage for a Connection Machine will be provided for
researchers at Boston University. This equipment is provided under the
Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer and Information Science and
Engineering program. The equipment will be used for several research projects,
including in particular: Computational Investigations of Field Theories, Dynamic
Micromagnetic Simulations of Magnetic Films, Chaotic Dynamics, Massively
Parallel Implementations of Neural Nets and Geometric Surfaces for
Manufacturing.
