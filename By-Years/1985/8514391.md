Equipment (a high performance computer graphic display system with both vector
and raster capabilities) is acquired for use in several research projects, and
in graduate education, in the Mechanical Engineering Department of the State
University of New York at Buffalo. The system will be used in mechanical
dynamics research in active-control studies of complex structures, and
manufacturing, ergonomic, and biomechanical research.
