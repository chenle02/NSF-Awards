Image processing equipment will be provided for researchers at the University of
South Carolina for research in the Center for Machine Intelligence. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which the
equipment is to be used will be in the areas of dynamic scene analysis, robot
planning and control strategy simulation, and shape recognition using depth
data.
