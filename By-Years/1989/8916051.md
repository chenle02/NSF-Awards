In this project, the P.I will carry out planning activities as co- chairman of
the Science Working Group for COARE (Coupled Ocean Atmospheric Response
Experiment). Principal tasks include refinement of the scientific plan for
COARE, Development of an implementation plan, and establishment of a COARE
Project Office.
