This award is for partial support for the annual meeting on Hepatitis B Viruses.
Topics to be covered include strategies of genome replication, tissue specific
control of transcription, and structure and function of viral gene products. The
meeting will consist of seven sessions of 10 oral presentations and two poster
sessions collectively dealing with a variety of highly relevant topics.
