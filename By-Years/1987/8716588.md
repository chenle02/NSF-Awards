This award will provide travel funds for Dr. Albin A. Szewczyk to present an
invited general lecture at the XVIII the Biennial Fluid Dynamics Symposium,
Warsaw, Poland. September 6-11, 1987. This Biennial Symposium is a meeting of
scientists working in different branches of fluid mechanics interested in
presenting and discussing current work on fundamental problems. The proceedings
will be published by the Polish Academy of Sciences.
