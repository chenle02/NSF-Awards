&lt;br/&gt;This is a planning project that focuses on evaluation capacity
building for Native Americans in the area of environmental science. It proposes
to develop a culture-based curriculum on evaluation in Native American contexts
through a partnership between Kansas State University and Haskell Indian Native
University.&lt;br/&gt;
