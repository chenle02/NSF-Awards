9317559 Linsky The purpose of this task plan is to provide travel and conference
support and a workshop proceedings document for the Directorate on Social,
Behavioral and Economic Sciences (SBE). N-BioS will subcontract with Courtesy
Associates to provide travel arrangement assistance and N-BioS will provide
administrative support, some conference support and technical editing of the
workshop proceedings document.
