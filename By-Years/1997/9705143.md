This grant will support continued activities of the steering committee and
science office for Marine aspects of Earth System History (MESH), a component of
the US Global Change Research Plan. Activities include meetings of the steering
committee, workshops, training institutes to integrate data and modelling, and
communications and travel associated with participation in the international
IMAGES program (International Marine Global Change Study), a core program of the
International Geosphere/ Biosphere Program.
