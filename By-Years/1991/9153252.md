A consortium of institutions that includes Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute,
SUNY Albany, a number of community colleges in New York, and local high schools
is being organized to adapt, refine, and implement a revised calculus curriculum
at their institutions. The faculty are attending workshops and computer-calculus
classes at Albany and Rensselaer.
