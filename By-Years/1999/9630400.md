9630400 Reed The purpose of this award is to provide partial travel expenses
for fifteen students to attend the 33rd Annual Meeting of the Society of
Engineering Sciences to be held at Arizona State on October 20-23, 1996. This
meeting brings together scientists and engineers to discuss a wide variety of
topics in the broader fields of engineering science and mechanics, materials and
manufacturing. ***
