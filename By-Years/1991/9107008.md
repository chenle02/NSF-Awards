This award supports a U.S.-U.S.S.R. Cooperation project between S. Shlomo at
Texas A&M and G. Vagradov and S. Akulinichev at the Institute for Nuclear
Research in Moscow. The collaboration focuses on theoretical studies of quarks
in nuclei as probed by deep inelastic lepton-nucleus scattering.
