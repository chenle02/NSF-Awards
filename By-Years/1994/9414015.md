9414015 Sameh This award will establish a high bandwidth network of high
performance workstations for research in parallel computing. The research
supported by this infrastructure includes research on distributed sparse matrix
algorithms, parallel algorithms for signal processing, parallel algorithms for
the n-body problem, large scale distributed neural databases, parallel task
scheduling, and parallel operating systems. ***
