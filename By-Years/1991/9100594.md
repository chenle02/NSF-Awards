This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduate (REU) Site proposal
to provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in marine molecular
biology, to acquaint the students with the excitement and opportunities of
academic research and to encourage them to pursue graduate studies and a career
in ocean sciences.//
