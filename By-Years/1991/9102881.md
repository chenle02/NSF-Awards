Geological Sample Storage facilities provide important and essential services
to the geological research community. The core repository facility at Oregon
State University, Department of Oceanography is being funded to archive cores
and other geological rock samples from the seafloor and provide the important
services of sample distribution and related data dissemination to the marine
geologica, sedimentological and paleoceanographic communities.
