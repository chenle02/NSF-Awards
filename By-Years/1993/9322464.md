9322464 Wipke Dr. W. Todd Wipke and other members of the Chemistry Department of
the UC-Santa Cruz are being supported to continue a Research Experiences for
Undergraduate site (REU) in Chemistry. For the period 1994-6, ten undergraduate
students each summer will spend ten weeks in a variety of research projects.
Projects cover the traditional areas of chemistry- analytical, inorganic,
organic and physical. The program will focus on women, minority and handicapped
students.
