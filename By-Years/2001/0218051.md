The Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM) will to administer a
travel grant program for approximately 50-60 U.S. mathematical and computational
scientists and graduate students to attend the International Conference on
Industrial and Applied Mathematics (ICIAM03), to be held on July 7-11, 2003 in
Sydney, Australia. SIAM will partially support the travel and subsistence of
U.S. participants.
