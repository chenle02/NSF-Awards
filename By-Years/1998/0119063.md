EIA-0119063 &lt;br/&gt;Wessner, Charles&lt;br/&gt;National Academy of
Sciences&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;ITR: Measuring and Sustaining the New
Economy&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The Board on Science, Technology, and Economic
Policy will conduct a study designed to identify and address the policy issues
associated with the development and growth of the New Economy, stimulated by the
growth of information technology. The Board will identify and evaluate key
issues associated with the measurement and analysis of the New Economy, the
technologies underpinning its development, and the government-industry
collaboration and regulatory framework necessary to sustain its
growth.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
