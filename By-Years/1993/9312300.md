9312300 Moll This proposal requests support for the travel of graduate students,
post doctoral students and young investigators to attend and present their
research at the Second World Congress of Herpetology in Adelaide, Australia.
Attendance at this meeting, which covers a variety of presentation on the
biology, ecology and biodiversity of amphibians and reptiles, would be valuable
to these young scientists. ***
