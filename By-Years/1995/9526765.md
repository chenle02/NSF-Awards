9526765 Griggs This grant provides $41,870 as partial support of the costs of
acquiring a Sun SPARC20 workstation, image processing software and peripherals
for use in the study of surficial and tectonic processes as determined from
digital analyses of aerial and satellite photography. The equipment will serve
as a central tool for the PI's research on regional and global shoreline
evolution and active tectonics as well as for the training of graduates students
and as a regional facility for quantitative geomorphology. ***
