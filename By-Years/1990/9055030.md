The Lincoln University Urban Center in cooperation with the University of
Pennsylvania will initiate a three-week, commuter Young Scholars (EAI) project
in Physics and Mathematics for 30 students entering grade 7. The emphasis will
be on the physics and biophysics of light and electricity. Participants will
return for follow-up activities on 20 Saturdays during the academic year.
