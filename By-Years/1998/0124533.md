The proposal plans to connect WSU to KanREN, to the Great Plains Network (GPN),
and then to the Abilene Network in support of research and education in the
following areas: experimental therapeutics and cancer biology, molecular
modeling of ionic liquids, collaborative design techniques for integrated
product development, low temperature astrophysics.
