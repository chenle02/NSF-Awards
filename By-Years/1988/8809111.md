Mr. T. Daniel Patrick Stack has been recommended for a Postdoctoral Research
Fellowship in Chemistry Award. Mr. Stack's doctoral degree will be from Harvard
University under the supervision of Professor R. H. Holm. He intends to continue
his research at the University of California at Berkeley under the sponsorship
of Professor K. N. Raymond. Mr. Stack's area of Postdoctoral Research will be
Solvent Proton Relaxivity Effected by Ferric Iron Complexes: Maximization of the
Contributions of Saturatively Coordinated Complexes.
