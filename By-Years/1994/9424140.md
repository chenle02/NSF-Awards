9424140 Thoennessen The support of the "Research Experiences for Undergraduates
Site" at Michigan State University will be continued. This program recruits
students nationally, and gives them the opportunity to contribute to a number of
leading research activities. In addition, other professional contacts and
attendance at scientific meetings lets the participants participate in all
aspects of research. ***
