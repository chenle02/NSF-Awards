undertaking study to address the history of key innovations in information
technology and their economic impacts. The committee will collect and document
more fully than has been done to date the histories of key information
technologies and see what can and cannot be reliably quantified (including
confidence assessments on what can be quantified); derive inference for public
policy from the historical and analytical material; and produce a report
summarizing the findings of the committee and articulating their
