Mater Dei College is a private, two-year, college located in rural St. Lawrence
County, New York. The College will use this grant to connect it's campus to the
Internet. The students and faculty of the Mater Dei College need the Internet
connections to be able to access information resources throughout the state, the
nation and the world. The connection will benefit the faculty, staff students of
this institution as well as the community in general. The award provides partial
support of the project for two years.
