9318902 Knopper This project is investigating and developing tools for
management and distribution of inter-domain policy-based routing information for
the Internet. The award provides for equipment and personnel for the routing
experiments, including a subcontract to Cornell for GateD modifications and
testing. Maximum duration of the award is 24 months.
