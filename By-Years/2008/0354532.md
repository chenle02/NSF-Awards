This conference award to Daniel G. Nocera, MIT, and Gregory H. Robinson,
University of Georgia, is supported by the Chemistry Collaboratives and Special
Projects Program. Nocera and Robinson will organize and host a meeting of the
investigators currently funded by the Collaborative Research in Chemistry (CRC)
Program. The goals of the meeting are to discuss
multidisciplinary/interdisciplinary research and training issues and discuss
best practices for international collaboration, publicity, and project
evaluations. A workshop report will be produced and widely disseminated.
