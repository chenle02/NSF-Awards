The proposal plans to connect SUNY-A to vBNS and Abilene via the NYSERNet
Network for the following research and educational purposes: electronic
government funded by the NSF PACI program; northeast regional climate modeling;
interactive real-time regional air quality forecast and management system; real-
time transmission of meteorological data; mesocale model and global analysis;
network-based visualization of large-scale databases.
