This project will deploy 30 ocean bottom seismometers at the
Quebrada/Discovery/Gofar transform fault system on the equatorial East Pacific
Rise for a period of one year to study the mechanical processes that control
earthquake nucleation and the relative partitioning of seismic and aseismic
fault slip.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
