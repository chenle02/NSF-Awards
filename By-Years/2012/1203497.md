This award is made as part of the FY 2012 Mathematical Sciences Postdoctoral
Research Fellowships Program. These fellowships support a research and training
plan at a host institution in the mathematical sciences, including applications
to other disciplines. The title of the research fellowship awarded to Matthew
Badger is "Geometry of Measures and Analysis on Rough Domains." The host
institution for the fellowship is Stony Brook University, and the sponsoring
scientist is Dr. Raanan Schul.
