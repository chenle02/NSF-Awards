9357864 Sahr This National Science Foundation Young Invesegotor intends to
expand his experimental and theoretical research in ionospheric plasma
irregularities. The experimental program includes the construction of a new VHF
coherent scatter radar interferometer, and development of improved radar
techniques. He also intends to develop and test new theories of plasma
turbulence in the auroral electrojets.
