9319090 Pederson Montgomery College requests support from NSF for connection of
their campus networks to SURAnet. SURAnet is the midlevel network located in the
southeastern United States. It will provide operations management and
information services and it will give Montgomery College a 56 thousand bits per
second connection to the NSFNET, a high speed (1.5 - 45 million bits per second)
National Backbone network. The college has experienced a growth in auxiliary
programs that has added value to the basic mission that the college serves to
aid the education of all persons of all ages. The networking plan at Montgomery
College is the foundation for uniting all the disciplines and campus groups.
