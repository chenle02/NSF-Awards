Type 2 diabetes is epidemic in the native populations, Navajo and Hopi, in
Northern Arizona. Through this proposal a partnership would be developed with
the community, the local Indian Health Services, and Northern Arizona University
researchers to address this problem through the use IT. This proposal would
explore the collection an integration of various data from medical devices such
as glucometers, provision of information using wireless devices and PDAs, test
various user interface concepts, and identify multicultural barriers.
