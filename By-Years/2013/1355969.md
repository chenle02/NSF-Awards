The University Corporation for Atmospheric Research (UCAR) proposes to staff
three booths at the STEM Careers Fair sponsored by the National Science
Foundation and U.S. Representative Frank Wolf in Dulles, Virginia in September
2013. The goal of the fair is to provide and opportunity for science-related
organizations to showcase some of the amazing STEM research and education
activities taking place throughout the United States and to highlight the vast
diversity of STEM career possibilities.
