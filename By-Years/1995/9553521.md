* 9553521 Reinhall The University of Washington will initiate a four-week,
summer commuter, Young Scholars project for 40 Seattle high potential and
economically disadvantaged students in grades 7th and 8th. The project provides
students with an opportunity to engage in classroom, laboratory, field trips,
and research activities with university faculty during the summer. The project
terminates during the academic year with the design and construction of a
complex engineering system involving a robot. ***
