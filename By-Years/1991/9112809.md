Dr. Ionel Haiduc of Babes-Bolyai University, Cluj-Napoca, Romania will visit
Dr. Robert Bruce King of the University of Georgia, Athens, to initiate joint
chemical research on planar selenocarbons and tellurocarbons. Results are
expected to contribute to the definition of a longer-term and mutually
beneficial cooperative research project in inorganic chemistry for consideration
by the National Science Foundation or the National Academy of Sciences.
