Stevens Institute of Technology will conduct a program in materials science and
engineering for sixteen eleventh grade students. Activities will include: (1) a
self-paced correspondence course on aspects of materials which would precede the
summer research experience, (2) a five-week research experience at local
industrial research laboratories or at states.
