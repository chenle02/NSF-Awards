This project supports 10 students to attend the seventh ACM International
Workshops on Underwater Networks (WUWNet)to be held in Los Angeles, California,
November 5 - 6, 2012. This will provide students exposure to the challenges and
techniques for setting up underwater networks and using them for continuous
monitoring of significant events.
