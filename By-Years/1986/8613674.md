This award will provide a high-performance liquid chromatography system for the
use of the members of the Department of Biological Sciences. It will facilitate
their studies of ovarian tissue metabolism of ecosanoids (unsaturated fatty
acids), the chlorophyll and carotenoid pigment content of lake sediments, and
insect hemolymph antifreeze proteins.
