9722854 Monk The Department of Mathematical Sciences at the University of
Delaware will purchase a four processor computer with 640Mbytes of memory and
15.6Gbytes of disk space which will be dedicated to the support of research in
the mathematical sciences. The equipment will be used for several research
projects, including in particular Mathematical modeling for thin liquid film
drainage, A posteriori error estimation for integral equations, Adaptive
estimation of linear functions in electromagnetism.
