9413782 Boult This award will establish a giga-op simulation/computation
facility at Lehigh University to support research in signal and image
processing; computer vision; parallel algorithm development; software systems
research; and wave-model computations in microwave IC design/packaging, optical
design and material studies. The instrumentation acquired by this award includes
an I/O server, a parallel compute server, a floating point server, a graphics
server, and a high speed network. ***
