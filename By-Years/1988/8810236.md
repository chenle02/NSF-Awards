This action is for continued partial support of the NAS/NRC Committee on
Seismology. The activities supported include: (1) A study and report on the
outlook for real time warnings of earthquakes in the United States. (2) A panel
report on activities related to regional and national seismographic networks,
particularly regional networks. Research using data from these networks is of
particular concern to the Committee. (3) Continued Committee review of progress
and needs for research on strong ground motions. This review includes the
developments in programs resulting from the joint Committee on
Seismology/Committee on Earthquake Engineering report: "Recommendations for the
Strong- Motion Program in the United States". (4) The conduct of meetings of the
Committee and its subgroups. (5) Initiation of additional studies, as
appropriate.
