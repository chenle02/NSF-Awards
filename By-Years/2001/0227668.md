Dr. David Bynum has been actively involved with enlarging programs of excellence
and success for underrepresented students since 1993. Through his efforts, he
has helped to change the campus environment. He has done a lot for the community
and the institution; working with students at all levels of the ladder. His
programs have become institutionalized, and have reached almost 200 community
college students. He has received grants from the Howard Hughes Medical
Institute, NIH and has created the Long Island Group Advancing Science
Education, an umbrella group that has received local, state and national
attention and awards.&lt;br/&gt;
