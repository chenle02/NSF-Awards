Funds are requested to support the continued Oceanographic Technical Services
Program for the UNOLS registered R/V Atlantic Explorer in 2009, with out year
requests for 2010 and 2011 to be negotiated after submittal of an annual report.
The RV Atlantic Explorer is a general-purpose research ship owned and operated
by the Bermuda Institute of Ocean Sciences (BIOS), a Bermuda-based United States
registered, 501c, not-for-profit oceanographic research and education
institution. The budget included with this proposal is for the first year of a
3-year continuing grant.
