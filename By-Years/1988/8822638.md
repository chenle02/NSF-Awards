The University of Missouri proposes to create the Missouri Research Network
(MORNET) in order to provide direct connection to the NSFNET regional network
MIDNET for access to the NSF National Supercomputing Center. MORNET will operate
as a subnet of MIDNET, connecting the University of Missouri-Columbus,
University of Missouri - Kansas City, University of Missouri - Rolla, and the
University of Missouri - St. Louis.***//
