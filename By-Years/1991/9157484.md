This Presidential Young Investigator proposes to study a) Quantum Hall effect,
including Ginzburg-Landau theory of the fractional effect and phase transitions
between states, including effects of disorder, and b) high temperature
superconductors and other strong correlated electron systems, through large-N
and other systematic methods.
