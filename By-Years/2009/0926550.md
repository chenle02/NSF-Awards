This award is funded under the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009
(Public Law 111-5).

This project proposes to investigate and implement a variety of tools for
enhancing the very widely used and popular Arxiv.org infrastructure, based on
information filters for assisted service discovery and selection, text-mining,
information genealogy, automated classification and identification of composite
resources, data-mining, usage analyses, matching and ranking heuristics, support
for next-generation document formats, and semantic markup.
