A committee is formed under the auspices of the Board on Chemical Sciences and
Technology of the National Research Council to prepare a short report on
research frontiers in catalytic science and technology. It is intended that this
report will be instructive for research students, scientists and engineers in
related areas, research managers in industry and government, and science policy
officials.
