This award is for a workshop to bring together a broad range of networking
researchers to discuss the focus, needs, and physical/managerial structures for
a new generation of networking research testbeds that w ill enable/enhance
cutting edge networking and network-application research over the next decade.
The output of the workshop will be a report documenting the discussion and
overall recommendations that emerge from the workshop.
