Funds are requested to acquire a phosphorimaging system to be shared by a group
of scientists at the University of Texas, particularly a group of five senior
investigators. The common research theme among these laboratories is structure-
function relationships of regulatory sequences, genes, and their interacting or
encoded proteins.
