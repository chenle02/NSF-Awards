Engineers and scientists, with expertise in catalysis and catalytic reactions,
from United States and Brazil will participate in workshops, seminars and visits
to take place in both countries. The purpose of these activities is to establish
the basis for collaborative research in these areas of high interest to both
countries and in fulfillment of the goals of the ongoing United States- Brazil
Science and Technology Initiative.
