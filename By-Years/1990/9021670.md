* Application of Chemical Adhesives in Masonry Mortars

* James Colville, University of Maryland, College Park
* Standard Grant
* John Scalzi

The project will investigate the feasibility of chemical adhesives, polymers and
epoxies as additives to increase the strength of mortars used in masonry
construction.
