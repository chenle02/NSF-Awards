The SEP Grantees Workshop will bring together recipients of the twenty 2012 NSF
Sustainable Energy Pathways awards for two days of discussion and interaction in
a cross-disciplinary forum., NSF staff, leadership and invited speakers will
participate to better understand linkages among researchers spanning from
physical science to social & behavioral sciences on the complex challenges of
transformative clean energy and its adoption by society.
