This study involves considerations of the analysis and forecasting of
demographic-economic changes at the regional, metropolitan, and sub-metropolitan
levels of detail. The applications of the results affect urban planning, public
facility locations, and investments. The data are also useful to private
organizations because they may determine the location, job expansion, and
marketing strategies for firms.
