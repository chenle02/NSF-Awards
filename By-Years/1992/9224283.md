9224283 Knight The proposed UNIX computing system will provide enhanced
capabilities for meteorological research and classroom teaching. To provide
students with an up-to-date education and expose them to state-of-the-art
research facilities, the PIs propose to provide experience in using UNIX systems
for atmospheric research. ***
