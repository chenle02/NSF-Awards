A high-end graphics workstation will be provided for researchers at the
University of Washington for research in the Department of Industrial
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of computer
vision, computer graphics, AI and human factors principals, focussing on
visualization techniques for generating and manipulating 3D objects.
