Description: This project supports U.S. scientists' participation in a
conference on Emerging Optoelectronics Technologies planned for December 18-20,
1991 at the Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore, India The Indian Chairman
is Dr. C.N.R. Rao, director of IISc. The topics in the conference include:
materials and fabrication technology; devices and ICs; novel phenomena;
optoelectronic systems; and lightwave communication systems. During the two days
prior to the conference, December 16, 17, several professional short courses
will be offered on related areas. The conference will have a planery session
during the first day where keynote lectures will be delivered by international
scholars. The following sessions will start by an invited speaker to be followed
by contributed papers. The sessions may run cincurrently based on the number of
papers submitted. Scope : It is expected that intense exchange of information
will occur among the large number of highly qualified scientists scheduled to
attend, and that recommendations for joint U.S- Indian collaboration will be
made. The host institute, the IISc. is by far the best known academic institute
in India, with a very large staff of excellent reputation in the field.
