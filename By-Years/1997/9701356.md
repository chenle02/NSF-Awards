9701356 McDuff This award to University of Washington provides shipboard
technical support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument
calibration for researchers using R/V Thomas Thompson, a research vessel
operated by the University of Washington School of Oceanography as part of the
University National Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet. The
technical support awarded here will assist NSF-funded ocean researchers conduct
a diverse suite of oceanographic studies in the North Pacific Ocean in 1997. ***
