The number of students trained in the area of information security and assurance
is underrepresented to satisfy the needs. The goal of this proposal is to
alleviate this problem by providing at Florida State University scholarship to
students under the Federal Cyber Service. Florida State University:&lt;br/&gt;
is an NSA (National Security Agency) Center of Excellence in Information
Assurance Education.&lt;br/&gt; has been certified by the National Security
Telecommunications and Information Systems Security (NSTISSC). The courses for
information security professionals in the Department of Computer Science satisfy
the NSTISSC 4011 standard. Students who have followed the appropriate courses at
Florida State University can receive the corresponding certificate.&lt;br/&gt;
courses in information security are also open to students of the FAMU/FSU
college of&lt;br/&gt;engineering.&lt;br/&gt; has 3 experts in Information
Assurance on its staff&lt;br/&gt; has a $125,000 laboratory on Security and
Assurance in Information Technology (SAIT) Laboratory where students become
familiar with the practical aspects of information security.&lt;br/&gt; is
clearly committed to continue growing this area.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
