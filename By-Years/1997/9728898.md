An Affinity sensors IAys Optical Biosensor will be purchased for studies in
assembly and regulatory reactions in replication and transcription, the
energetics of assembly of the bacterial chemotaxis signaling complex, the role
of SNARE proteins in controlling the specificity of membrane fusion in yeast,
assembly and structure of phage and transcription complexes, assembly and
function of the RNA polymerase II transcription complexes and the role of
chaperones in protein folding.
