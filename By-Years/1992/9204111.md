This award provides travel support for approximately twenty US scientists and
students to attend the Fourth International Conference on Southern Hemisphere
Meteorology and Oceanography which will be held 29 March to 2 April 1993, in
Hobart, Tasmania, Australia. It also provides partial travel support for several
scientists from Latin America. Support is provided by the Divisions of
Atmospheric and Ocean Sciences and International Programs at NSF as well as by
NOAA and NASA.
