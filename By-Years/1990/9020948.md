This award is for support to organize bimonthly meetings on scientific
visualization along the Front Range. The meetings will be in form of talks and
demonstrations by researchers in the scientific and engineering fields
conducting research and/or developing tools on vital areas of interest.
Facilities and special equipment will be provided by the University of Colorado.
