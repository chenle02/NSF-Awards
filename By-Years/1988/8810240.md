This award will enable several university scientists, including graduate
students and post doctoral investigators, to attend the Palmen Memorial
Symposium on Extratropical Cyclones to be held in Helsinki, Finland (29 Aug.2
Sept 1988). The Symposium will be jointly sponsored by professional societies in
Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, and the United States.
