This grant supports substantial upgrade to the computer hardware and software
supporting a computer graphics course. Specific enhancements realized are CPU
speed, resolution, input- device support, color, and up-to-date software. This
laboratory supports not only the computer science majors but also majors in
Mathematics, Business, Art, and Pre-architecture. This award is being matched by
an equal amount from the principal investigator's institution.
