This grant is to support a Conference of Research in Engineering Design and NSF
Design Theory and Methodology Grantee Workshop (Design Theory '89) at the
University of Massachusetts, Amherst; June 1989. The purpose of the conference
is to bring together the engineering design research community for exchange of
current research results, ideas and directions. A Proceedings will be published,
and papers presented will be considered for publication in the new Journal of
Research in Engineering Design.
