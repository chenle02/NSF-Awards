9419080 The American Chemical Society (ACS) Division of Polymeric Materials:
Science and Engineering is sponsoring a symposium at the Washington, D.C.
Meeting, August 21-26, 1994, entitled "Opportunities for Women in Polymer
Science: Theory, Experiment and Technology". The Women Chemists Commitee of the
ACS is a cosponser. This Symposium will be pedagogical, and is intended to
highlight the contributions of women in materials science, with special emphasis
on polymer science and engineering so as to present positive reinforcement to
women who are interested in, or just starting careers in polymers. %%% ***
