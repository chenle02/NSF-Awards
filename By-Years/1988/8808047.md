Ms. Jennifer Sue Brodbelt-Lustig has been awarded a Postdoctoral Research
Fellowship in Chemistry. Ms. Brodbelt-Lustig's doctoral degree is from Purdue
University under the supervision of Professor Graham Cooks. She intends to
continue her research at the University of California at Santa Barbara under the
sponsorship of Professor Michael Bowers. Ms. Brodbelt-Lustig's area of
postdoctoral research is state-specific ion/molecule reactions.
