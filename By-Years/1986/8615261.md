Resources are provided to support the editorial tasks of the Inernational
Conference on Wear of Materials-1987. The conferenc is held every two years and
attracts 300 scientists and engineers from the U.S. and abroad. Over 150
manuscripts and abstracts are reviewed for the conference and nearly 100 are
selected for publication in a bound volume.
