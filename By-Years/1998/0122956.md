This scholarship program provides financial, academic, and motivational support
to enable academically gifted low-income students to receive a bachelor of
science degree in mathematics. These students are involved in internship and
research opportunities that make them highly attractive to high technology
companies. Scholarship recipients also have access to a special mentoring
program that supplements the strong support systems that are available to all
students at the university.
