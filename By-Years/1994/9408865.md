9408865 Zelibor This award provides US dues for the committee on Biotechnology
(COBIOTECH) an interdisciplinary scientific consortium within the International
Council of Scientific Unions. The goals of COBIOTECH are to promote
Biotechnology world wide, to advance research, education and the transfer of
information and resources on biotechnology internationally, with special
emphasis on developing countries.
