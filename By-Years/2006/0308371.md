WestEd is providing travel for U.S. participants in a German-American workshop
devoted to research issues of interest to project teams associated with the NSF
workshop is to be held in Kiel, Germany, March 6-8, 2003. The living expenses of
American and German participants in Kiel are to be paid by a grant from the
Deutsche Forshungsgemeinschaft (DFG), which is the German equivalent of the
National Science Foundation.
