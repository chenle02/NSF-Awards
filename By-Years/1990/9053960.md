This project will coordinate programs designed to increase the numbers of
minorities and women in technical and scientific professions by creating
partnerships among universities, schools, the minority community, businesses and
government in the Boston area, under the leadership of Northeastern University.
The major programs to be used include: apprenticeships monitoring programs,
family and community involvement through outreach programs, improvement of
training programs for teachers, letter transmission of materials, and improved
retention of students.//
