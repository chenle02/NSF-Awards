This award is a planning grant under the NSF Program "Research Opportunities
for Women." This type of grant aids women to develop their own competitive
research programs. This activity comprises preparation of a detailed and
competitive research plan in scientific visualization. Visits to universities,
research centers, and producers of visualization software, as well as literature
search, are involved.
