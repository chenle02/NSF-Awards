The InterAmerican University of Puerto Rico, San German campus, will sponsor a
six-week, commuter Young Scholars project in the Environmental Sciences for 15
students entering grades 10,11. Participants will engage in a one-week lecture
program and a five-week environmental study of the Guanajibo River during the
summer session. The follow-up activities will take place during the Fall
semester of the academic year.
