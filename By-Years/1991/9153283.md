The materials and approach to teaching calculus being developed at Duke
University are being adapted and implemented. The revised course emphasizes
greater conceptual understanding through extensive writing, collaborative
learning in a discovery-based microcomputer laboratory, and using mathematics
for modeling real world phenomena.
