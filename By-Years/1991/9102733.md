* Swash Zone Sediment Transport on a Steep Beach

This mainly field program will examine the dynamics, both fluid and sediment,
within the swash zone on a steep beach. The net sediment transport is expected
to be determined by a balance between infragravity and incident wave energies.
These will be modified by microtopography on the beach face.
