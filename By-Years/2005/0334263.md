This award provides funding for a one year award to Loyola Marymount University
(LMU) for a Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) Site Program entitled,
"Summer Research Experience for Community College Professors/K-12 Teachers,"
under the direction of Dr. Omar Es-Said. This program will involve 16
participants, 3 community college professors and 13 K-12 teachers from the Los
Angeles area, in research projects at LMU in the areas of materials engineering,
mechanical engineering, biochemistry, and biology.
