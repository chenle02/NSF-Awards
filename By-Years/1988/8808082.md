Dr. Luke Hanley has been recommended for a Postdoctoral Fellowship in Chemistry
Award. Dr. Hanley's doctoral degree was from the State University of New York at
Stony Brook under the supervision of Professor Scott L. Anderson. He intends to
continue his research at the University of Pittsburgh under the sponsorship of
Professor John T. Yates, Jr. Dr. Hanley's area of Postdoctoral Research will be
Probing bonding of adsorbates to Ni (III) by vibrational spectroscopies: CO,
with B2H6, and B5H9 under electron impact.
