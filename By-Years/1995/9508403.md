9508403 Slattery The Department of Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science
at Marquette University will purchase, one Sun SPARC station 20, one Sun
workstation upgrade, software, and support staff. These machines will be
dedicated to support mathematical research in several projects including 1)
Computation in Finite Simple Groups and 2) Computation in Finite Solvable
Groups.
