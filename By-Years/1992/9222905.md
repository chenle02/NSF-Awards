Video display and recording equipment shall be purchased to augment existing
research equipment in order to allow simulation of the effects of various
algorithms and errors on the quality of digital video sequences under realistic
viewing conditions. Particular projects include: * Compression of video data. *
Restoration and enhancement of video data through nonlinear and nonstationary
filtering. * Visual communication system channel error effect compensation.
