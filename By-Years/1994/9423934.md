9423934 Dynes This grant will initiate a "Research Experiences for
Undergraduates" Site in the Physics Department at the University of California-
San Diego. The program will involve students, recruited primarily from southern
California, in the research programs of the department. A number of professional
events will be arranged for the students. ***
