The University of South Alabama plans to expand their laboratory infrastructure
for cybersecurity to facilitate research, education and training in hardware
design, prototyping, reverse engineering, forensic de-obfuscation, side-channel
characterization, and program analysis.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This CRI award in
cybersecurity is important to the workforce development for the region and
extends programs at the new Center for Forensics Information Technology Security
(CFITS) at the University of South Alabama.
