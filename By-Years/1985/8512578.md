Support is provided to enable young researchers to attend and to participate in
the IEEE International Symposium on Information Theory. The program includes
five invited plenary session lectures and several special sessions of invited
papers with the topic, VLSI and information theory, being highlighted. The
meeting will offer the opportunity for young and experienced investigators to
interact.
