This award is for travel to the 1995 Sixth International Structure Faults and
Repair Conference to be held in London, England. The participants will present
technical papers, and chair sessions. Following the conference, a workshop will
be conducted to organize a U.S. - European workshop on bridge engineering to be
held in 1996.
