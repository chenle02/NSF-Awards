This grant funds a workshop on artificial Intelligence (AI) Methodology, held at
the University of Massachusetts on June 2 through June 4, 1991. The purpose of
the workshop is to assess the current stat of AI methodology and discuss
specific methodological improvements. Especial attention is given to the
relation of AI theory to empirical research, and the soundness of the state of
the art methods in generating hypotheses, predictions, experiments and in
allowing replication of results. Approximately 25 scholars in the field of AI
are invited to participate in this workshop. The workshop output is a volume of
edited papers on a range of methodological issues that is published in the form
of a book.
