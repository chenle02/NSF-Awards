This residential project is designed to expose outstanding young precollege
hearing impaired students (in grades 8 and 9) to the interdisciplinary study of
Marine Science. Thirty hearing impaired students will be given a four week
educational program involving lecture, hands-on field, laboratory, and research
experiences. The program will be located at the Wallops Island Marine Science
Consortium at Wallops Island, Va.
