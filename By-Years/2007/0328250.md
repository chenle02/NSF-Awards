This award is for a one-and-a-half-day workshop on "Fundamental Research in
Networking." The workshop will bring together researchers with expertise
covering a broad spectrum of areas within networking. The purpose of this
workshop is to provide vision and direction for fundamental research in
networking. The outcome of the workshop will be a detailed report documenting
the discussions and will provide overall recommendations that will be available
to the community and can help to better articulate the future research agenda in
networking. The workshop will stimulate far-reaching future research,
collaborations and evolvement of the networking research community.
