This proposal enhances the Survey of Earned Doctorates data records and analyzes
the data to &lt;br/&gt;investigate the role that geography plays in the
placement of new Ph.D.s going to industry.&lt;br/&gt;Specific goals of the
project are: (1) Code verbatim files for placements; (2) Augment recoded
records; (3) Create GIS codes for locations of industrial employers; (4) Analyze
the data;&lt;br/&gt;and (5) Provide NSF with a documented data set that permits
other researchers to analyze the data.&lt;br/&gt;
