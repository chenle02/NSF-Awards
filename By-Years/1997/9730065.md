9730065 Dougherty ScienceGate II is the second in a series of conferences of
developers of comprehensive high school science curricula to explore the
connections between disciplinary curricula. In the previous conference it was
discovered that there was a common vision about the nature of science and how
science is taught. This conference continues that dialogue and explores other
issues of mutual concern including evaluation and dissemination.
