The funds are being requested to organize a meeting on infinite-dimensional
aspects of representation theory and applications. The conference will be held
at University of Virginia in May 2004.&lt;br/&gt;The meeting will feature three
mini courses meant to help graduate students to enter new and exciting areas of
mathematics, as well as plenery talks by many leaders of the field. More than
half of the requested funds will cover the student and junior faculty
participation.
