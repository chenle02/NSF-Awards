ABSTRACT&lt;br/&gt;OPP0424901&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This is a dissertation
research proposal to examine the changes in technology among the Alutiit in the
Kodiak region of Alaska at the time of contact. Utilizing experimental
techniques for tool analysis, as well as ethnohistoric, archaeological and
material analyses the research proposes to gain a more holistic understanding of
the factors that influence technology changes among
foragers.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
