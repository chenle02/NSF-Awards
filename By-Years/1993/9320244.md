9320244 Watson The National Science Foundation has joined forces with the
National Endowment for the Humanities and the Library of Congress to foster
public attention to the interrelations of science, technology, and the
humanities. This award transfers funds to the National Endowment for the
Humanities (NEH) in support of this joint effort. The funds will be used for
public programs on "Nature, Technology and Human Understanding" sponsored by
several of the NEH State Humanities Councils. ***
