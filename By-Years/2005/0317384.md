The current award is to enhance the capabilities of the web site entitled the
Plasma Page at plasmas.org. This site has reliably served the plasma science and
engineering community worldwide. These enhancements will include adding an
internal search engine, comprehensively checking and extending link listings and
descriptions in all categories of plasma science and engineering, and revising
and updating content and graphics to better represent research and education
programs and opportunities in the plasma sciences.
