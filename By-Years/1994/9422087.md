9422087 Nakajima This award is to purchase a VLSI tester which will be dedicated
to support research in computer and information science and engineering. The
equipment will be used for several research projects, including in particular:
High-frequency Digital and Analog Electronic and Optoelectronic Integrated
Circuits, Prototyping and Testing Integrated Circuits for Biological
Applications, Minimizing the Cost of Production Testing of Analog Integrated
Circuits, Prototyping and Testing of High-Performance VLSI Signal/Image
Processors, and Prototyping and Testing of Application-Specific RISC Processors
and Concentrator Switches. ***
