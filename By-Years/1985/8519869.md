Support is being provided for the conduct of a conference on aquatic plants for
biomass production, water treatment, and resource recovery, to be held from July
20-24, 1986. Topics to be considered include biology and ecology of aquatic
plants, water treatment systems, biomass utilization, and economic and
environmental impacts.
