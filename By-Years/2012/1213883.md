This award supports a conference for staff of the NSF-funded Centers for
Chemical Innovation. This conference was initiated by CCI center staff to
maximize the impact that the individual centers have in terms of research,
innovation, education, public outreach and broadening participation of
underrepresented groups. The meeting will take place February 27-29, 2012, in
Anaheim CA.
