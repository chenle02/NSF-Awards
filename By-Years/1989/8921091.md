* Mechanistic Study of the Corrosion of Steel in Concrete

This award will investigate the evolution and mechanism of the corrosion
process of steel in salt-contaminated concrete by means of electrochemical
techniques such as polarization resistance and electrochemical independence
spectroscopy.
