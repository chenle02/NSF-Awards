This award enables Rose.Hulman to connect to NSFNET through a 56,000 bit per
second link to the University of Illinois. Access to NSFNET provides the faculty
and students with access to supercomputer, data archives, a variety of network
gateways, automated library catalogs, and a wide range of software. Primary
applications include file transfers, electronic mail communication between local
and remote researchers, and remote login to and use of supercompters and other
research facilities.
