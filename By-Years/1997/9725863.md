9725863 Stevens The grant facilitates a student paper competition at the 19th
Southeastern Conference on Theoretical and Applied Mechanics at Florida Atlantic
University on May 3-5, 1998. The project stimulates student professional
interest in mechanics, materials, and related fields, encourages participation
of women and minority students in these technical areas, provides a forum for
students to interact with leading professionals from around the world, and
provides students s direct experience with the professional presentation and
publication process. ***
