9400167 Heine With this award, the Chemistry Division is supporting Dr. Harold
Heine of the Department of Chemistry at Bucknell University in his efforts to
establish a novel M.A. degree in Chemistry for High School teachers. The
projected curriculum combines mentored research with course work to afford a
broad-based program for high school faculty. ***
