9504870 Scowcroft This award in partial support of the Connecticut Logic Seminar
will allow the seminar to invite at least one outside speaker per month for each
of the next three nine-month academic years. The seminar typically ranges over
most areas of current interest in mathematical logic and has had a successful
history of weekly meetings since its founding in 1974. Participating
institutions represent Connecticut and several nearby states. ***
