This Empirical Implications of Theoretical Models training award affords the
student the opportunity to take a year of courses at the University of Chicago
in formal and empirical training. The training will involve formal course work
in dynamic contract theory and the corresponding formal and statistical
techniques. The training will strengthen the dissertation and future research on
the study of international cooperation, organization, and
law.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The training has the broader social value of enhancing
understanding of the implications of domestic change for the sustainability of
international cooperation.&lt;br/&gt;It also strengthens the research and
teaching skills of this future member of the professorate&lt;br/&gt;
