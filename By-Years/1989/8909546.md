The moment method generates converging upper and lower bounds to the discrete
spectrum for a wide variety of Hamiltonians. It has been successfully applied to
the Zeeman effect for superstrong magnetic fields. It is proposed to extend the
method to the discrete spectrum of the 3-body problem.
