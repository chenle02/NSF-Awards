The Department of Mathematics at Tulane University will purchase workstations
which will be dedicated to support research in the mathematical sciences. The
equipment will be used for several research projects, including in particular:
Research in Computational Biofluiddynamics Research in the Adaptive Solution of
Systems of Parabolic Partial Differential Equations Research in Numerical Fluid
Dynamics and Combustion
