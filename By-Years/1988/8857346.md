This award initiates support for research in atomic physics by Dr. James F.
Kelly, a Presidential Young Investigator. Dr. Kelly will pursue two research
topics: 1) a study of the nonlinear response of a collisionally broadened Raman
scatterer; 2) the scattering of electrons from heavy atoms.
