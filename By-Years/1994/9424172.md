This REU site award provides support for ten undergraduate researchers in
mathematics. The research at this site will be on topics in experimental
mathematics in areas such as algebra, analysis, geometry, and topology. The
program will run for eight weeks. Graduate students as well as faculty members
will be involved in working with the undergraduate project teams.
