9630592 PITTENGER The NSF-owned Research Vessel OCEANUS has been operated under
a charter party agreement by the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution since it
was put into service in 1975. This project extends the Institution's operation
of the OCEANUS for five years under an updated, modified charter party
agreement. ***
