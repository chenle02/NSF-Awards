This award is made under the high performance connections portion of NCRI's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64. It provides partial
support for two years for a DS-3 connection to the vBNS. Applications include
projects in astronomy, networking research, economics, intelligent network-based
tutoring, genetics, distributed mulitmedia information retrieval, remote sensing
and aerial image understanding. Collaborating institutions include the Jet
Propulsion Laboratory, Carnegie-Mellon, Washington University, SUNY, University
of Wyoming, University of Oklahoma, Johns Hopkins University, Naval Research
Laboratory and Pennsylvania State University.
