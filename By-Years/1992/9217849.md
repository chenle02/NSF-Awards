This proposal describes a plan for study under International Technology Research
Institute (ITRI) of Japanese and European R&amp;D in nine technologies -- where
focused attention could accelerate progress in the U.S. The detailed research
will be done by expert panels that will analyze foreign literature, visit
foreign laboratories, and present their findings in workshops in technical
reports.//
