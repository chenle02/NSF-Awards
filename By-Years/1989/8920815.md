This Special Minority Award provides a second year of support for Michael
Moscoso, a graduate student at the University of Texas at Austin. The award will
allow Mr. Moscoso to finish his course work more expeditiously and reach the
research participation phase sooner. This award is an integral part of the
strategy of the National Science Foundation to strengthen and increase minority
participation in the field of physics.
