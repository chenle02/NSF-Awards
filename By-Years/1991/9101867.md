This project is a two-day bridge engineering workshop to be held in Japan, in
May 1991, followed by a study tour of bridge sites. This in the seventh such
workshop, conducted under the auspices of the U.S. - Japan panel on wind and
seismic effects of the UJNR Program. The objective of the workshop is to have
presentation of technical papers related to retrofitting, modeling
experimentation, and monitoring of bridges of steel and concrete.
