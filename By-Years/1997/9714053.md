University of Illinois Chicago Title: A Feasibility Study: Integration of Mixed-
Transmission Lines Types Using Micromachining Techniques This is a Research
Planning Grant to initiate a program to develop a circuit design methodology for
high frequency mixed signal (microwave and optical) integration that can be used
in high speed applications within the field of communications.
