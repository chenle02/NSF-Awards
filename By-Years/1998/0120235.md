0120235&lt;br/&gt;Matthews&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This grant supports a planning
visit to Hong Kong by Dr. Alexander Matthews, Kansas State University. Dr.
Matthews plans to carry out a series of discussions with senior researchers at
the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology to develop research protocols
for a multi-year program of cooperative research and exchange of students in the
