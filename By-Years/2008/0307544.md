This award supports 25 Chief Technology Officers from urban and rural school
districts to attend the CoSN conference to receive technical training in the
management of school networks. The meeting is being held February 25th to
February 27th in Arlington, Virginia. The applications for support will be
evaluated by the Consortium for School Networking and the Council of Great City
Schools.
