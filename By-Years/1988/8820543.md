A robot-manipulator configuration will be provided for researchers at the
University of South Carolina for research in the Center for Machine
Intelligence. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of: Motion
Stereo Vision Coordinated Tracking of Multiple Point Targets with Multiple
Cameras Multiple Sensor Integration for Robotics
