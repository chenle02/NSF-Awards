9705658 Durrett This award provides funds to partially support a research
conference in probability to be held at Cornell University, June 28 - July 1,
1998. The purpose of the conference is to look at exciting areas of current
research in probability and to identify important problems for the next ten
years. One hour expository talks will be given by some of the leaders in the
study of probability.
