This research concerns the growth of n-type "delta-doped" layers in GaAs by
molecular-beam epitaxy (MBE). These layers approximate fixed charge sheets when
depleted, and they can be used to fabricate shallow, non-alloyed, n-GaAs ohmic
contacts or novel bulk-barrier devices.
