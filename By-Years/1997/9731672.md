ABSTRACT This grant supports a design study for the mid-infrared spectroscopic
and imaging camera system designated MIRABU (Mid-InfraRed Array for Boston
University). The study will result in a complete, detailed design for MIRABU, a
detailed cost estimate, and a detailed management plan for the construction and
commissioning of the instrument.
