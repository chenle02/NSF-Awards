9451541 Williams Enhanced instrumentation in spectrophotometry is used as a
basis for emphasizing quantitative laboratory investigation. A number of
laboratory classes including General Biology, Oceanography, Cellular, Molecular
and Developmental Biology, Biochemistry and student Research classes incorporate
use of diiode array spectrophotometers.
