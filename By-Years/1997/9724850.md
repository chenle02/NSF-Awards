Dr. Castillo-Chavez is a Professor of Biometrics and Chair of the Biometrics
Unit at Cornell University. He is recognized as a researcher, dedicated teacher
and mentor. Dr. Castillo-Chavez has amassed an outstanding record of achievement
over a ten-year period including the following: Strong letters of support
attesting to his success in helping students succeed. Seeking and receiving
substantial grant support for mentoring programs. Internationally known
researcher, who serves as a role model for aspiring Latin scientists. Numerous
honors and awards, both National and International, for his work in science.
Serving on the Boards of Directors , Society for the Advancement of Chicanos and
Native Americans in Science and the Society for Mathematical Biology. Involving
many students in research at the undergraduate and graduate level.
