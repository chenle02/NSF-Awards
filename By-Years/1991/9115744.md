This grant will support a single investigator who will collaborate with Italian
physicists. He will analyze data from one of the worlds largest and foremost
particle detectors, the Colliding Detector Facility at Fermilab. This award
would provide funds for the investigator to travel to Padua, Italy to use the
computing facilities of his collaborators and to work closely with them.
