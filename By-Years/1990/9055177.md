Oklahoma State University will initiate a four-week, residential Young Scholars
project in Economics for 25 students entering grades 11,12. The program will
consist of three general areas: 1) development of basis framework of economics;
2) application of framework to contemporary issues of social justice and ethical
behavior in business; and 3) an examination of the impact of globalization on
the U.S. economy.
