9353142 Blank This project will continue to develop and analyze indicators for
mathematics and science education. Particular emphasis will be placed on
developing new indicators of Opportunity to Learn, Teacher Professional
Development, and Cities/urban education. There will also be state-by-state
analyses of the condition of mathematics and science education and a special
effort reviewing curriculum frameworks in all states.
