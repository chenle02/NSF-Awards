VLSI design and test equipment will be provided for researchers at the
University of Southern California for research in the Department of Electrical
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the study of
communication signal processing algorithm implementations on VLSI chips.
