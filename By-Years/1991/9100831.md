This request is for the support of participants for a meeting "Eukaryotic
Chromosome Replication" to be held at Cold Spring Harbor from September 4 to
September 8, 1991. This is the third such meeting with the goal of bringing
together scientists working in this rapidly expanding field in order to
encourage the exchange of data and techniques and to allow for the critical
evaluation of the status and progress of the on-going research.***//
