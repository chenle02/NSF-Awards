9355860 Gal This proposal, "Conference on Assessment Practices in Statistics
Education," was submitted to the Research in Teaching and Learning program as a
conference proposal. The aim of the conference is to inform current programs for
teaching education and statistics learning. The outcomes of the conference
include the publication of preliminary guidelines and recommendations for
education and a book on assessment issues in statistics education.
