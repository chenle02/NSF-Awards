ABSTRACT Bellcore PI: David Sincoskie NCR - 9320762 This award supports the
creation, operation and management of a Network Access Point (NAP) and provision
of related NAP services in the Chicago, IL and California area as called for by
Program Solicitation NSF 93-52. This is one of three for such NAP services (at 4
locations) to be funded as a result of that solicitation.
