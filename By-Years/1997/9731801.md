9731801 Goad This award to University of Texas will provide instrumentation of
oceanographic research for use on R/V Longhorn, a research vessel operated by
University of Texas marine Science Institute as part of the University-National
Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet. Specific instruments to be
acquired include weather station instrumentation and new transducers for the
echosounder. The shared-use instrumentation supported here will assist marine
scientists conduct studies from R/V Longhorn in the Gulf of Mexico in 1998 and
in future years.
