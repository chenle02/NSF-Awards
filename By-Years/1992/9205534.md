The Department of Mathematics and Center for Applied Mathematics and Statistics
at the New Jersey Institute of Technology will purchase a graphics workstation
environment at an estimated discounted cost of $23,000 a Silicon Graphics Indigo
with mass storage devices, and a color printer/plotter which will be dedicated
to the support of research in the mathematical sciences. The equipment will be
used for several projects, including in particular: 1. Microwave heating of
materials by Gregory Kriegsmann 2. Acoustic Focalization by Michael Porter 3.
Dynamics of Curved Detonations by Bruce Bukiet
