This conference is to be given by the Special Interest Group on Programming
Languages of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM SIGPLAN) to be held in
Cambridge. Mass. in April l993. Its objective is to create a significant
addendum to the definitive record of the history of programming languages which
began in l978.
