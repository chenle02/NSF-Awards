9352964 Fleischman Villanova University seeks to intimate a 6-week, residential
Young Scholars project in Biology and Mathematics for forty students entering
grades 11 and 12. The summer component combines course work with extensive
laboratory and research experience, and includes field trips and panel
discussions designed to increase students' interest and awareness of research
career paths. Academic year follow-up activities includes the implementation of
individual research projects designed by the students during the summer.
Students will be recruited throughout the Middle Atlantic metropolitan corridor
from New York to Washington, D.C.
