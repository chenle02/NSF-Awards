The Department of Statistics at the University of Arizona will purchase UNIX
workstations for research in the mathematical sciences. The equipment will be
used for several research projects, including in particular: 1) Group Sequential
Methods for Clinical Trials; 2) Spatial Spread of Epidemics and Their Control;
3) Critical Values for Markov Random Field Percolation.
