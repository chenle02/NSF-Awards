9400701 Ramond A physics workshop on "Yukawa Couplings and the Origins of Mass"
will be held February 11-13, 1994 at the University of Florida. The workshop
will address the question of the origin and the observed pattern of the masses
of the elementary particles. The question is a great puzzle, and discussion
among experts, which the workshop is designed to encourage is very important.
