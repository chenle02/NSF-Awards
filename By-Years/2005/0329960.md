The project is an inaugural conference on statistics in teacher preparation by
The American Statistical Association (ASA), with additional sponsorship and
organizational support from The University of Georgia's Department of
Statistics, College of Arts and Sciences, and the Department of Mathematical
Education, College of Education is developing the conference. The dates of the
conference are October 30 - November 1, 2003. A major goal is to create
awareness among teacher-educators of necessary preparation for K-12 teachers to
give instruction in probability and statistics. Another goal of the conference
is to build a leadership team that can periodically meet to examine issues and
provide guidance in methods to improve teacher preparation.
