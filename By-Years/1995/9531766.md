This award provides travel support for graduate students and recent post-
graduates who live in the United States to attend the First Alfven Conference in
Kiruna, Sweden, in September, 1996. The conference is titled "Low-altitude
investigation of dayside magnetospheric boundary processes" and will address
topics ranging from the solar wind input to optical auroral observations. The
organizers of the meeting are encouraging the broadest possible attendence and
have agreed to wiave conference costs and registration fees for young
scientists.
