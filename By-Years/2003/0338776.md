This award provides funding for a three year standard award to support a
Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) Site Program at the University of South
Florida entitled, "Engineering RET." The program will provide each year of the
program, a five week summer research experience for seven middle school
teachers, six high school teachers, seven pre-service teachers, and two
community college faculty from three Florida school districts.
