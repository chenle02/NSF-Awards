Description: This award is for support of a planning visit by Dr. Sameeh A.
Mansour of the National Research Center (NCR) in Cairo to the laboratory of Dr.
Mohyee Eldefrawi of the University of Maryland at Baltimore. The purpose of the
visit is to develop a collaborative research activity in the area of biosensors
development for applications in environmental and medical diagnostics. The
visitor is expected also to visit laboratories at other US institutions where
work in this area is being done. This project is being supported under the US-
Egypt Joint Fund Program, which provides grants to scientists and engineers in
both countries to carry out these cooperative activities.
