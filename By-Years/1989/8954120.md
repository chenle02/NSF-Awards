This project, developed under the leadership of Loyola University of Chicago, is
establishing the Chicago Prototype Center for Minorities. Cooperating
institutions and organizations, in addition to Loyola include the following:
Chicago State University, Northwestern University, the Chicago Public Schools,
and the Chicago Urban League.
