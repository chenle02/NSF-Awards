This project will support students to attend the International conference on
distributed systems (ICDCS) to be held in Minneapolis, MN in June 2011. ICDCS is
a premier conference in distributed systems area and it is important to expose
students to this conference. The students will be chosen based on a open
competition and preference will be given to students without support and those
that belong to underrepresented groups.
