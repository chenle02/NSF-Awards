ABSTRACT&lt;br/&gt;#0428089&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This is a dissertation project
to examine the historical development of environmental policy and management
regimes in Alaska and the Yukon. Utilizing ethnographic and historical
methodologies, the researcher will explore comanagement practices of the US and
Canada over the last 50 years in order to better understand aboriginal-state
relations. &lt;br/&gt;
