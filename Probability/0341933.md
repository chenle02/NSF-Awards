0341933 Burdzy The 2004 Seminar on Stochastic Processes will be held at the
University of British Columbia from May 22 to 24, 2004. The seminars have become
one of the most important regular seminar series for probabilists. They attract
and bring together an active group of eminent researchers and young specialists
in probability and stochastic processes. This award will provide travel support
for US participants. The funds will be used primarily for women and junior
researchers.
