9510116 HAYES This action creates a Bareboat Charter Party Agreement between the
United States of America and the Trustees of the Columbia University in the City
of New York for the operation of the Research Vessel MAURICE EWING. This action
is taken in accordance with Article 7 of Cooperative Agreement OCE 88-22860
"Replacement of the R/V CONRAD" which gives the Foundation the option to have
title to the R/V MAURICE EWING (Ex BERNIER) vested in the U.S. Government upon
completion of the Agreement. ***
