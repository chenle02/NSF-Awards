This action provides the first year's support for the Presidential Young
Investigator (PYI) Award, FY 87. This support includes the NSF matching to the
industry contribution provided to the P.I. Dr. Shing's nomination was considered
as "Must Fund: by the Divisional PYI panel which convened on September 25-26,
1986. The award will be used in support of the research on seismic resistant
design of structures.
