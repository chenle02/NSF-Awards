A Conference on Stochastic Analysis and Harmonic Analysis will be held at
Northwestern University from June 26-30, 2000. The conference is part of an
Emphasis Year in Probability Theory in the Department of Mathematics at
Northwestern. The funds will be used primarily for support of women and junior
researchers to allow them to attend the conference.
