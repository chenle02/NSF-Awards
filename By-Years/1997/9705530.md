9705530 Perry Partial travel support is provided for speakers and graduate
students attending the 1997 Symposium on Fundamental Investigations of
Tribological Interfaces to be held during the 1997 National Meeting of the
American Chemical Society. The symposium will explore molecular aspects of
tribochemical events, of nanomechanics, and of lubricant design at the molecular
level. ***
