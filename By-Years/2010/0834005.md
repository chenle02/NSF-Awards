The PIs and Co-PIs of grants supported through the NSF-NIH Collaborative
Research in Computational Neuroscience (CRCNS) program meet annually. This will
be the fourth meeting of CRCNS investigators. The meeting brings together a
broad spectrum of computational neuroscience researchers supported by the
program, and includes poster presentations, talks and plenary lectures. The
meeting is scheduled for June 1-3, 2008 and will be held at the University of
Southern California.
