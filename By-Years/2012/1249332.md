This grant provides travel support for students wishing to attend the 2012
Operating Systems Design and Implementation (OSDI) conference, which is to be
held in Hollywood, California, from October 8-10, 2012. The organizing committee
expects that more than 500 people attend the OSDI 2012 conference, with at least
100 student attendees.
