The Estuarine Research Federation (ERF) will sponsor the Ninth International
Estuarine Conference on October 25-29 1987 in New Orleans, Louisiana. This
series of biennial conferences is intended to assure effective exchange of
information among scientists from various disciplines engaged in current
research on estuarine and coastal processes. The Ninth Conference will convene
sessions on the following major topics: estuarine recruitment mechanisms, the
impact of pollutants on estuarine ecosystems, ecosystem modeling, regulation of
species distribution, sediment dynamics and technological requirements for
future estuarine research.
