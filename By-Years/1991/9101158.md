This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduate (REU) Site proposal
to provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in oceanic and
global change research associated witj the Bermuda times series site. It will
acquaint the students with the excitement and opportunities of academic research
and encourage them to pursue graduate studies and a career in ocean sciences.//
