9553531 Najar The California State University, Fresno will initiate a 6-week,
commuter Young Scholars project for 25 students entering grade twelve. The
disciplinary focus of the program is in mathematics with an emphasis on
combinatorics, graph theory, group theory, and number theory. Summer activities
will include seminars, a computer short course, field trips, discussions of
mathematics careers and the ethics of science. Four one-day sessions during the
school year will follow-up the summer component.
