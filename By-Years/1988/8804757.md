University of Kansas-Main Campus is being supported to establish a Research
Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) SITE. The purpose of this SITE is to
provide meaningful hands-on research opportunities in contemporary chemistry for
outstanding undergraduate students under the supervision of experienced research
chemists. For the summer of 1988, the program will support 8 students recruited
from Kansas and other institutions. Research activities will include studies in
the following areas: o Bioanalytical chemistry o Analytical chemistry o
Inorganic chemistry o Organic chemistry o Physical chemistry
