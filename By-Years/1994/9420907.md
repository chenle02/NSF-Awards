9420907 Slakey The American Physical Society will continue a series of regional
"Roundtables" which will bring together academic and industrial scientists and
administrators to identify critical scientific issues that directly address the
economic and educational challenges facing each region. The meetings will also
foster cooperation in research and education. ***
