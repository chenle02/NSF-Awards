HRD 03-28641 The Center for the Advancement of Hispanics in Science and
Engineering Education (CAHSEE) is nominated for the 2003 Presidential Award for
Excellence in Science, Mathematics, and Engineering Mentoring (PAESMEM) for its
significant achievement in the mentoring of more than 1,000 Hispanic and other
underrepresented minority students in the fields of science, mathematics, and
engineering.
