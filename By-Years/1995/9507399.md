9507399 McIntosh An international meeting, "Plant Mitochondria: From Gene to
Function" will be held at Durham North Carolina from April 7 to April 12, 1995.
The meeting will bring together different communities of scientists asking
questions about plant mitochondria. The overriding goal of this meeting will be
to bring together those individuals working on the function of mitochondrial
processes and those working on genetic structure and regulation to intermingle
their ideas and approaches. ***
