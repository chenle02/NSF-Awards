This is an ethnographic study of the organic chemists and flavorists who
develop, manufacture, and market synthetic flavorings. Research will involve
twelve months of multi-sited fieldwork in the United States and include from
participant observation and 20-35 in-depth qualitative interviews.

The research will result in a dissertation and trained professional. The
findings should be of value to food scientists and the food industry.
