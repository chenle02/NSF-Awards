9505348 Deutscher Description: This is an individual travel award, for Dr. Irwin
Deutscher, Professor Emeritus at the University of Akron. He is to visit several
institutes in India to discuss research areas in social sciences concerning
national identities and diversity among the various castes, nationalities, and
groups in the Indian citizenry. His primary host will be the Department of
Anthropology at Calcutta University. ***
