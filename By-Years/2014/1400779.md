This award is made as part of the FY 2014 Mathematical Sciences Postdoctoral
Research Fellowships Program. Each of the fellowships supports a research and
training project at a host institution in the mathematical sciences, including
applications to other disciplines, under the mentorship of a sponsoring
scientist. The title of the project for this fellowship to Karol Koziol is
"Functoriality of Pro-p-Iwahori-Hecke Algebras in Characteristic p." The host
institution for the fellowship is the University of Toronto, and the sponsoring
scientist is Dr. Florian Herzig. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This award is being
supported by funding from the Office of International and Integrative
Activities, International Science and Engineering Section, International
Postdoctoral Fellowship Fund.
