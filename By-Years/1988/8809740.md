Dr. Norbert Franz Scherer has been awarded a Postdoctoral Research Fellowship in
Chemistry. Dr. Scherer received his doctoral degree from the California
Institute of Technology under the supervision of Professor Ahmed H. Zewail. He
intends to continue his research at the University of Chicago under the
sponsorship of Professor Graham Flemming. Dr. Scherer's area of postdoctoral
research is femtosecond time-tesolved studies of the molecular aspects of
solute-solvent interactions. This is to include dipolar solvation and solvation
of a charge transfer species.
