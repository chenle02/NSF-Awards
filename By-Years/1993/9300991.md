* Recovery of Mangrove and Seagrass Ecosystems After HurricaneAndrew

Hurricane Andrew crossed south Florida in late August of 1992 producing
considerable damage in the coastal ecosystem. In this study a group of
researchers will assess damage to mangrove and seagrass habitats in the Shark
River area and document the initial stages of recovery.
