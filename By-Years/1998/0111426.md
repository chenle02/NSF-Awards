This award provides for acquisition of items of equipment to replace equipment
presently on loan to North Carolina State University from General Motors, the
U.S. Air Force, and NASA. Items to be acquired are a Nd:YAG pumped dye laser, a
digital oscilloscope, and ultraviolet lenses for existing digital cameras. They
are to be used for studies of interaction between chemical kinetics and fluid
dynamics in premixed and diffusion flames.
