This award provides funding to help cover the travel expenses of participants to
the "2014 Red Raider Mini-Symposium" to be held November 6-9, 2014, at Texas
Tech University in Lubbock, Texas.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The symposium will
feature five plenary talks on the topics of gravitational lensing, circle
packing, hyperbolic metrics, and other topics in Geometric Function Theory and
Mathematical Physics. There will be special talks of general interest from
Professor M. Dorff on "Movies and Mathematics" and Professor C. Beneteau on
"Women in Mathematics". Further, there will be a special reception during which
undergraduate and graduate students may meet and discuss mathematics with the
plenary speakers. Opportunities will be made available for eary career
mathematicians to present and discuss their work to participants at this
conference.
