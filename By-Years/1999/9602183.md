The 1996 Gordon Conference on Multiphoton Processes, held in New London, NH, wil
feature a series of presentations on the lastest developments in the field of
multiphoton, correlated ionization, coherent control, and spectroscopy. The
conference will be attended by both chemists and physicists, experimental and
theoretical. Particular attention is given to involving young scientists in the
program.
