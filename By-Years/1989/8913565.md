This award will support in part the International Workshop on the Physics and
Experimental Techniques of High Energy Gamma Ray and Neutrino Particle
Astrophysics, May 10-13, University of Arkansas at Little Rock. The workshop
will concentrate on discussions of: particle physics and astrophysics to be
studied by the next generation of detectors; the latest experimental and
theoretical results; and the technical advances related to the development and
operation of detectors.
