9531373 Gallian This REU site award provides support for six undergraduate
researchers in mathematics working under the supervision of a faculty advisor.
The area of research is discrete mathematics. The program will run for ten
weeks. Students each give a presentation of their work during the summer. At the
end of the program, the students will prepare their work for possible
publication.
