NSF representatives met with counterparts from the Czechoslovak Academy of
Sciences for review of joint activities during first year of the
U.S.-Czechoslovak Cooperative Science Program. In addition to management
overview, discussions featured university-based chemistry research as an
illustrative area for balanced and mutually beneficial projects.
