A revitalized calculus curriculum is being implemented in a laboratory equipped
with IBM PS/2 computers running MicroCalc. The graphing and numerical
capabilities enable students to experiment and to improve their intuition and
understanding of concepts. The institution will contribute an amount equal to
the award.//
