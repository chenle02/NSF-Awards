This grant provides funds to upgrade the water resources and environmental
engineering program at Pratt Institute. The major items of equipment consists of
equipment for the Hydraulics Laboratory: hydraulic benches, fluid circuit
systems, a pump and turbine test set; and the Environmental laboratory:
sedimentation, filtration, ion exchange columns, oxygen transfer cylinders,
activated sludge tanks and an anaerobic digester. This laboratory also provides
the opportunity to introduce water quality and quantity workshops for area high
school teachers and provides a strong basis form industrial interaction. The
institution is providing matching funds in an amount equal to or greater than
the funds provided by the Foundation.
