ABSTRACT CTS-9530048 Funds are supplied to support a postcongress symposium on
calorimetry to be held July 7-10, 1996, in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico. The symposium
will follow (and be linked to) the Eleventh International Congress on Catalysis.
Funds are used mainly to support travel between the Congress site in Baltimore
and the symposium site in Mayaguez.
