This award offers partial support for the 1992 Aspen Winter Astrophysics
Conference. These conferences have played a central role in the development or
topics at the frontier of astrophysics for some years, and should continue to
enhance fruitful collaboration between leaders in the discipline and many
promising younger investigators.
