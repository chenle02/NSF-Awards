Finding new and creative ways to educate engineering and science students forms
the focus of the proposed research. Efforts will be directed to determine how
current techniques of artifical intelligence, computer visualization, and
engineering pedagogy can be combined or enhanced stimulating students to excel
in their studies while retaining quality education.
