9521795 Mitchell This award adds two higher education institutions to the
MOREnet state network: William Jewell College Cottey College Faculty and
students at the institutions benefit from access to the resources of the
Internet, including libraries and supercomputers. In addition, they are able to
communicate and collaborate with colleagues at other campuses in pursuit of
research and educational projects. The award provides partial support of the
project for two years.
