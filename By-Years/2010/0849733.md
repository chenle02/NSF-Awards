The Arctic Research Support and Logistics program provides support in Greenland
for science programs funded by the Division of Arctic Sciences, as well as
various other NSF Divisions and other Government agencies. This award provides
funds for the research support provided in Thule, Greenland by 821 Air Base
Group of the U.S. Air Force.
