This award supports a Research Experiences for Undergraduates site proposal to
provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in the marine sciences,
in order to aquaint them with the excitement and opportunities of academic
research and to encourage them to contemplate going on to graduate studies and
eventually careers in research.
