The nuclear physics research program at a predominantly undergraduate
institution, Oberlin College, is supported by this grant. The investigator and
his students will study the nuclear reactions and structure of light radioactive
nuclei and stable lithium isotopes using facilities at the National
Superconducting Cyclotron Laboratory and Notre Dame University.
