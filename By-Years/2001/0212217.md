This award provides funding to Johns Hopkins University, Center for Computer
Integrated Surgical Systems and Technology (CISST) for the support of a
collaboration getween the Center, the Mashantucket Pequot Tribal Nation (MPTN),
The American Indian Higher Education Consortium (AIHEC), New Mexico State
University (NMSU), The Center for American Indian and Alaskan Native Health
(CAIANH), and Howard Communitiy College (HCC). The purpose of this collaboration
is to develop an innovative pilot program modeled in part after both the REU and
RET programs to attract and retain the future scholars, role models, educators
and leaders from American Indian communities.
