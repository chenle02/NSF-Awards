2015 Siegman International School on Lasers will take place place 2-7 August,
2015 in Amberg, Germany. The Siegman International School is a week-long program
that exposes students to in-depth learning of lasers and their applications from
internationally recognized academic and industry leaders in the field. The scope
includes all aspects of lasers and photonics. This program consists of lectures,
networking events, professional development programs, poster presentations and
research sharing and brings together the top 100 student applicants from around
the world. With a commitment to ensuring diverse representation, OSA strived for
a minimum of 10% attendance from developing nations.
