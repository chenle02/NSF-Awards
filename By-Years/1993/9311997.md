ABSTRACT: PI-Gerstenfeld, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, MSS-9311997: The
objective of the workshop is to investigate the preparation of engineering
students for managerial decision making. Topics to be discussed are: engineer's
role as managers, global competitiveness, and how best to integrate management
training in engineering curricula.
