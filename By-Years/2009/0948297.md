Abstract:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The Travel Grant Request:&lt;br/&gt;Research
Partnership with Romanian Universities&lt;br/&gt;Communications and Training
Simulations in Disaster Management &amp; Mitigation written by Pamela McCauley
Bush, Ph.D., C.P.E.; University of Central Florida; Orlando, FL
(mcbush@mail.ucf.edu) is recommended for funding for the amount requested of
$6,635; co funded by both ENG and CISE directorates.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The
objective of this grant is to establish an international collaboration with two
female engineering faculty members in Romania to further the research being
conducted in by the Human Factors and Simulation in Disaster Management Research
team at the University of Central Florida. The UCF HFDM Research is lead by Dr.
Pamela McCauley Bush and the collaborative partners are Dr. Valentina Balas and
Dr. Mihaela Albu. The long term goals of the collaboration are to further
research in an international environment that has similarities to the current
research environment in the state of Florida in order to validate results and
extend activities. Additionally, the goals include faculty interaction for
creating broader reaching outcomes, student exchange and international
dissemination.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Specific Travel
Requested:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Trip A:&lt;br/&gt;a. Meet with Professor
Valentina Balas for research discussions&lt;br/&gt;b. Plenary Speaker at IEEE
SOFA Conference: Topic Advances in Simulation&lt;br/&gt;Technologies for
Disaster Management&lt;br/&gt;- The PI has been invited to provide a plenary
speech on my research for the upcoming conference, IEEE International Workshop
on Soft Computing Applications (SOFA)&lt;br/&gt;- Location: Szeged - Hungary and
Arad - Romania.&lt;br/&gt;- Date: July 29 - August 1, 2009&lt;br/&gt;Trip
B:&lt;br/&gt;a. Meet with Professor Mihaela Albu for research
