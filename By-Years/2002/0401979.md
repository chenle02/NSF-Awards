This award provides funding for a 3 year continuing award to support a Research
Experiences for Teachers (RET) Site program at Georgia Institute of Technology
entitled, "STEP-UP: Summer Teacher Experience in Packaging, Utilizing Physics--
RET Site," under the direction of Dr. Leyla Conrad. This site will host 36 in-
service high school physics teachers from metropolitan Atlanta public school
systems. The STEP-UP program will provide a comprehensive high school teacher
research experience at the microelectronics Packaging Research Center (PRC), an
NSF Engineering Research Center (ERC), in collaboration with the School of
Physics at Georgia Tech.
