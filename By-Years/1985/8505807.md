An MTS material test system will be acquired for a large number of research
projects concerning automotive structural components using high strength sheet
steels, fracture and fatigue mechanism of adhesively bonded joints,
characterization of cyclic delamination growth in fibrous laminated tough
composites, positron annihilation studies of fatigue cycling damage, study on
the constitutive properties of rocks, fracture studies
