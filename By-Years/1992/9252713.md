A symposium will be held a New York University on February 28, 1992 involving 60
participants that represent the university/college and pre-college sectors, the
private sector and private foundations. The symposium will highlight the efforts
of the National Science Foundation to strengthen pre- college and undergraduate
education. The symposium will also serve to encourage participants to become
more actively involved in the design and development of new teacher preparation
programs. The invited plenary speakers will address issues related to
preparation of mathematics and science teachers, recruitment and retention of
women and minorities and promotion of technical literacy.
