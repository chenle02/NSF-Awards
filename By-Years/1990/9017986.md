This is a request for partial support for a conference entitled "Future
Directions for Lasers in Medicine and Surgery II". The conference is to be held
on February 24 - March 1, 1991 in Palm Coast, FL. The conference, the second in
a series, will bring together engineers, scientists and physicians to assess
progress made on the application of lasers to medicine. This conference should
aid in developing future avenues of research in an important and growing area of
research.
