The PIs and Co-PIs of grants supported through the NSF-NIH-ANR-BMBF-BSF
Collaborative Research in Computational Neuroscience (CRCNS) program meet
annually. This eleventh meeting of CRCNS investigators brings together a broad
spectrum of computational neuroscience researchers supported by the program, and
includes poster presentations, talks, plenary lectures, and workshops. The
meeting is scheduled for September 28-30, 2015 and is hosted by the University
of Washington.
