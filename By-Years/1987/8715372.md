Dr. J. Beer, a leader in the Combustion field, has organized the US
contribution to the International Symposium on Coal Combustion to be held in
Beijing, China, in September of 1987. He will give the keynote lecture on Coal
Science and Technology. China is one of the three leading nations in Coal
production. The exchanges and contacts expected from Dr. Beer's visit will
enhance the US position in managing this major source of domestic energy.
