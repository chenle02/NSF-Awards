The 5th International Symposium on Meson-Nucleon Physics and the Structure of
the Nucleon will be held. Currently, there is great interest worldwide in this
topic. Experts will gather to assess the experimental and theoretical status of
the field and discuss future plans. Strong international participation is
expected.
