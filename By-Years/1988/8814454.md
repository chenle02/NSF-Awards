Professor Jerome Friedman of Stanford University will be principal lecturer at
an NSF-CBMS Regional Conference to be held at George Washington University,
Washington, DC in June of 1989. The lectures will discuss the recently developed
general method of projection pursuit for analyzing multidimensional statistical
data.
