This workshop will provide a forum for US and German researchers to discuss
recent advances in protein purification, new advances and concepts in
chromatographic separation, membrane associated recovery processes and equipment
design for downstream processing. The workshop is to be held at the Gesellschaft
fuer Biotechnologische Forschung (GBF), Braunschweig, Germany, during October,
1991. A total of 13 U.S. participants, 8 from universities and 5 from industry,
has been planned. Equal participation from Germany is anticipated.
