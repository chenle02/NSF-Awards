Abstract

Funds are provided for the support of MARGINS Program office at Boston
University. The MARGINS Program provides a focal point for research on the
dynamic evolution of continental margins. The Program office aims to enhance the
outcome of individual research projects by providing communication, data
archiving and access, planning for multi-disciplinary experiments and providing
advice through the steering committee, holding workshops and theoretical
institutes, and developing an educational and outreach program.
