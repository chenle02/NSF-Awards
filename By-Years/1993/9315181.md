9315181 Kumar This award of $180,000 is for curriculum development in
computational science and engineering. The focus is on to develop new Railroad
Transportation Cuuriculm for undergraduates and starting graduates. The core
courses and selective courses will be deveoped in Mechanical Engineering, Civil
Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, and Business. The
certificate program will be developed to satisfy the need of the industry by
providing a series of related courses in the rail engineering area for
retraining staff. A introductory graduate courses and program will also be
developed.
