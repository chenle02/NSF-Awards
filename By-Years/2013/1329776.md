The PIs and Co-PIs of grants supported through the NSF-NIH-BMBF Collaborative
Research in Computational Neuroscience (CRCNS) program meet annually. This ninth
meeting of CRCNS investigators brings together a broad spectrum of computational
neuroscience researchers supported by the program, and includes poster
presentations, talks, plenary lectures, and discussions. The meeting is
scheduled for June 9-11, 2013 and is hosted by the Massachusetts Institute of
Technology.
