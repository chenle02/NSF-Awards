The 2003 EPSCoR National Conference will focus on future opportunities in
research and development, strategies to address the current and future
challenges to the S&T enterprise, promoting economic growth through research and
development partnerships and collaborations, and increasing the participation of
underrepresented groups in science, research and education. The conference will
be held in Las Vegas, Nevada from September 7-9, 2003. Participants will include
representatives from national and state governments, academe and the private
sector. The Nevada EPSCoR staff will organize and manage the conference.
