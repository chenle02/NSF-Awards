The properties of hot, rotating nuclei will be modeled using the finite
temperature Hartree-Fock-Bogoliubov formalism. Mean values and fluctuations of
nuclear shapes and pair gaps will be computed. Electromagnetic strength
functions and rotational damping widths will be determined.
