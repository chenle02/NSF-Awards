This Special Minority Award provides a year of support for Arnoldo R.
Laracuente, a graduate student at the University of Colorado. The award will
allow Mr. Laracuente to finish his course work expeditiously and reach the
research participation phase sooner. This award is an integral part of the
strategy of the National Science Foundation to strengthen and increase minority
participation in the field of physics.
