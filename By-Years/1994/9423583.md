9423583 Crawford The proposed project involves theoretical and numerical studies
of ideal plasmas. The research concentrates on numerical algorithms to solve the
Vlasov equation, short time scale dynamical studies of instabilities and the
nonlinear dynamics of coherent lineraly unstable waves. ***
