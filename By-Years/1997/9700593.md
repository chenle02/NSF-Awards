9700593 Willis This award to Oregon State University provides shipboard
technical support, shore-based support, instrument maintenance and instrument
calibration for researchers using R/V Wecoma, a research vessel operated by the
Oregon State University College of Oceanographic and Atmospheric Sciences as
part of the University National Oceanographic Laboratory System research fleet.
The technical support awarded here will assist NSF-funded ocean researchers
conduct a diverse suite of oceanographic studies in the North Pacific Ocean in
1997. ***
