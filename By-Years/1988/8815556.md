This project is a follow-up to NSF Grant No. 8617912. It will involve the
convening of a workshop of 18 designated regional program coordinators to
discuss recommendations and plans for the National Earthquake Hazards Reduction
Program. These recommendations will focus on ways to improve research
applications in the program.
