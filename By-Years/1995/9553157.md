9553157 Selen This Presidential Faculty Fellow award supports research on the
properties of B-meson decays observed in the CLEO detector at the Cornell
University colliding beams facility CESR. The PI will continue a program of
detector research and development to improve particle identification techniques.
He will maintain and expand a community- outreach educational effort in the
local area and develop materials for a freshman level introductory mechanics
course at the University of Illinois. ***
