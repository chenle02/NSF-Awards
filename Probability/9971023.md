9971023 Kurtz The 26th Conference on Stochastic Processes and their Applications
will be held in Beijing, China, from June 14-28, 1999. This conference provides
a valuable opportunity for US researchers to interact with prominent researchers
from other countries. This award will provide travel support for US
participants. At least half of the funds will be used to provide support for
junior researchers.
