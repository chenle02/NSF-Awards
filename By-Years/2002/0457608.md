An Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers planning meeting has been
awarded to determine the feasibility of the University of California-Los Angeles
joining the existing Wireless Internet Center for Advanced Technology. The
research activities of the proposed UCLA research site will utilize the
resources of the existing "Wireless Internet for the Mobile Enterprise". The
main focus of the research will be on wireless application technologies for
enterprise-class needs.
