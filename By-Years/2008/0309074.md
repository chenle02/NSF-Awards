0309074&lt;br/&gt;Sandovol&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This is a planning
proposal for Dr. Eric Sandvol, the University of Missouri, to visit Nanjing
University to develop a collaborative project on tectonics and seismic structure
of the Tien Shan Mountain belt. This is an important region in China for
understanding seismic hazard determination. The proposed visit will allow Dr.
Sandvol to lay the foundation for developing this US-China collaborative
project. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
