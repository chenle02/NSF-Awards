The workshop will focus on providing directions for data mining. Specifically
the focus will be on the following areas:&lt;br/&gt;1. Data Mining for
Bioinformatics&lt;br/&gt;2. Data Mining for Security&lt;br/&gt;3. Semantic Web
and Data Mining&lt;br/&gt;4. Data Mining for Pervasive
Computing&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Prominent researchers in data mining and machine
learning as well as government program managers will be invited to participate
in the workshop. The output will be a report describing the areas of future
research in data mining. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
