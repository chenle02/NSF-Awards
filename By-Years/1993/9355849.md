9355849 Reddish The project which was proposed originally was intended to study
how students' expectations affect their learning in a number of specific cases.
The results are intended to lead to modification of materials produced at the
University of Washington which are aimed at helping students articulate and
explicate their views of the process and content of science. The project
involved two primary dynamic studies looking for student attitudes;and, the
other focused on factors influencing students' self-debugging skills
