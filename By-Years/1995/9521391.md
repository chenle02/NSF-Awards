9521391 FOWLER This award will allow 20 US psychologists, half of them within
ten years of their Ph.D., to travel to the XXV Interamerican Congress of
Psychology in San Juan, Puerto Rico, July 9-14, 1995. This Congress will provide
a forum for North American scientists to make contact and share their experience
with their Latin- American counterparts. ***
