9732536 Baskaran This award to Texas A&amp;M University will provide a new high-
resolution gamma ray detector for use in the existing radioactive counting
laboratory in Galveston. It will provide needed additional capability for
studies of marine processes, particularly in the area of colloidal chemistry of
the waters of the Gulf of Mexico. ***
