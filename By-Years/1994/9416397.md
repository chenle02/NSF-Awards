94416397 Scott This project provides funding for an evaluator to study the
industry/university interactions occurring in two Industry/University
Cooperative Research Centers over a three year period. One is the Center for
Process Analytical Chemistry at the University of Washington and the other is a
multi-university Center for Design and Analog/Digital Integrated Circuits at
Washington State University, University of Washington, Oregon State University
and State University of New York at Stoneybrook. The evaluator is highly
qualified to perform this study. The Program Manager recommends Dr. Craig Scott
be awarded $42,000 for three years for this project.
