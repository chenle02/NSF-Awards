This symposium aims to bring together researchers from computer system
architecture and networking systems to promote cross-disciplinary research
activities and outreach. The funds requested will be used to provide travel
support for students, post-doctoral researchers, and junior faculty to attend
the symposium and have an outlet for exposure and involvement in these
activities.
