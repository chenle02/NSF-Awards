This award is made under the high performance connections portion of NCRI's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64. It provides partial
support for two years for an OC-3 connection to the vBNS. Applications include
projects in astrophysics, physics, computer science and languages. Collaborating
institutions include the National Center for Supercomputing Applications, Apache
Point Observatory, Fermilab National Accelerator Laboratory, Johns Hopkins
University, Carnegie-Mellon University, the National Astronomy and Ionosphere
Center, Stanford University, University of California-Berkeley and Columbia
University.
