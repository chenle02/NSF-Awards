9527991 Elias This Americas Program award will support travel to Peru and
related expenses of Dr. Luis R. Elias, Director of the Center for Research and
Education in Optics and Laser (CREOL), at the University of Central Florida
(UCF). His collaborator in this project is Dr. Manfred Horn, of Universidad
Nacional de Ingenieria, Lima (UNI). The purpose of the visit is to establish
collaborative research and educational programs in lasers and optics between UCF
and UNI. The joint research program will involve not only faculty researchers,
but physics and engineering students also. ***
