9322079 Merlic UCLA Dr. Craig A. Merlic and other members of the Chemistry
Department of UCLA are being supported in a new Research Experiences for
Undergraduates site in Chemistry. For the period 1994-6, nine undergraduates
will spend ten weeks each summer actively engaged in a variety of research
projects. A unique feature of the program will be the focus on bioorganic
chemistry.
