Formation and Change of Public Beliefs about Global Warming How the issue of
global warming will be dealt with depends critically on public perceptions
regarding the seriousness of the problem and its likely consequences. This study
examines the processes through which American citizens form their opinions on
global warming, what determines the strength of those opinions, and how those
opinions change in response to media coverage and personal experience.
