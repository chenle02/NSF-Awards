This is an interagency agreement between NSF and the Department of State for
reimbursement of costs for administrative services provided to NSF staff
stationed in Tokyo, Japan (NSF/Tokyo Office) and Paris, France (NSF/Europe
Office). These costs are a necessary part of NSF's overseas activities.
