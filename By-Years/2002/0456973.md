An Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers planning meeting has been
awarded to determine the feasibility of Texas Tech University joining the
existing Center for Engineering Logistics and Distribution. The focus of the
research site will focus on Industrial Engineering that will emphasize the human
interaction aspects and impact on logistics in the work environment.
