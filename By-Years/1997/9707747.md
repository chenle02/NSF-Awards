The Department of Mathematics at U.C. L.A. will purchase computer equipment
which will be dedicated to the support of research in the Mathematical Sciences.
The equipment will be used for several research projects, including in
particular: Computation in Algebra, Computation in Algebraic Geometry, and
Computation in Interacting Particle Systems.
