9505721 Buller This proposal seeks to provide a direct Internet connection
between Native American communities including Community Colleges that as of yet
no network connectivity. Training for the classroom use of the Internet will
provided and will be done in conjunction with the AISES Teacher Enhancement
Project. This will provide over 100 educators of American Indian students with
their first look at network use and networked information. Thus the major goal
of facilitating access to the Internet for as many Native American as possible
will be provided.
