The 19th Annual International Symposium on Computer Architecture will be held
May 19-21, 1992 in Queensland, Australia. It is co-sponsored by the Association
for Computing Machinery and the IEEE. Since 1980, the symposium has been held
outside the U.S. about every three years. The grant provides travel support for
20 U.S. scientists to attend the symposium.
