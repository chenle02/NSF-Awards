This award provides funding for a three-year Combined Research-Curriculum
Development (CRCD) program, entitled "CRCD in Geoenvironmental Engineering," at
Kansas State University, under the direction of Dr. Lakshmi N. Reddi. The
overall objective of this project is to develop a research-integrated curriculum
in geoenvironmental engineering to serve the needs of senior-level undergraduate
and first-year graduate students in allied science and engineering disciplines.
