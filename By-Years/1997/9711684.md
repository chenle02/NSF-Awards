9711684 Pisias This award to Oregon State University will provide a new
shipboard core analysis laboratory (containerized van) for use on research
vessels operated as part of the University-National Oceanographic Laboratory
System. It will also provide support for modifications to piston coring systems
operated by Oregon State University, and available to researchers at all U.S.
universities. ***
