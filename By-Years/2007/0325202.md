This award will support, in part, the travel expenses of ten (10) graduate
students/postdoctoral fellows for participation in the "IUPAC International
Symposium on Ionic Polymerization," to be held at the Tremont Hotel, Boston,
Massachusetts, on June 30 - July 4, 2003. This symposium is being organized by
Professor Rudolf Faust, University of Massachusetts, Lowell, and Professor
Roderick P. Quirk, University of Akron, Akron, OH. The symposium is sponsored by
the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry, the Polymer Chemistry
Division of the American Chemical Society, the University of Massachusetts,
Lowell, and the University of Akron.&lt;br/&gt;***
