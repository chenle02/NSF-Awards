Laboratories and classrooms equipped with Macintosh computers running
Mathematica are being used by students to learn calculus in a radically
different mode. Materials developed by Duke University's Project CALC (funded by
NSF) are being modified for a liberal arts college setting. The institution will
contribute an amount equal to the award.
