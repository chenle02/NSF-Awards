C.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The US 2013 (National) Combustion Meeting will bring
together experts of a wide range of areas related to combustion and fire, from
academia, industry, and government agencies, where exchange of ideas and
research results and brainstorming take place among attendees. The proposed
funding is to provide travel support to students who will be presenting their
research at the meeting.
