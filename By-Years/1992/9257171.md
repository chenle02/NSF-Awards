This young investigator's research focuses on studies of the Earth's interior
utilizing high-pressure and high-temperature experiments. Specific research
interests include the physics and chemistry of high-pressure minerals and melts
and the thermal structure of the Earth's interior. Her research is conducted at
the Institute of Tectonics of the University of California at Santa Cruz.
