This is an award to provide a support for the investigator's participation in
the Eighth International Conference on Chemistry for Protection of the
Environment that will be held in Lublin, Poland from September 16-20, 1991. The
investigator will chair a technical session, present an invited paper and
participate in an International Roundtable discussion of issues addressed by the
conference including pollution prevention, waste minimization, advanced
physicochemical treatment of water and wastes, fate of chemical pollutants in
the environment, and advances in tracking pollutant diffusion and dispersion in
surface and groundwater under environmental stress.
