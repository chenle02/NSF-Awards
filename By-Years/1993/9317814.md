Carol Ehrhardt SBR- 9317814 This exploratory investigation is a feasibility
study of a highly endangered monkey species, the Sanje mangabey. This is one of
two rare species restricted to the Uzungwa Mountains of southeastern Tanzania.
The objectives are to a) set up transect lines for survey purposes, b) locate
study groups, c) assess the possibilities of habituation, d) experiment with
various observational methods, e) collect data on morphology, vocalizations,
social structure, and feeding ecology, f) record vocalizations, and g) establish
ties with Tanzanian officials, researchers, and students. ***
