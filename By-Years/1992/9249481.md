This supplement funds the second year of a three-year continuing award under
the "Self-Sufficient Partnership for Research" Program at the University of
Texas, Arlington Industry/University Cooperative Research Center for Advanced
Electron Devices and Systems. The Center continues to meet the renewal criteria
requirements of the Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers Program.
The Program Manager recommends the University of Texas, Arlington be awarded
$50,000 for the second year of three-year continuing award.
