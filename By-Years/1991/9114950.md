A workshop will be held April 20-21, 1991, in which researchers from the
various fields of cognitive science will discuss current and prospective lines
of research in cognitive science, resulting in a report that will advise NSF
personnel regarding ways in which scientific progress can be facilitated
productively by new or continuing NSF activities and support.
