This is an attendance and travel grant for the 22nd Annual International
Symposium on Computer Architecture on June 22-24, 1995 in Santa Margherita
Ligure, Italy. It is co-sponsored by the Association for Computing Machinery and
the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers. The symposium, through a
combination of invited talks, panel sessions, tutorials, workshops and refereed
paper presentations, continues to serve the various needs of the computer
architecture research community. It is an important conference in the computer
architecture area. This grant provides support to help several graduate students
attend the symposium.
