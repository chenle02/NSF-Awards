The Control Systems Society of the IEEE will hold its 25th annual meeting, the
Conference on Decision and Control (CDC), in Athens during December, 1986. The
conference is generally considered the premier control theory conference in the
world, and holds a wide variety of both applied and theoretical sessions ranging
from robotics to adaptive control, computer-aided design, system identification,
process control, automated manufacturing, etc. It generally attracts around 700
attendees, and produces high quality proceedings.
