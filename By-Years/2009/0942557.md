This award is funded under the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009
(Public Law 111-5).&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;A request is made to fund additional
and back-up instrumentation for the &lt;br/&gt;R/V Pelican, a 116 foot Coastal
vessel operated by the Louisiana Universities Marine Consortium (LUMCON) as part
of the University-National Oceanographic Laboratory System (UNOLS) research
fleet. The award includes one:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; Teledyne DMS-05 Attitude
Sensor&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Broader Impacts: The acquisition,
maintenance and operation of shared-use instrumentation allows NSF-funded
researchers from any US university or lab access to working, calibrated
instruments for their research, reducing the cost of that research, and
expanding the base of potential researchers.
