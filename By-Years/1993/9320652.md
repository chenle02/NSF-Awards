9320652 Hauck The American Political Science Association will provide travel
grants to junior American scholars participating in the Sixteenth World Congress
of the International Political Science Association. The World Congress will be
held in Berlin, Germany from August 21-25, 1994. The theme of World Congress
will be Democratization. ***
