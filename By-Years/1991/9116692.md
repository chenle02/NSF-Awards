The objective of the 28th Society of Engineering Science Annual Meeting, to be
held at University of Florida, November 6-8, 1991, is foster exchange of
information among 200-250 scientists and engineers. The particular goal of this
proposal is to secure NSF support to subsidize travel and meeting expenses for
100 graduate students enrolled in Ph.D. programs throughout the nation. Women
and minorities will be particularly encouraged to attend.
