This award provides funds to help establish a core laboratory for DNA structure
analysis to support automated DNA sequencing and synthesis for a large,
multidisciplinary group of investigators in the Department of Basic and Clinical
Research of the Research Institute of the Scripps Clinic. The laboratory will
also serve as a shared resource for development and acquisition of new
technologies and instrumentation. The investigators are engaged in meritorious
