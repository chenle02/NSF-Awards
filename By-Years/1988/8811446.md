This grant will provide travel funds, primarily for graduate students and junior
faculty, to attend the 1988 Solid-State Sensor Workshop, from June 6-9, 1988.
This workshop is a key event in the field of sensors and actuators, and this
grant will enable an important contingent of researchers to attend.
