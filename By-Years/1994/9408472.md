9408472 Davis This workshop will define new research directions needed to
advance high-performance computing in the designated National Challenge areas:
health care, education, environment, manufacturing. The workshop will focus on
software issues that differ from those raised by the Grand Challenge problems.
To assure concrete discussion, the workshop will focus on health care
applications. Participants have been selected to provide broad representation
across the high-performance computation community. Application experts will also
participate. ***
