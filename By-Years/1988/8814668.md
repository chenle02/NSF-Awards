This grant is for support for travel by 21 black American physicists to the
First Bouchet International Conference in Trieste, Italy. This conference is
sponsored by the International Center for Theoretical Physics (ITCP) and the
Black American Friends of ITCP. It will be attended by African as well as
American physicists. Its purpose is to share research results, discuss current
topical issues in physics, address problems of mutual concern, and create a
continuing organization. It is funded under the Minority Research Initiation
Program of NSF.
