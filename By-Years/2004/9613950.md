The College of the Redwoods requests support from NSF to connect its Mendocino
Coast Center in Fort Bragg, California to the Internet. This grant will be used
to help the institution get access to the Internet. Pacific Internet, a local
service provider, will provided the the lines used to access to the Internet.
The Mendocino Coast Center needs the Internet connection to be able to access
information resources throughout the state, the nation and the world. The
connection will benefit the faculty, staff students of this institution as well
as the community in general.
