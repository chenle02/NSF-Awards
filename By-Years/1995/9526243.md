9526243 This action provides support for the U.S. Navy Arctic Submarine
Laboratory for baseline and special data acquisition during an extended
scientific cruise of a submarine under the Arctic Ocean ice cover in the summer
of 1995. The basis for this support is a Memorandum of Agreement among four
operational units of the Navy and three civilian federal agencies, the National
Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, the U.S. Geological Survey, and the
National Science Foundation. ***
