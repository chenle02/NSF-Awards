93-53807 Engstom 1993 National EPSCoR Conference This proposal requests funding
for the 1993 National EPSCoR Conference. The conference will be held in Rapid
City on November 7-9, 1993. The conference will involve speakers, posters, and
discussion on a variety of topics focusing on the theme, "EPSCoR - Reaching Out
to Each Other and the Nation." Special travel grants will be awarded to up to
five individuals from each state who represent the future leadership of EPSCoR
in that state. A conference proceedings will be prepared afterwards for
distribution to the participants.
