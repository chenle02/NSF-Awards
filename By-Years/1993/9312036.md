9312036 Gould The Museum of Comparative Zoology at Harvard University houses one
of the world's premier collections of invertebrate fossils. The collections is
an important resource for scientists. This project will provide compactors for
storage of the specimens. This will provide space for growth and more efficient
curation of the collections. It will also permit MCZ to become more active in
acquisitions.
