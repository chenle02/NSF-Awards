9528002 Singh The International Conference on Beam Processing of Advanced
Materials will cover the latest research activities around the world with both
invited and contributed presentations. The conference will include speakers from
under-developed countries. Advanced materials processing by laser, ion beam,
electron beam, microwave, radio frequency and plasma will be covered at this
symposium, which is organized by the American Society of Metals.
