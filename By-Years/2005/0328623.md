HRD 03-28623 Dr. Lisa A. Pruitt is a professor of Biomedical Engineering at
University of California at Berkley. Mentoring is a significant part of the
undergraduate research program in Biomedical Engineering and is incorporated
into the development of curriculum in this program. A training aspect to her
program seeks to develop mentoring skills in students who may serve as peer
mentors. Dr. Pruitt serves as a mentor and as an excellent role model.
