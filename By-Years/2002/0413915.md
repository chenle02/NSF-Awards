This action continues funding for a third year for a Minority Postdoctoral
Research Fellowship. The research focuses on the regulation of reproductive
development in nomadic finches, with emphasis on the neuroendocrine basis for
the extent to which species adhere to a strictly seasonal schedule of breeding.
