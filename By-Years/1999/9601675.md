Academic Research Infrastructure: Development of a Novel Systems Software for
Multimedia and High Performance Computing This Academic Research Infrastructure
award supports the development of a distributed network of workstation for
multi-media research. The research projects supported by the award include video
server applications, distributed circuit simulation, test generation, and device
level multimedia scheduling.
