The project involves 15 undergraduate students doing research in chemistry,
biology and engineering in ongoing research projects done at the university. The
students are from both the university and associated regional colleges and
universities. The research areas in engineering deal with chemical engineering,
civil engineering, environmental engineering and biotechnology.
