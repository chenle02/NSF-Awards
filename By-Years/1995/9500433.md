9500433 Ellis The "Research Experiences for Undergraduates" in the Physics
Department of the University of Toledo will be continued. This program places
eight students each summer in the research programs of the Department, which
cover a number of topics in Physics and Astronomy. The students will be given a
number of opportunities to interact professionally. ***
