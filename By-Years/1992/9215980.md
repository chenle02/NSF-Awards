This award provides infrastructure support for the development of the research
and educational activities in the Department of Mathematics and Computer
Sciences at the University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras Campus. The program will
have a graduate component, including a new Ph.D. program, as well as
undergraduate and precollege activities. The support includes computing
resources, as well as personnel to support the program. In its initial phases,
there will be an emphasis in research in the areas of (1) computer algebra, (2)
discrete mathematics for computer science, and (3) numerical methods.//
