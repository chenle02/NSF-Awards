This project expands the laboratory instructional and training of undergraduate
students in the areas of geotechnical analysis and design through the use of
advanced geotechnical analysis equipment, electronic instrumentation, and
laboratory sterilization equipment. This equipment involves design and analysis
of soil substructures, facility design, municipal process design,, analysis data
base management, project development, and documentation.
