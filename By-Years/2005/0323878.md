This supersymmetry conference will focus on the following topics: Formal Aspects
of SUSY; SUSY Phenomenology, Experimental Searches and Bounds; Higgs Physics,
Neutrino Masses and Oscillation, CP violation, GUTS, Formal Aspects of String
Theory as well as astrophysical and Cosmological Connections. Speakers include
Cumrum Vafa, Tony Tyson, Leonard Suskind, Ignatios Antoniadis, and
representatives from several major experiments such as Tevatron Run II, Lep, and
the B-Factory.
