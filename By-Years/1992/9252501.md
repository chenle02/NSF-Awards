Through a planning grant, Volunteer State Community College is coordinating a
regional effort. Based on surveys with the local rural high schools, critical
aspects concerning mathematics instruction in the region will be identified.
Through a series of workshops involving consultants, a regional coalition
strategy for improving the quality of math instruction will be developed.//
