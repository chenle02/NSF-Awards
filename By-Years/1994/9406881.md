9406881 Linsky This contract is for initial data entry services in three
Postdoctoral fellowship programs operated by the Biological Sciences
Directorate. It provides for capturing the initial data related to applications
to the Plant postdoctoral Fellowships, Minority Postdoctoral Fellowships, and
Molecular Evolution Postdoctoral Fellowships programs. The number of
applications is estimated to total about 250. Data will be entered in the NSF
mainframe computer and the file jackets will be assembled using the computer-
generated forms. ***
