Abstract OPP-9707905 LeMoine The purpose of this project is to initiate the
identification and documentation of photographs taken between 1908 and 1954 in
the Thule district of Greenland by explorer Donald B. MacMillan. These
photographs, now in the Peary-MacMillan Arctic Museum at Bowdoin College, depict
MacMillan's exploratory expeditions as well as everyday life in Greenland. This
award fundq an initial field trip to assess the feasibility of a larger
ethnohistorical/anthropological project.
