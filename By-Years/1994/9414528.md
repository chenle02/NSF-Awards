This is an attendance and travel grant for the 21st Annual International
Symposium on Computer Architecture on April 18-21 in Chicago, Illinois. It is
co-sponsored by the Association for Computing Machinery and the IEEE. The
Symposium, through a combination of invited talks, panel sessions, tutorials,
workshops and refereed paper presentations, continues to serve the various needs
of the computer architecture research community. It is an important conference
in the computer architecture area. This grant provided support to help twenty
graduate students attend the symposium.
