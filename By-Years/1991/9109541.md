This is a Research Planning Grant to enable Dr. Jackson to initiate studies on
the regulation of expression of the key metabolic enzyme, argininosuccinate
synthetase. It is anticipated that this award will help Dr. Jackson obtain
preliminary results necessary to launch an independent research career.
