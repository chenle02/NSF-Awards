The Hilltopper Mathematics and Computer Science Scholarship (HMCSS) project
provides support to 20 students for a period of three years. The target audience
will be current freshmen enrolled at St. Edward's University and transfer
students articulating from Community Colleges. The HMCSS projectl builds on the
existing College Assistance Migrant Program (CAMP) which is designed to offer an
educational opportunity to the sons and daughters of migrant and seasonal farm
workers.
