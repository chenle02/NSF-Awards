9422982 Pinkerton The University of Wisconsin Madison is extending WiscNet at 56
kbps to 17 Wisconsin institutions, comprising seven independent colleges and ten
units of the Wisconsin Technical College system. The 17 schools are: Alverno
College Mid-State Technical College Bellin College of Nursing Milwaukee Area
Technical College Blackhawk Technical College Mount Mary College College of the
Menominee Nation Nicolet Area Technical College Fox Valley Technical College
Northcentral Technical College Gateway Technical College Northeast Technical
College Lakeland College Silver Lake College Lakeshore Technical College
Wisconsin Indianhead Technical Marian College of Fond du Lac College Faculty and
students will benefit from access to the resources of the Internet, including
libraries and supercomputers. In addition, they will be able to communicate and
collaborate with colleagues at other institutions in pursuit of research and
educational projects. The award provides partial support of the project for two
years.
