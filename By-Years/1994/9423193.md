9423193 A Block Travel Grant award covers expenses incidental to the
participation of students and young scientists in the Moriond Workshop on Tests
of Fundamental Laws in Physics on January 22-28, 1995 in Villars sur Ollon,
Switzerland. The annual Moriond workshops provide an opportunity for close
interaction among physicists in a number of disciplines whose common interests
in general fundamental problems provide the theme of the meetings. The sessions
are organized around presentations in diverse fields of physics - Dark matter in
cosmology, Clocks, and Low-energy nuclear tests of fundamental laws. ***
