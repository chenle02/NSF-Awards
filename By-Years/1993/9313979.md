9313979 Oppo This award will cover partial costs of purchasing an atomic
absorption spectrophotometer (remaining costs to be provided by the
institution). The instrument will be devoted to analyses of Cd in biogenic
calcite, a technique which shows much promise for reconstructing former level of
biologic production and carbon flux in the deep ocean. ***
