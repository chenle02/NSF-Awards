This project provides travel grants to participants in the August 1986
International Federation of Automatic Control Workshop in Beijing, China. The
topic is Modelling, Decision and Games with Applications to Social Phenomena.
This meeting has a strong interdisciplinary nature and will attract researchers
from different parts of the world and different backgrounds.
