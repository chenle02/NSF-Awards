This conference is to provide a forum for participants from the global
biotechnology community and the US to discuss recent advances in the "Recovery
of Biological Products". This is the sixth in a series of conferences that have
become a leading international forum for the discussion and development of new
areas in the exploration and commercialization of biological products,
especially pharmaceutical products. The conference is to be held in Interlaken,
Switzerland, on September 20-25, 1992. Registration is to be restricted to 225
participants.
