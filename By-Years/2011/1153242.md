This is a proposal to fund the travel of two Alaska Native researcers, MA
student Allan Hayton and Tribal scholar Dewey Hoffman, to present their research
on Gwichin and Koyukon Athabaskan language and culture at the international
World Indigenous Peoples Conference on Education in Cusco, Peru, August 2011.
