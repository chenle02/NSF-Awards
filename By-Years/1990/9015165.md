This proposal request funds to establish a travel award program for American
scientist to attend the 3rd International Congress of Comparative Physiology and
Biochemistry which will be held in Tokyo, Japan, August 25-30, 1991. The award
program will be run by the Division of Comparative Physiology and Biochemistry
of the American Society of Zoologists and administered by that Society as
well.//
