Project Summary&lt;br/&gt;This project is providing scholarships to 29 low-
income, academically talented engineering students. There are student service
programs available to the recipients that focus on retention, academic
excellence, industrial or research experience and professional development. In
addition, the scholars have access to career guidance and placement services.
