A series of conferences and workshops are proposed to assess the state of the
art, the state of practice, and the needs for research and development in
North,Central and South America, and the Caribbean. The fields of Repairing,
Rehabilitation, Retrofitting, Renovation, Reconstruction, and Rebuilding of
Americas' infrastructure will be covered. One conference per year for two years
are proposed in one of the following topics: Low Rise Building Structures,
Highway Pavements, Bridges, Soil Related structures, Live Public Services
Infrastructure Systems. Topics to be selected with NSF concurrence. A three day
conference with public attendance will assess the state of the art in each
specific topic. A fourth day workshop for the speakers only will assess the
needs for specific research and development of common interest to participating
institutions, agencies and countries.
