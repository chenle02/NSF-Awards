This grant provides travel support to students so that they can attend the
International Conference on Software Maintenance (ICSM) which this year is being
held at William &amp; Mary in Williamsburg, Virginia in September 2011. ICSM is
a high quality conference addressing the research needs of the software
development lifecycle. Attending enables students to hear the latest results in
the field, meet with other researchers, and build community with the next
generation of researchers, including the global community.
