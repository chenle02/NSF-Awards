The PI is using this award to organize a one-day workshop on writing successful
NSF CAREER Proposals. The proposed workshop is to be conducted on May 18th, 2012
near Arizona State University, Tempe, AZ. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The objective of
this workshop is to bring together experienced proposal writers, recent NSF
CAREER awardees, program directors from the NSF, and young faculty who are
interested in gaining knowledge on how to turn their visionary ideas into
successful CAREER proposals and jumpstart their successful careers.
