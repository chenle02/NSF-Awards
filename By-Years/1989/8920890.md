A twenty-node multiprocessor system (16-node hypercube + 4-node hypercube) will
be provided for researchers at the University of South Florida for research in
the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. This equipment is provided
under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer and Information
Science and Engineering program. The research for which the equipment is to be
used will be in parallel algorithms for vision, and parallel algorithms for
reasoning.
