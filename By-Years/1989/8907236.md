Image - Processing Equipment will be provided for researchers at the University
of Minnesota for research in the Department of Electrical Engineering. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which the
equipment is to be used will be in the areas of Image Compression, Edge
Detection for Object Recognition, and Textured Image Segmentation.
