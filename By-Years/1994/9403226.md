9403226 Toutanji A conference/workshop is scheduled for August 29-31, 1994 at
The University of Puerto Rico at the Mayaguez campus. The conference/workshop
will discuss repair/rehabilitation of the infrastructure of the Americas. Papers
will be presented, and a session will be devoted to research projects which
would be of common interest and coordination between researchers of different
countries. ***
