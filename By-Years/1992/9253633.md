Funded under the new Presidential Faculty Fellow program (PFF), research and
teaching will be supplemented in the area of fluid dynamics related to
biomedical systems. The research is experimental, and is focused on particle
tracking in branching and curved tubes. The research is applicable to flow
through large blood vessels, and other arterial flows.
