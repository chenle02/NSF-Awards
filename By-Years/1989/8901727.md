A half-day symposium entitled "Molecular and Genetic Organization of Plant
Genomes" will be held between August 6 and August 10, 1989, at the annual
meetings of the Genetics Section of the Botanical Society of America in
conjunction with the American Institute of Biological Sciences at the University
of Toronto. This symposium represents part of a continuing effort by the
Genetics Section to encourage the application of molecular genetics to botanical
problems.
