An existing research laboratory will be upgraded to a high- speed networking
research laboratory. Projects relating to high- speed networking shall be
supported including: * Priority scheduling of multimedia traffic. * Multicast
communication and group management. * Congestion control in high-speed networks.
* Task scheduling in a distributed environment.
