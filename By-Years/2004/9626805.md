9626805 Peck The mechanism by which stars produce energy is, with the exception
of cataclysmic terminal events, the burning of nuclear fuel. The details of how
this occurs has been a field of intense study for the past sixty years,
necessitating knowledge of many subfields such as nuclear physics and stellar
evolution. ***
