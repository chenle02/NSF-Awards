This award funds a Research Fellowship in Plant Biology for two years. This
project is entitled "Genetic Analysis and Molecular cloning of the auxl Gene of
Arabidopsis." The research Fellowships in Plant Biology are granted to
applicants trained in traditional areas of plant biology who wish to extend
their training in new techniques and to those who wish to gain experience
working with plants. These fellowships are designed to increase the number of
outstanding young scientists in plant biology.
