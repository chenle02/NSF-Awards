9406613 Fornaess The Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan
will purchase equipment which will be dedicated to the support of research in
the Mathematical Sciences. The Department has undertaken several projects
involving the use of advanced Unix workstations to (i) explore Mathematics using
computational and visualization tools; and (ii) give vigorous computer-assisted
proofs.
