This proposal requests funding for carrying out partial support for young
physicists to attend a six-day lecture/workshop at Vanderbilt University on
subjects related to cosmology/astrophysics, nuclear, and particle physics. The
subjects raised are related to each other and will be addressed by experts in
their respective fields.
