This is an interagency agreement between NSF and the Department of State (DOS)
to reimburse DOS for the cost of administrative services provided to NSF staff
stationed in Tokyo, Japan, and Paris, France. In addition, support is given to
NSF travelers to Tokyo, Paris, New Delhi, Cairo, and Tel Aviv. These costs are a
necessary part of NSF's overseas activities.
