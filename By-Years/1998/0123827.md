This grant provides partial support of the fifth in a series of international
conferences on applications of airborne remote sensing to be held at San
Francisco, CA on 17-20 September 2001. It is estimated that approximately 800
people will attend the San Francisco meeting and that the technical program will
consist of at least 300 papers on different aspects of airborne remote sensing.
The purpose of the conference is to foster communication and collaboration
between the scientific, governmental, and commercial communities in the use of
recent developments in airborne remote sensing technology.
