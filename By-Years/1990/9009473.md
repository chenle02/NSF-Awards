This is the 6th Bridge Workshop to be conducted under the auspices of the UJNR
Panel on Wind and Seismic effects. This meeting will take place in May 1990 in
California and Nevada. Papers will be presented and site visits made to bridge
structures to observe new concepts and construction details. Proceedings will be
published and distributed.
