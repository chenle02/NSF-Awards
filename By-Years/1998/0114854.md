Abstract

Proposal number: 0114854 PI: Anne Cunningham Institution: UC Berkeley Title:
Teachers' Disciplinary Knowledge of Reading and its Relationship to K-3 Pedagogy
and Student Achievement

This is a planning grant for a project that will investigate the current level
of K-3 elementary school teachers. disciplinary knowledge, beliefs, and
knowledge calibration in reading processes, development, and pedagogy. The
project will use a set of workshops and development activities in the planning
of how to conduct a series of experimental, longitudinal, and qualitative
studies that will lead to a greater understanding of the type and depth of
knowledge needed to be an effective teacher of reading (as evidenced by student
performance) and the models of professional development that facilitate the
growth of teachers. disciplinary knowledge, beliefs, and pedagogy in reading and
literacy.
