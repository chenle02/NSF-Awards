This conference identifies new research needs and opportunities in the field of
dynamic systems and control. This workshop is a follow-up activity to four
separate mini workshops. The goal is the review of the results of the mini
workshops and the identification of cross-cutting and interdisciplinary issues.
A report identifying research needs in the dynamic systems and control area will
be generated.
