9554451 Johnson Syracuse University will initiate a 4-week, summer residential,
Young Scholars project in interdisciplinary science and mathematics for 50
economically disadvantaged students from New York State entering grade 8. The
program will provide high ability and high potential middle school student with
early experiences in mathematical problem solving, laboratory science, computer
science, and research in a highly charged environment. The summer component
combines course work with extensive laboratory and research experience, and
includes field trips designed to increase students' interest and awareness of
research career paths. Academic year follow-up activities include an extension
of activities and research in which the students were involved during the
summer.
