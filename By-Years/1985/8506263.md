This project provides equipment to support research at Rutgers University. The
equipment comprises a Hewlett Packard minicomputer together with assorted
support equipment. It will be used to support research on controlling large
flexible structures, a heart model, control systems for robots, bipedal walking
models, and walking aids for the handicapped.
