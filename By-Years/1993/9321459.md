9321459 Wilkins UC-Riverside Dr. Charles L. Wilkins and other members of the
Chemistry Department of the University of California at Riverside are being
funded for a Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) site in Chemistry.
For the period 1994-6 , ten undergraduate students will spend ten weeks each
summer actively engaged in a variety of research projects. A unique feature of
this program will be the focus on bioanalytical an environmental analytical
chemistry.
