9318821 Clark This Conference will highlight scientific as well as engineering
aspects of enzyme technology. The program will include sessions devoted to new
biocatalysts, advances in biosensor technology, metabolic and protein
engineering, metal-based biocatalysts, and new biomolecules with enzyme-like
properties. Industrial applications are also to be presented. ***
