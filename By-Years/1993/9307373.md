ABSTRACT This action is to help defray the costs of American scientists who are
attending an international meeting. The meeting is the XXIV Interamerican
Congress of Psychology which will bring together American and South American
behavioral and social scientists for the purpose of interacting in meaningful
scientific dialogue. The meeting provides opportunities for these scientists to
share their research experiences as well as a chance to initiate collaborations
amoung and between scientists of diverse nations.
