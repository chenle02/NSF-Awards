An international seminar on deduction organized by Prof. Wolfgang Bibel
(Technische Hochschule Darmstadt, Germany), Prof. Koichi Furukawa (Keio
University, Japan), and Dr. Mark Stickel (SRI International, Menlo Park,
California) was held 20-24 March l995 at the Dagstuhl Seminar Center in Wadern,
Germany. About 50 researchers working in the field of automated deduction from
around the world were invited to participate.
