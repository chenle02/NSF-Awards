This conference will be held at Johns Hopkins University on May 16-19, 1988 to
mark the establishment of the Japanese-U. S. Mathematics Institute. The
conference will feature participants from the U. S. and Japan active in the
fields of algebraic analysis, number theory, and algebraic geometry. Emphasis is
on encouraging the interaction between the first of these fields and the other
two. Participant support is available.
