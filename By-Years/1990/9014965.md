Funds will be used to partially support travel for U. S. participants to the
1990 IEEE International Workshop on Information Theory. The workshop will be
held at the Congresfacility Koningshof in Veldhoven, The Netherlands, on June
10-15, 1990. Priority for the supplemental travel awards will be given to junior
researchers or to plenary speakers who would otherwise be unable to attend the
workshop.
