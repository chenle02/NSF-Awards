This award is made under the high performance connections portion of NCRI's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64. It provides partial
support for two years for a DS-3 connection to the vBNS. Applications include
projects in atmospheric sciences, astronomy, computer science, high energy
physics, telemedicine, and geosciences. Collaborating institutions include most
of the Internet 2 institutions.
