This workshop will bring together over 100 key researchers in computer science,
engineering, and educational technology to discuss future trends in research and
to develop a research agenda. The workshop will have six parallel working
sessions which will focus discussion and develop working papers on a set of key
research topics in the field of educational technology. These papers will be
assembled with an overview paper and distributed widely.
