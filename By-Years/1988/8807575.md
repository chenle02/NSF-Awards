This is an individual award in the Postdoctoral Research Fellowships in Plant
Biology Program for 1988. The Fellow will conduct research on "Pathogenesis
Genes of the Corn Smut Fungus, Ustilago Maydis" in the laboratory of Dr. Ira
Herskowitz, University of California, San Francisco, California. The award
provides training in research in a critical area of plant biology to a promising
young scientist. It should help to contribute to the future vitality of the
Nation's scientific effort and lead to advances in our knowledge and
understanding of the basic biology of plant systems.
