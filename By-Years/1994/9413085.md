9413085 Conway Cornell University and corning will develop an innovative,
practice-oriented, full-semester course in modern manufacturing to be offered to
master's students in Engineering, Management, and Labor Relations. This
15-credit course will be the student's only class for an entire semester. Its
innovative, total immersion approach to improving manufacturing education could
serve as model for use by other universities.
