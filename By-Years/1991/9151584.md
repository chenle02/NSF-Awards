The University of Wisconsin - La Crosse is equipping their biochemistry
laboratory for undergraduates to isolate, purify and characterize an enzyme from
previously uninvestigated sources. The students experience the excitement of
scientific discovery within a regularly scheduled course by using standardized
protocols.
