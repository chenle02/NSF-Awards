This is the fourth Bridge Workshop under the auspices of the Joint UJNR-Panel on
Wind and Seismic Effects. The workshop is for three days, in San Diego,
California preceding the annual meeting of the representatives of the two
countries in Washington, D.C. The Workshop will discuss current research
projects and plans for future cooperative programs.
