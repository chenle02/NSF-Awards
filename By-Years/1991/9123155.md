This grant will support the Plasma Science Committee of the National Academy of
Sciences-National Research Council. The Plasma Science Committee convenes
meetings, receives briefings from agencies, and forms study panels to prepare
reports on a broad range of issues important to the health of basic and applied
plasma science.
