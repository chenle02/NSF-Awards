This grant will continue support for a summer REU Site at the Molecular Physics
Laboratory at SRI International, Inc. Students will be recruited nationally, and
will work with SRI staff scientists who will serve as mentors. Research topics
will cover a broad range of atomic, molecular, and optical physics.
