* Behavior Traditionally Connected Timber Frames

* Standard Grant
* John Scalzi

Project will develop analysis capability for connected timber frames, such as
mortise and tenon joints, and fork and tongue joints in post and beam
construction. Results will be useful in analyzing the safety of historic
structures and assure safety in new construction.
