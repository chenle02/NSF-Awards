9531633 Findley This award provides funding to purchase shipboard
instrumentation in support of NSF-funded research. The instruments will be used
aboard the R/V SEWARD JOHNSON, a 176 foot vessel, which is owned by Harbor
Branch Oceanographic Institution (HBOI). Under the terms of a coopeartive
agreement, HBOI operates the R/V, and RSMAS provides technical support for
research cruises. Instrumentation funded includes some network upgrades for the
ship, a CTD and water sampler, slip rings, a transducer array, and data
acquisition hardware. The instrumentation requested is for support of NSF-funded
cruises aboard the R/V SEWARD JOHNSON during 1996 and will enhance the
scientific capability of the vessel in the future. ***
