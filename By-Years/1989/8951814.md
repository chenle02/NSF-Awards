This grant supports the development of a state-of-the-art computing environment
for undergraduate majors in computer science and the upgrade of the curriculum
by enhancing the existing courses in graphics and operating systems and
developing courses in networking and artificial intelligence. The computing
environment purchased includes an advanced lab of networked multitasking
workstations; a classroom equipped for demonstration; and curriculum development
resources. The institution is proving matching funds in an amount equal to or
greater than the funds provided by the Foundation.
