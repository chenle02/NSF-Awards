The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at California State
University, Los Angeles will establish a microcomputer laboratory for advanced
undergraduate courses. The computation nature of the numerical analysis,
numerical calculus, linear programming courses will be revised and applied
linear algebra will be added to the curriculum. Two thirds of the student body
are minorities. The laboratory will consist of 30 computers with access to all
campus computers, video projector and printing stations. The grantee is
providing an equal sum obtained from non-Federal sources.
