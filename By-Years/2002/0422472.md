Santa Fe Community college is awarding 30 scholarships annually to financially
needy and academically talented students majoring in mathematics or engineering
who intend to transfer to a baccalaureate degree program in one of the CSEMS
eligible disciplines. The mathematics project, GatorTRAX, a program for students
in grades 6 - 12 is identifying promising candidates and encouraging them to
apply for a scholarship. The CSEMS scholarship is increasing the number of women
and minorities enrolling in curricula designed to lead to a career in
mathematics or engineering.
