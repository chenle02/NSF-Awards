This award is made under the high performance connections portion of NCRI's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64. It provides partial
support for OC-3 ATM connections to Rice University, Baylor College of Medicine
and the University of Houston for two years. Among the numerous applications are
those involving distributed parallel computing, immersive collaborative
environments, imaging for molecular genetics and structural biology,
computational biology and bioinformatics, and computational engineering.
