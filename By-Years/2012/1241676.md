This award supports the third annual NSF Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) program's
Principal Investigator meeting, Gaylord National Hotel, National Harbor,
Maryland, October 3-5, 2012. This working meeting enables increased NSF
interaction with projects, visibility into progress of the CPS research
portfolio, and information exchange and interaction across CPS research
projects. The PI meeting also provides an opportunity for interaction among CPS
research community and industrial participants, international speakers, and
officials from U.S. government agencies.
