This award provides funding to Loyola Marymount University, for the support of a
Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) Workshop at LMU on March 1, 2003. This
workshop will bring together NSF Principal Investigators supported by four NSF
Directorates, K-12 teachers, and community college faculty from the Los Angeles
area for the purpose of describing the opportunities for teachers and community
college faculty to participate in a research experience with an NSF-funded
principal investigator.
