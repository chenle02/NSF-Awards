9729181 Bacon The principal investigator and his colleagues at the Woods Hole
Oceanographic Institution will continue to management center for the U.S. Joint
Global Ocean Flux Study (U.S. JGOFS). This will include the planning of
workshops for the U.S. JGOFS Synthesis and Modeling Project and operation of the
Data Management Office as well as coordination support for closing out the U.S.
JGOFS process studies and the continuation of the time-series stations at Hawaii
(HOT) and Bermuda (BATS).
