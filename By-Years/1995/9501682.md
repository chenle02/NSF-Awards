9501682 Mehregany This short course will provide an overview of MEMS technology
with an emphasis on how to make use of the MultiUser MEMS Process (MUMPS)
program at MCNC. This program exists to provide low-cost, easy access to MEMS
technology for all domestic organizations including academia. The short course
will be attended by twenty academics who will incorporate the MEMS capabilities
discussed within undergraduate and graduate courseware. ***
