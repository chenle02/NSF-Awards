9314611 Spiess The investigators have installed sets of long lived precision
transponders on the Juan de Fuca plate and on the Cleft segment of the Juan de
Fuca ridge. This program will re-survey these installations during the summers
of 1994, 1995, and 1996 to determine the pattern of strain accumulation at the
ridge crest and the plate are moving relative to the Pacific and North American
plates.
