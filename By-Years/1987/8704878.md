This is an award to purchase an atomic spectrophotometer for the purpose of
metals analysis in connection with on going environmental engineering research
at Unversity of Nevada. This equipment will be used for the following research
projects: 1) hydrogen peroxide treatment of organic hazardous waste spills on
soils, 2) photochemicals oxidation of wastewater effluents, 3) calibration of
dynamic water quality models by in-situ microcosms, 4) characterization of
mercury in the suspended load.
