The aim of this project is to increase the scientific literacy and laboratory
proficiency of undergraduate students in the following academic courses: "Marine
Biology","Phycology", "Marine Science for Teachers", "Invertebrate Zoology
Seminar", "Research Problems", "Developmental Biology", and "Aquaculture".
