A file server will be provided for researchers at Columbia University for
research in the Department of Computer Science. This equipment is provided under
the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer and Information Science and
Engineering program. The research for which the equipment is to be used will be
in the areas of system building, especially software development environments
and programming languages.
