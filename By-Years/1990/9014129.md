* Study of the Feasibility of Making an Electron-Positron     Plasma (Physics)

A series of computer simulation studies is carried out with the objective of
establishing the feasibility of demonstrating and studying an electron-positron
plasma. Such a plasma has been assumed to be unrealizable and would have unique
properties of fundamental interest.
