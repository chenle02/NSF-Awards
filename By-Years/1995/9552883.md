9552883 Meisegeier Gallaudet University will initiate a 4-week, summer
residential, Young Scholars project in the fields of biology, chemistry, and
physics which will offer science enrichment activities for 15 students entering
grades 9, 10, 11. The summer program is for deaf students conducted in a
research oriented environment. Students will be provided opportunities to
participate in classroom instructions, field trips, hands-on laboratory
activities, and research under the leadership and guidance of university
professors.
