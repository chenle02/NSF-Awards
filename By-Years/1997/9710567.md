This award is made under the high performance connections portion of NCRI's
"Connections to the Internet" announcement, NSF 96-64. It provides partial
support for the installation and mperation of a OC-3 ATM connection to a
cooperative PoP connecting their local service provider NYNEX's ATM network and
the the Very High Performance Backbone Network Service (vBNS). Applications
include: - Astrophysics - High Energy Particle Physics - Molecular Cellular
Biology - Whole Plant Physiology - Digital Library Applications - Data Center on
Social Science - Medical Applications - Networking Research Collaborating with:
- Indiana University - University of Cambridge - National Center for
Biotechnology Infomatics - Berkeley Drosophia Genome Project The award provides
partial support of the project for two years.
