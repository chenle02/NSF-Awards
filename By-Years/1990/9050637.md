Students are using an Advanced Computer Laboratory, equipped with networked SUN
SPARC workstations and Mac IIci computers, in their advanced mathematics
courses. All mathematics majors are required to encounter contemporary
mathematical computing tools for symbolic, graphical, statistical, and numerical
purposes.
