* Development of Hybrid (FRP &amp; Steel) Reinforcement

This project will investigate the feasibility of combining a steel center core
encased with various types of like material such as glass, aramids, carbon, etc,
to form a hybrid reinforcement to resist corrosion when used as reinforcement in
concrete structures.
