&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This award provides funding for a three-year Combined
Research-Curriculum Development (CRCD) program, entitled "Real-World Wireless
Communications in the Graduate and Undergraduate Curriculum," at the University
of Notre Dame, under the direction of Dr. Thomas E. Fuja. The overall objective
of this project is to bring elements of the Notre Dame digital communications
research and the capabilities created by the wireless facility, the WAND Lab,
into the undergraduate and graduate electrical engineering curriculum at Notre
Dame. &lt;br/&gt; &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
