This is a proposal requesting travel funding for fifteen participants in the
ACM/SIGIR International conference on Research and Development in Information
Retrieval. This conference is being held in cooperation with the (U.S.)
Association for Computing Machinery (AMC)/SIGIR Special Interest Group in
Information Retrieval (SIGIR). Topics covered include information retrieval
theory, artificial intelligence and information retrieval, interface issues,
hypertext and multimedia systems applications, and implementation issues.
