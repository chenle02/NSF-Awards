9508285 Hagstrom The Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University
of New Mexico will purchase hardware and software to better equip its research
computing laboratory. The laboratory will be used by several ongoing research
projects in pure and applied mathematics including: 1. Cohomology and Geometries
Associated to the Sporadic Simple Groups 2. Computational Analysis of Wave
Propagation Problems with Multiple Scales 3. Multiphase Flow and Interfaces
