This proposal seeks funding to continue a series of quarterly workshops starting
in June 2004 focused on coordination of PKI infrastructures and bridge
certificate authorities among higher education and federal government. Federal
agency participation includes DOE, Department of Education, NIH, GSA, NIST, and
NASA. A host of institutions with existing or developing PKI infrastructures,
and Internet2, will also participate. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
