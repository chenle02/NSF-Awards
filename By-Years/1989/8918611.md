This grant is for International Travel Support. An invited lecture is to be
given by the grantee at the XIXth. Symposium on Advanced Problems and Methods in
Fluid, Mechanics, Warsaw, Poland, September 3-8, 1989. The grantee will also act
as session chairman at the symposium. In addition, he is to spend ten days in
pursuit of a collaborative research effort in Warsaw at no additional expense to
this grant. The topic of the lecture and collaborative research is the effect of
ambient fluid stratification on the wake characteristics of blunt obstacles.//
