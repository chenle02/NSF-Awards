This award is made as part of the FY 2012 Mathematical Sciences Postdoctoral
Research Fellowships Program. These fellowships support a research and training
plan at a host institution in the mathematical sciences, including applications
to other disciplines. The title of the research fellowship awarded to Ana
Caraiani is "Geometric aspects of the Langlands program." The host institution
for the fellowship is University of Chicago, and the sponsoring scientist is Dr.
Matthew J. Emerton.
