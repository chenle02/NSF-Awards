The Young Investigator (NYI) intends to express and assemble biological
superstructures using recombinant DNA technology, and evaluate the relevant
intracellular kinetic and equilibrium parametersin order to maximize formation
of these superstructures. This will require the tools of cellular and molecular
biology, recombinant DNA technology, chemical kinetics, and mathematical
modeling.
