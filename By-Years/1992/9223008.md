A graphics computing facility including an FDDI interface, high-resolution
color graphics monitor and framebuffer, stereoscopic viewing glasses, and 3D
interface equipment shall be acquired to support research efforts in computer
and information science and engineering. This, and AVS scientific visualization
software will be used for rendering, visualization, and animation research
projects in mathematics, physics, virtual reality, scientific computation,
discrete and combinatorial structures, and parallel program performance.
