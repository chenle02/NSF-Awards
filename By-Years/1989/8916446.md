In this project, the P.I. will continue theoretical and numerical studies of
mesoscale eddy formation and evolution using Contour Dynamics (CD). Specific
focus will be on meandering and eddy detachment in thin jets, formation of warm
outbreaks, and the evolution of Meddies.
