This award supports the renewal of the cooperative agreement between the
National Science Foundation and the National Center for Supercomputing
Applications (NCSA). Dr. Larry Smarr is the Director of the Center. NCSA is one
of the national supercomputer centers funded by the Division of Advanced
Scientific Computing and provides supercomputer access to scientific researchers
as well as training/educational and other related scientific services. The
effective date of the agreement is October 1, 1990. This is an administrative
action.
