Travel funds will be provided for 50 U.S. and foreign scientists to attend the
Seventh Marcel Grossman Meeting on Recent Advances in General Relativity at
Stanford University, Stanford, California, on July 24-30, 1994. This meeting
will provide for dissemination of the latest developments in gravitational
physics.
