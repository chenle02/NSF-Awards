This is a unique conference which brings together academic and industrial
researchers working in VLSI, architecture, CAD tools, and theory. It provides
forum for interaction between people from different backgrounds, with different
priorities and different perspectives. It has one session on at any one time as
apposed to other IC design conferences, and this allows better sharing of
information among different among different research topics in VLSI. The
conference is designed to contribute to a better understanding of the Nation's
scientific and engineering research base.
