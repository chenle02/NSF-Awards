9521267 Burns This award provides 56Kbps Internet access to seven sites through
Westnet: College of Santa Fe Santa Fe, NM Luna Vocational Technical Las Vegas,
NM Institute Navajo Community College Shiprock, NM The School of American
Research Santa Fe, NM San Juan College Farmington, NM University of Wyoming
Family Casper, WY & Practice Residency Program Cheyenne, WY Faculty and students
at the schools benefit from access to the resources of the Internet, including
libraries and supercomputers. In addition, they are able to communicate and
collaborate with colleagues at other institutions in pursuit of research and
educational projects. The award provides partial support of the project for two
years.
