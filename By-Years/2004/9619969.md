9619969 Greene This award to San Jose State University Foundation will provide a
new echosounding system for use on the research vessel R/V Point Sur, operated
by Moss Landing Marine Labs as part of the University National Oceanographic
Laboratory System research fleet. The shared-use instrumentation supported here
will assist ocean researchers conduct studies in the North Pacific Ocean in 1997
and in future years, and will be especially useful for studies along the
California margin. ***
