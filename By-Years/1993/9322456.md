ABSTRACT: 93322456, PI-ROWE: This project is to initiate a REU SITE in
Oceanography at Texas A &amp; M University. The program will support 12
students/year for three years in a 10 week program. Students will work either on
the Galveston or College Station campus. The unifying scientific theme for this
project will be the structure and function of coastal ecosystems.
