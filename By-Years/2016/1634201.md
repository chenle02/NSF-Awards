This award provide support for the annual SuperDarn workshop to be held at the
Geophysical Institute, University of Alaska in Fairbanks. The award would
provide student travel support as well as other auxillary conference expenses
including the expense required to enable visitors to visit the Poker Flat Rocket
Range. This award is co-funded between the AGS-Aeronomy program and the PLR -
Polar Programs (Antarctica), Astrophysics and Space Sciences.
