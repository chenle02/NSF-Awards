9321013 Covert This award assists the MIDnet mid-level network in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks and to the rest of the Internet. The
award, made under the guidelines published in NSF Program Solicitation 93-52,
partially funds one T-3 (45 million bits per second) connections. Anticipated
duration of the award is four years.
