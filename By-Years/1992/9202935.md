Albright College is establishing a connection to NSFNET via a 56,000 bits per
second line through the PREPnet mid-level network. The connection enhances
collaborations of campus scientists with colleagues in fields including
chemistry, biology, religion, English and political science. This award funds
part of the costs for two years.
