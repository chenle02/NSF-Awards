Support will be provided for U.S. participation in a workshop on research needs
relating to the pollution of surface runoff water from urban and urbanizing
land. The objectives of the workshop are to review current knowledge, define
research needs, and facilitate the exchange of information among participants as
a step toward cooperative research on the pollutional effect of urban stormwater
runoff.
