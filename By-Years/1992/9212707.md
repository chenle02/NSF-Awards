The award supports the purchase of an infrared microscope, video camera,
recorder and a sectioning saw equipment dedicated to support measurement of
delamination profiles in silicon/polymer/metal microelectronic structures,
measurement of stresses at bimaterial corners, and measurement of residual
stresses in the silicon component of encapsulated microelectronic devices.
