9318682 Zoback This award provides financial support for project planning and
coordination efforts associated with a proposed long-term (e.g. 10 year) project
to drill and core deeply into the San Andreas fault zone and to conduct a broad
range of scientific investigations in and around the drill hole. The planning
and coordination efforts are being conducted in close collaboration with the
USGS and the DOE (Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory). ***
