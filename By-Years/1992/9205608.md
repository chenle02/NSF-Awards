This award provides partial support for a full-time research technician who
will operate the electron microprobe system in the Center for Lithospheric
Studies at the University of Texas in Dallas.The University is committed to
providing the remaining funds required. The electron microprobe instrument
provides quantitative chemical analysis of small inhomogeneous rock and mineral
samples for the research and teaching programs of members of the Geosciences
faculty at the Center for Lithospheric studies.
