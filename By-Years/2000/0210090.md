&lt;br/&gt;This study will synthesize findings of the portfolio of research
projects funded by the ROLE program. Research methods include the analysis of
survey findings, document analysis, and interviews with appropriate elite
informants. Dissemination plans include presentations at conferences (including
the ROLE PI meeting) and scholarly publication.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
