0233369&lt;br/&gt;Jabbari&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The 41st IEEE Decision and
Control Conference (CDC02) is the premiere, annual international conference on
systems and automatic control research. It is sponsored by the IEEE Control
Systems Society and conducted in cooperation with the Society of Industrial and
Applied Mathematics, and the Institute For Operations Research and the
Management Sciences. This year it will be held at the Venetian Hotel, in Las
Vegas, Nevada, December 10-13, 2002. Pre-conference wrkshops will be held on
December 8 and 9, 2002. The Conference provides an excellent opportunity for
researchers to present the latest developments in the field, promote new ideas,
and discuss and evaluate past accomplishments. The technical content of the
Conference has traditionally been of the highest quality drawing leading
scientific researches from all over the world. The Conference typically draws
some one thousand participants each year. Such a venue is of special importance
for the training of new researchers and graduate students in systems and
control.&lt;br/&gt;
