The current research interests of the nominee are: 1. high speed optoelectronic
devices in the millimeter wave frequencies. 2. architecture of lightwave
networks based on partially tunable lasers. 3. wavelength tunable optical
devices for multi-access lightwave networks. 4. non-linear optical techniques
for producing high pwoer semiconductor laser sources and for dense optical
switching networks.
