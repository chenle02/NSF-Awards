9411364 Shaulis The Community College of Allegheny County requests support from
NSF for connection of its campus network to PREPnet. PREPnet is the midlevel
network located in the state of Pennsylvania. PREPnet will provide operations
management and information services and it will give the Community College
connection to the Internet, a collection of interoperable networks that connect
most research and education organizations in the United States The colleges need
greater functionality and network speed to be able to access supercomputers,
libraries, and make file transfers along with being able to implement a more
reliable mail system. The connection will benefit the faculty research, staff
and students. This is also a unique opportunity for the school to explore
innovative educational resources.
