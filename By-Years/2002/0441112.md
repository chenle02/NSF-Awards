This Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) Phase I project is to develop and
apply magnetoelastic sensors as a single-use, widely dispersible distributed
wireless sensor system. &lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The commercial application of this
project will be in protection against bioterrorism.
