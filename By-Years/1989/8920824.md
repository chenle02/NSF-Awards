A workstation and electronic test equipment will be provided for researchers at
the University of Florida for research in the Department of Electrical
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the area(s) of Modular
arithmetic systems; attached array processors, and neural network simulators.
