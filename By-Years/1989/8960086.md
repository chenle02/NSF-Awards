* Evaluation of the Quality of Fresh Concrete by Ultrasonic   Means

This proposal will investigate the application of ultrasonic waves of high
frequency to determine concrete physical properties before and after placement
in the forms. The objective is to determine the best method of placing concrete
through quality assurance and quality control of fresh concrete.
