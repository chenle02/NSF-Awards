This award is to support student participation in the annual ACM Symposium on
Computer and Communication Security conference (CCS), to be held November 4-8,
2013.

The ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security (CCS) is the flagship
annual conference of the Special Interest Group on Security, Audit and Control
(SIGSAC) of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). The conference brings
together information security researchers, practitioners, developers, and users
from all over the world to explore cutting-edge ideas and results. It provides
an environment to conduct intellectual discussions. From its inception, CCS has
established itself as a high standard research conference in its area.
