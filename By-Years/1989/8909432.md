This award, made under the Mathematical Sciences Research Groups initiative, is
for the collaborative support of graduate students and postdoctoral associates
working with the group of modern analysts at Berkeley. Research specialties
represented include operator algebras, representation theory for Lie groups, and
ergodic theory.
