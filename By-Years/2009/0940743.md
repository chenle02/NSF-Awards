Intelligence and Security Informatics (ISI) has been established as an
interdisciplinary subject that focuses on the development and use of advanced
information technologies, including methodologies, models and algorithms,
infrastructure, systems, and tools, for local, national/international, and
global security related applications through an integrated technological,
organizational, behavioral, and policy based approach. The international
conference was first held in 2003; ISI 2009 will be held in Richardson Texas.
This award will support student scholarships to attend the meeting, encouraging
participation from a wide range of students.
