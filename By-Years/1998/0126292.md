Travel funds are provided for Donna Nelson at the University of Oklahoma and
five of her undergraduate students to travel and attend the August 2001 National
Meeting of the American Chemical Society in Chicago, IL. Donna Nelson is an
invited speaker for two talks and the students will have two poster
presentations. The subject of these talks and presentations is the diversity of
the faculties of the "Top 50" chemistry departments.
