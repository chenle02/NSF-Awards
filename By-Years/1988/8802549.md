A Workshop on Water Waves and Floating Bodies will be held, April 10 - 13, 1988,
at the Swope Center, Marine Biological Laboratory, Woods Hole. An international
community of engineers and applied mathematicians will meet with a mix of senior
and junior participants from both universities and industry to exchange ideas
and share in the information exchange. Area of special focus will be the current
activities in large-scale computations as applied to water waves and floating
bodies.
