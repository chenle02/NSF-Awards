This Scientific Program Order supports the University Corporation for
Atmospheric (UCAR) Educational Outreach Program and related activities.
Specifically, this SPO will support visiting scientists at UCAR, postdoctoral
research in ocean modeling, summer employment program, and workshops involving
the atmospheric community.
