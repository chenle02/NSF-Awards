This is a block travel grant for U.S. researchers to attend the Seventh National
Power Systems Conference(NPSC) at Calcutta, India. The NPSC is a comprehensive
conference devoted to power systems and is widely attended by scientists,
engineers, consultants and manufacturing representatives from all over India.
This grant will provide travel funds for 20 U.S. participants.
