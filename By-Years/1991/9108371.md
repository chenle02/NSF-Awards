* Undergraduate Design Projects to Aid the Disabled

* K. M. Mudry

The proposal is to fund 5 to 6 undergraduate engineering students yearly to
design aids for specific disabled individuals. The grant will expose students to
a rewarding real-world design experience, and should motivate them to choose a
career in engineering design and research to aid the disabled.
