A minicomputer server will be provided for researchers at Cornell University
for research in the Department of Mathematics. This equipment is provided under
the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer and Information Science and
Engineering program. The research for which the equipment is to be used will be
in the areas of computational algebraic geometry, algebraic K-theory, splines on
polyhedral complexes, dynamical systems, and applied logic.
