At an NSF-funded workshop held in 1988 the process of establishing a network of
well-documented geotechnical experimentation sites in the United States, open to
all interested researchers, was initiated. The objective is the proposed
workshop is to identify the sites to be developed, establish guidelines for
their use, and select a System Management Board.
