9528500 Balling This award assists the regional network, SURAnet, in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks in the United States and to the rest of
the Internet. The award made under the guidelines published in the NSF Program
Solicitation 93-52, partially funds two T-3 connections over an anticipated
duration of 4 years.
