This award permits the principal investigator to give an invited plenary lecture
at the next meeting of the International Federation for Information Processing,
Working Group on Automation of Production Planning and Control. The meeting will
be held in Budapest, in August 1985. He plans also to make a number of site
visits, including the Hungarian Academy of Science, Vuoso Research Institute,
German Democratic Republic Machine Tool Research Institute, Lucas Institute, The
University of Aachen, and other research organizations.
