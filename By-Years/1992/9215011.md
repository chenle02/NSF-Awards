Professor Oh will continue his work on aspects of symplectic geometry related
to minimal surfaces,mean curvature flow and Kahler-Einstein manifolds.This work
follows on from spectacular work of Arnold and Floer concerning the geometry and
toplogy of Lagrangian submanifolds in symplectic geometry.
