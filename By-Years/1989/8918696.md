Activities in support of the establishment of a national institute of
statistical sciences will be carried out by a committee and its head under the
sponsorship of the American Statistical Association. These activities include
committee meetings and travel by the head of the committee for consultation with
various potential sponsors of the institute. They also include the development
of a formal prospectus as well as preliminary versions of a more detailed
proposal.
