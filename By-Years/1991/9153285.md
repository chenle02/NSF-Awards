Faculty at the University of Tennessee, Chattanooga, the Chattanooga State
Technical Community College, Southern College, and area high schools are working
together to improve their curriculum by adapting and implementing the model
calculus program being developed at St. Olaf College. Graphical, numerical, and
algebraic viewpoints are brought to bear on calculus ideas to improve students'
conceptual understanding.
