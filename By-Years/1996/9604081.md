9604081 Buhse This proposal is a request by the Society of Protozoologists is
for funds for participant travel to the Xth international Congress of
Protozoology to be held in Sydney, Australia In July, 1997. The International
Congress of Protozoology is held every four years under the sponsorship of the
Commission on Protozoology of the International Union of Biological Sciences.
This meeting will bring together approximately 800 scientists from around the
world to present their research and exchange ideas. ***
