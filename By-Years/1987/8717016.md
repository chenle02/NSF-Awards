A powerful color workstation for a Vision/graphics lab will be provided for
researchers at SUNY-Stony Brook for research in the Department of Computer
Science. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of: (1) An
Integrated Approach to the Structural Description of 3D Scenes for Robot
Manipulation (2) An Architecture for 3D Voxel-Based Graphics (3) Compression of
Digital Images (4) Recognition of Schematics (5) Regularization with
Discontinuities.
