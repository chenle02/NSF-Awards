This award provides funding to Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) for the
support of a Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) Workshop to be held at IIT
on September 27, 2003. This workshop will bring together NSF Principal
Investigators supported by four NSF Directorates, K-12 teachers, and community
college faculty from the Chicago, IL area for the purpose of describing the
opppotunities for teachers and community college faculty to participate in a
research experience with a NSF-funded principal investigator.
