9321107 Heker This award assists the regional network, JvNCnet, in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks in the United States and to the rest of
the Internet. The award made under the guidelines published in the NSF Program
Solicitation 93-52, funds one connection providing 45 million bits per second
service over an anticipated duration of 4 years.
