The FASEB Conference on Somatic Cell Genetics will be held at the Vermont
Academy in Saxtons River, Vermont from June 19, 1989 to June 23, 1989. This
meeting, which has been highly successful in past years, brings together
scientists and students from different fields of research in a atmosphere which
encourages intellectual interaction.
