Partial funding is provided for the 10th U.S. National Congress of Applied
Mechanics, June 16-20, 1986, University of Texas-Austin. It is planned as
broadly inclusive in solid and fluid mechanics and emphasizes the emerging areas
of dispersed systems, texturing and micromechanics of solids, mechanics of
damage, and mechanics of processing.
