An assessment of the Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) program in
Engineering is the goal of this workshop. The objectives of the workshop are to
evaluate the REU program in terms of program content, schedules, management and
overall success in achieving the stated goals for the program as well as provide
recommendations for strengthening undergraduate involvement in research. The
participants in the Workshop include the current REU Engineering Site Directors,
the Education Coordinators form the 19 Engineering Research Centers, and some
leaders in undergraduate education.
