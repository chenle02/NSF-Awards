The recent acquisition of a Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrophotometer is
enabling the Chemistry Department at Mount Holyoke College to significantly
expand the scope and sophistication of the laboratory curriculum in physical
chemistry, in a methods of measurement course, in micro-scale organic chemistry
and in independent research.
