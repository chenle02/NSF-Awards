This proposal seeks funding to support travel of student registrants at the 2014
Combustion Institute Spring Technical Meeting from March 16-18, 2014,in Tulsa,
Oklahoma, hosted by The Central States Section of the Combustion Institute
(CSSCI). The broader objectives&lt;br/&gt;of The Combustion Institute focus on
discovery, advancement, and application of combustion/fire science and
engineering. By participating in the meeting students and student researchers
will have the first-hand interaction with experts, as well as peers, in exchange
ideas, thus promoting their education, learning, while also fulfill recruitment
and retention of students in STEM fields.
