9722061 Siggia This award is to provide partial support for a Workshop on
Biophysics of the Cell, to be held August 11 - September 5, 1997, at the Aspen
Center for Physics, Aspen, Colorado. The Workshop will bring together and foster
interactions among cell biologists, biophysicists, and physicists (theoretical
physicists working in areas of soft condensed matter and dynamical systems). ***
