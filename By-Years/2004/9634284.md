Abstract This is an international travel grant to Dr. M.S. Ghausi, Dean, College
of Engineering, University of California, Davis. Dr. Ghausi will travel to
Paris, France to attend the UNESCO World Congress of Engineering Educators and
Industry, to present a paper entitled, Cooperative Training and Education
between University, Government Laboratory and Industry. The dates of the meeting
are July 2-5, 1996.
