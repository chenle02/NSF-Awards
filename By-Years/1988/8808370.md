Dr. Eric Van Anslyn has been awarded a Postdoctoral Research Fellowship in
Chemistry. Dr. Anslyn's doctoral degree was from the California Institute of
Technology under the supervision of Professor Robert H. Grubbs. He intends to
continue his research at Columbia University under the sponsorship of Professor
Ronald Breslow. Dr. Anslyn's area of postdoctoral research is the development of
ribonuclease Mimics.
