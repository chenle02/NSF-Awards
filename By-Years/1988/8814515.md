The National Radio Astronomy Observatory (NRAO) is one of the worlds pre.eminent
radio astronomical facilities. It operates telescopes in Green Bank, West
Virginia, near Socorro, New Mexico, and near Tucson, Arizona. Headquarters are
located in Charlottesville, Virginia. The NRAO is currently in the process of
constructing the Very Long Baseline Array, an array telescope with 10 antennas
spread between Hawaii and the U. S. Virgin Islands and an Operations Center
located in Socorro. The NRAO is a National Center with its instruments available
to any scientist to carry out scientific programs on a peer.reviewed,
competitive basis.
