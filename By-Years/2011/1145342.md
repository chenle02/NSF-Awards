The 43rd North American Power Symposium (NAPS) conference will take place at
Northeastern University at Boston, MA in August 2011. It is the objective of
this proposal to request support for the travel expenses for students travelling
to the conference from the US. Matching funds will be sought from power system
utilities, manufacturers and the IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES).
