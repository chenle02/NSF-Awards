A workstation cluster plus oscilloscope will be provided for researchers at
Columbia University for research in the Department of Computer Science. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research in Computer
and Information Science and Engineering program. The research for which the
equipment is to be used will be in the areas of distributed operating systems
and distributed database.
