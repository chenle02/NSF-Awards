Travel support will be provided to allow a participant to deliver a paper at the
Third International Conference on Tall Buildings to be held in Hong Kong and
Guangizhou, China, from December 10-15, 1984. The conference will provide an
international forum for the exchange of scientific information on the design and
construction of buildings.
