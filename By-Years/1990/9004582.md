This proposal constitutes a continuation of studies of Feng, Gilmore and
Vallieres in Research in Nuclear Physics and Related Topics; namely, (1) The
fermion dynamical symmetry model, (2) Shell model calculations; (3) The baryon-
baryon interaction; and (4) "quantum chaos" and non-linear dynamics.
