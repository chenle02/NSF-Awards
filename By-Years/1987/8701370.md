A workshop will be conducted to explore a new manufacturing research initiative.
The "Strategic Manufacturing Research Initiative" workshop will bring together a
small group of experts in manufacturing from universities, industry, and
government to exchange ideas and provide input and direction on the new
initiative. The goal of the initiative is to move top priority manufacturing
technology frontiers ahead in large jumps through major innovations and other
means, and to improve competitiveness of the USA in manufacturing.
