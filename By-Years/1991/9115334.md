This award supports a continuation of the collaboration between the National
Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA) and Jackson State University.
These institutions are developing scientific visualization tool sets
specifically in the IBM Pc MS- DOS environment. These tools fill pressing needs
in the area of scientific visualization and advance the state of the art tools
available to many users. All software, source code and documentation will be
available for dissemination and widespread use.
