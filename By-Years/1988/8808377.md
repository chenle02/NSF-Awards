Mr. Richard Anthony Gibbs has been recommended for a Postdoctoral Research
Fellowship in Chemistry Award. Mr. Gibbs doctoral degree will be from the
University of California at Riverside under the supervision of Professor William
H. Okamura. He intends to continue his research at Pennsylvania State University
under the sponsorship of Professor S. J. Benkovic. Mr. Gibbs' area of
Postdoctoral Research will be the design and preparation of catalytic antibodies
for the aldol condensation.
