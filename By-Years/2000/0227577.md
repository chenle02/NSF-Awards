This award provides funding for a three year continuing award to support a
Research Experiences for Teachers (RET) Site, at Northeastern University,
entitled, "Research Experiences for Teachers-A Program Linked to Urban Schools
(RET-PLUS). This program will be conducted by the Center for the Enhancement of
Science and Mathematics Education (CESAME), in collaboration with the Center for
Subsurface Sensing and Imaging Systems (CenSSIS), and Engineering Research
Center. The program will provide a six-week summer research experience for ten
urban middle and/or high school math and/or science teachers and one
undergraduate education student.
