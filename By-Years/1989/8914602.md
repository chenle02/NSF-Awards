Funds for a ultracentrifuge and gel analyzer for Whitney Lab, University of
Florida, are requested. All the research efforts will be directed toward aquatic
systems and vary over a broad range of subjects. Recently, molecular genetics
has been added to the research portfolio at the lab. Among the species studied
will be clams, shrimps, and jellyfish.
