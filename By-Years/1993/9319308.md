9319308 Mijovic This grant provides partial funding for the purchase of
equipment for dielectric measurements. Three departments will be represented in
research which requires the use of this equipment: Chemical Engineering,
Chemistry, and Electrical Engineering. No similar equipment is available at
Polytechnic University, which will be used in a variety of research activities.
