An atomic absorption spectrometer, a UV-VIS spectrophotometer, a Laboratory
Information Management System and a dedicated laboratory network server will be
purchased. These will be used in studies of the biogeochemistry of small
forested watersheds, atmosphere - forest interactions, aquatic-atmosphere
interactions and aquatic nutrient cycling and foodweb dynamics.
