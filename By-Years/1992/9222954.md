This is a minority fellowship travel award for exploratory visits to the
University of Baltimore, University of Virginia, and Princeton University and to
attend and meet with potential mentors at the American Political Science
Association meetings in Chicago. In these visits the PI will make additional
contacts necessary for the conduct of her planned research on the nature of
political ideology among African-Americans.
