Ripon College is acquiring a gas chromatograph-mass spectrometer (GC/MS) system
for use by students in the organic microscale laboratory, and in the non-majors
course in forensic science. The instrument is also being used in advanced
laboratory and instrumental analysis courses, as well as in undergraduate
research.
