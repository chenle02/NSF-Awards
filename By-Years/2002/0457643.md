An Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers planning meeting has been
awarded to determine the feasibility of the University of Nebraska joining the
existing Center for Engineering Logistics and Distribution. The focus of the
research site will be in the area of Radio Frequency Identification. This
expands the current research agenda of the center and addresses a current major
new interest of a number of companies.
