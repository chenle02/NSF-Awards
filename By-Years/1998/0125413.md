0125413 McGruder

This award supports a planning visit to South Africa in August 2001 for
Professor Charles McGruder, Department of Physics and Astronomy, Western
Kentucky University, Bowling Green, KY. Professor McGruder, who is the President
of the National Society of Black Physicists, will meet with leading South
African scientists to formulate joint research projects between graduate
students in the United States and South Africa. He will also meet with Patricia
Whitelock, Deputy Director of the South African Astronomical Observatory, to
discuss a planned graduate course in basic space science to attract students
from all over Africa.
