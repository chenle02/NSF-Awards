9319327 McCormick This project connects Coe College to NSFNET through CICNet
over a 56 kbps line. Faculty and students will benefit from access to the
resources of the Internet, including libraries and supercomputers. In addition,
they will be able to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at other
institutions in pursuit of research and educational projects. The award provides
partial support of the project for two years.
