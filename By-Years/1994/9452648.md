9452648 Medicinebull The project will establish a formal coalition among
representatives of small, land-based tribes in Eastern California, Southern
Nevada, and Western Arizona. The coalition will include representatives from
state and private agencies concerned with American Indian education. The PI will
convene an organizing conference to begin discussions, reinforce connections,
examine the implications of systemic reform, and identify an executive committee
structure to begin preparation for writing and submission of a Development
project proposal.
