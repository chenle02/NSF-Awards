0245648&lt;br/&gt;Ren&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This is a one-year bridge
award to Dr. Shang-Fen Ren, Illinois State University, to continue a program of
Research for Undergraduate Experience (REU) for physics students from the United
States to spend a summer conducting research at Beijing University, China. This
award will enable six U.S. students to spend four weeks conducting research in
nano and materials science, and will allow Dr. Ren to carry out collaborative
research with her colleagues at Beijing University. &lt;br/&gt;
