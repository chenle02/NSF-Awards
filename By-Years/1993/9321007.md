9321007 Burns This award assists the Westnet mid-level network in obtaining
connectivity to other regional networks and to the rest of the Internet. The
award, made under the guidelines published in NSF Program Solicitation 93-52,
partially funds eleven T-1 (1.54 million bits per second) connections.
Anticipated duration of the award is four years.
