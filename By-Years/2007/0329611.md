0329611 Danyluk Partial travel support is provided to a group of researchers and
graduate students from the Former Soviet Union and other countries to attend the
International Conference on Wear of Materials in Washington, D.C., March
31-April 4, 2003. The purpose is to facilitate communication of research and
development of international collaborations. ***
