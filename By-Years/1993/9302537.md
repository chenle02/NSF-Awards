This award provides travel support for 20 women in the CISE disciplines who are
starting or ready to start academic careers, to attend a 1-day workshop on
Academic Careers, to be held on May 15, 1993, immediately prior to the opening
of the Federated Computing Research Conference (FCRC) in San Diego. The workshop
will include sessions on the tenure process, obtaining an academic position,
building a research program, obtaining external funding, teaching, making
connections with people in the field, and time management. Funds are also
provided to support the awardees in registering for one of the participating
research meetings at FCRC.
