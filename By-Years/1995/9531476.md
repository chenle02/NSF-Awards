This action funds a Minority Graduate Student Travel Award as offered in
program announcement NSF 94-133 . The travel award permits travel to the
University of Uppsala in Sweden where the grantee will consult with his proposed
sponsoring scientist on his research and training plan for an application to the
NSF Minority Postdoctoral Research Fellowship program in fiscal year 1996.
