9421455 Williams This award will support field-based research to study the
eruptive activity and deposits of Llaima volcano in southern Chile. Eruption
began on 17 May 1994. The goals of the study are to initiate research on the
effect of lava flows on glaciers at an erupting volcano and to improve
understanding of the controls on lahar production at glaciated volcanoes.
