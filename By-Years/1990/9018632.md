The Georgia Institute of Technology NSF/GEE program is to support eight students
for graduate and professional study in the following areas: (1) Electrical
Engineering, (2) Mechanical Engineering, and (3) Industrial and Systems
Engineering. Awards are made to persons in the underrepresented groups of women,
minorities, and/or persons with disabilities for study in these areas.
