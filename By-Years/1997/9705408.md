An international seminar on automated deduction, organized by Prof. Wolfgang
Bibel(Technische Hochschule Darmstadt, Germany), Prof. Ulrich Furbach
(University of Kopblenz, Germany), and Dr. Mark Stickel (SRI International,
Menlo Park, CA, USA), will be held 24-28 February 1997 at the Dagstuhl Seminar
Center in Waden, Germany. About 70 automated deduction researchers from around
the world have been invited to participate. This grant supports the travel of
American invitees to the seminar. ***
