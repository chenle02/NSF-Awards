Salish Kootenai College&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;Abstract&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt; Salish Kootenai College (SKC) is the third tribal college
to take up the challenge of filling the secondary STEM teacher void. The
Developing American Indian Secondary Educators Project (DAISE) will initiate a
Bachelor of Science in Secondary Science education degree program. The five
objectives of the DAISE Project are:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;
&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;1. Develop and implement a high quality, culturally
competent, sustainable &lt;br/&gt;2. Bachelor of Science in Secondary Education
degree program at SKC &lt;br/&gt;3. Increase the number of American Indian
secondary science and math teachers. &lt;br/&gt;4. Improve coordination of
student services between high schools and SKC. &lt;br/&gt;5. Increase the number
of American Indian high school students completing higher level STEM coursework.
&lt;br/&gt;6. Enhance the SKC faculty, curriculum and infrastructure to enable
better support for American Indian students.
