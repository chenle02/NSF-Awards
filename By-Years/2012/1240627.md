TECHNICAL SUMMARY:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;A workshop on the future of research in
the Metals and Metallic Nanostructures field will be held at the University of
California, Santa Barbara on June 13th and 14th, 2012. This workshop will
identify important new research trends, assess research activities in terms of
national priorities, examine international competitiveness in this field, and
evaluate workforce development needs. Experts from diverse backgrounds will be
invited to participate in this event. The workshop will produce a report that
will be published and made available for public
dissemination.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;NON-TECHNICAL
SUMMARY:&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;A workshop on the future of research in the Metals
and Metallic Nanostructures field will be held at the University of California,
Santa Barbara on June 13th and 14th, 2012. A variety of experts from diverse
backgrounds in this field will be invited to participate. The workshop will
produce a report that will be published and made available for public
dissemination. This report will be of use in guiding the future of advanced
research in this field.
