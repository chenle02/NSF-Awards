Workstation based digital signal processing environment will be provided for
researchers at Georgia Tech for research in the School of Electrical
Engineering. This equipment is provided under the Instrumentation grants for
Research in Computer and Information Science and Engineering program. The
research for which the equipment is to be used will be in the areas of: Signal
Processing Computer Architectures Speech Processing Image Processing General
Digital Signal Processing Theory
