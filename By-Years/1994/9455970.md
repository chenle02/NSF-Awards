9455970 Hughes-Hallett Faculty from the University of Arizona, Duke University,
Harvard University, University of Illinois, Ohio State University, University of
Pittsburgh, Utah State University, and Weber State University are working
together to establish or strengthen links between mathematics departments and
other departments to improve undergraduate course offerings. A three day
conference is being conducted to discuss particular needs in each field and what
can be done to discuss these needs.
