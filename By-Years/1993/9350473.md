9350473 Novak With the goal of developing an innovative polymer science program,
Dr. Novak's research is focused on the development of new living
polymerizations, new types of organic-inorganic hybrid materials, photoactive
polymers, and aqueous-based polymerization chemistry. His teaching plan is to
establish a polymeric materials program at both the undergraduate and graduate
levels. ***
