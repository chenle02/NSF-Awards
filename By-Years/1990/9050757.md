Calculus students are using a new computer laboratory equipped with Macintosh
computers running Mathematica to perform experiments and do their own
investigations. The are learning to confirm or reject graphical evidence with
numerical and symbolic computations and numerical evidence with graphical and
symbolic computations. A student guide has been produced that helps the students
use Mathematica. The institution is contributing an amount equal to the award.
