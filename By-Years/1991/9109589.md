This project will fund undergraduate engineering students to design devices and
systems to aid the disabled. Coordination will be performed by Technology
Assistance for Special Consumers, an organization within the School of
Engineering. The opportunity to design for specific disabled individuals will
provide engineering seniors with a unique and rewarding real-world educational
experience.
