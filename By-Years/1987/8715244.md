Workstations will be provided for researchers at Case Western Reserve University
for research in the Department of Computer Engineering and Science. This
equipment is provided under the Instrumentation Grants for Research and
Information Science and Engineering program. The research which the equipment is
to be used will be in the area(s) of Nested Relational Database Management
System, Parallelism in Logic Programs and Synthetic Query Response Construction
in Realtime/Time- Constrained Databases
