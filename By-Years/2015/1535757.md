This proposal request conference support for student participation in the 2015
InterPACK/ICNMM conference, July 6-9, in San Francisco, CA.
&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;The NSF funding will be used to offer 17 travel grants to
both female graduate students and students from under-represented minority
groups. The opportunities of this NSF grant will benefit these student
participants in broadening their educational experience and preparing them for
their careers with a preview of professional and technical communities.
