This award supports participation of students in the sixth GREPSEC workshop,
held August 7-8 2023 in conjunction with the USENIX Security Conference. GREPSEC
promotes increased engagement in computer security research, specifically
targeting women and underrepresented groups through mentorship. The goals of the
workshop are to increase diversity in the field. Prior GREPSEC attendees have
gone on to publish in a range of venues, mostly focused on the field of
cybersecurity.

This award reflects NSF's statutory mission and has been deemed worthy of
support through evaluation using the Foundation's intellectual merit and broader
impacts review criteria.
