This CEDAR proposal will provide funds for capital equipment for the Jicamarca
Radio Observatory for two specific purposes: (1) to continue and complete the
modernization of the Jimamarca data acquisition and computing system begun with
a 1988-1989 CEDAR grant, and (2) to purchase and install a modern ionosonde at
Jicamarca.***//
