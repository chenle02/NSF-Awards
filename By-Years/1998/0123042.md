&lt;br/&gt;This program is providing educational and financial support services
to thirty talented and financially disadvantaged mathematics and engineering
students. The scholarships are increasing the rate of degree completion and
subsequent entry into the workforce or postgraduate studies. In addition to the
financial support an ongoing comprehensive mentoring and guidance system
designed to build leadership skills, maturity and responsibility has been
established.
