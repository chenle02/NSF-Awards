The goal of this project is to bring together researchers from a number of
disciplines to focus on the current state of research and new research
directions for Operations Research in manufacturing for the last decade of the
20th century. The conference is sponsored by the Operations Research Society of
America, the German Operations Research Society, and the National Institute of
Standards and Technology.
