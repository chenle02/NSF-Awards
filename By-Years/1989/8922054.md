This grant is to provide partial support for the IXth International Conference
on Multiple Criteria Decision Making, which will be held August 5-8, 1990. The
theme of the conference is "Multiple Criteria Decision Making and Support
Systems at the Interface of Industry, Business and Government. The program
features a collection of sessions on theoretical advances and applications in
decision theory, modeling of uncertainty and imprecision financial planning,
computer aided interactive decision support, expert knowledge acquisition, and
other topics. The conference will provide an important forum for interactions
among researchers in the field of multiple criteria decision making.
