Geological Sample Storage facilities provide important and essential services
to the geological research community. The core repository facility at University
of Rhode Island, Graduate School of Oceanography is being funded to archive
cores and other geological rock samples from the seafloor and provide the
important services of sample distribution and related data dissemination to the
marine geological, sedimentological and paleoceanographic communitites.
