9625588 Leinen The University of Rhode Island will operate the research vesssel
ENDEAVOR in 1996 in support of a variety of oceanographic research programs for
the federal agencies. The ENDEAVOR is a 184' general purpose research vessel
with a schedule of 149 operational days in 1996 of which 78 days are in support
of NSF-funded research. The ship will support cruises in the North Atlantic for
NSF, ONR, and DOE.
