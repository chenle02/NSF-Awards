This grant provides funds for students and junior faculty to travel to the
International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE 2019), which takes place
in Montreal, Canada in May of 2019. ICSE is the flagship conference in the
field. The conference is a venue for technical presentations of research
results, interactions between academia and industry, and international
collaborations. The specific objective of the grant is to increase and broaden
participation in mentoring programs such as the Doctoral Symposium, the New
Faculty Symposium, and the Student Mentoring Workshop. The objective is to
provide education, training and mentoring and to nurture a globally-aware
workforce of research and educators within the US and build the community of
researchers in the field of Software Engineering.&lt;br/&gt;&lt;br/&gt;This
award reflects NSF's statutory mission and has been deemed worthy of support
through evaluation using the Foundation's intellectual merit and broader impacts
review criteria.
