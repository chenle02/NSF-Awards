*** 9552943 Meeker The University of New Hampshire will initiate a three-week,
summer residential, Young Scholars project for 25 talented students entering
grade 10 from New Hampshire and Maine. The project integrates probability,
statistics, and computer technology within a marine science experience focused
on a field-based study of the Great Bay estuarine system in coastal New
Hampshire. Students engage in classroom lectures and discussions, field trips,
laboratory and research activities to gain an understanding and appreciation of
scientific techniques. ***
