9421638 Thibeault This project connects Curry College to the Internet over a
256,000 bits per second line. Faculty and students at the school benefit from
access to the resources of the Internet, including libraries and supercomputers.
In addition, they are able to communicate and collaborate with colleagues at
other institutions in pursuit of research and educational projects. The award
provides partial support of the project for two years.
