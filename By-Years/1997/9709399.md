9709399 Hauri Funding is requested to support a 2-person, 6-week field program
in the island of Rapa, located in the Austral Islands chain of French Polynesia.
The field party will construct a geologic map and collect samples of volcanic
rocks and mantle nodules. Funds to support the geochemical analysis of these
samples are not requested here.
