ABSTRACT OPP-9522627 Myers The Interagency Arctic Research Policy Committee
(IARPC) provides leadership for the US Arctic Monitoring and Assessment Program
(AMAP). The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) functions as
the U.S. Secretariat to the AMAP program. Funds are provided via this
interagency transfer for the U.S. data Management efforts, and to assist in
preparation of heavy metals, climate change and UV-B effects, and hydrocarbon
pollution chapters.
