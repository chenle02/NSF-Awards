This proposal is for a conference on "Fundamental Aspects of Quantum Theory" at
the University of South Carolina, December 14-16, 1989, to commemorate the 30th
anniversary of the Ahronov-Bohm effect. Topics include quantum cosmology,
Berry's phase, and the measurement problem.
