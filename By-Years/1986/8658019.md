This action provides the first year's support for the Presidential Young
Investigator (PYI) Award, FY 87. This support includes the NSF matching to the
industry contribution provided to the P.I. The award was made to enable the
investigator to do research on the development of design methodologies for
earthquake resistant design of steel structures.
