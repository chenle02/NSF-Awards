This Interagency Agreement ("IA") provides that the Natinoal Science Foundation
Directorate for Engineering (NSF) will order services from the Library of
Congress Federal Research Program, which is administered by the Library of
Congress Federal Research Divison (LOC/FRD), to fulfill NSF's requirement for
research reports, translations, and analytical studies.
