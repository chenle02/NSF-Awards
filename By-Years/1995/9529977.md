This is a proposal to hold a workshop involving about forty leading researchers
from academia and industry to discuss: 1) the importance and value of Structured
Design Methods for MEMS; 2) the research opportunities in this area, and, 3) the
likely increase in effectiveness of MEMS design utilizing these methods.
