This award to University of Tennessee, Knoxville, entitled "MirNET: High-
Performance International Internet Services" (MirNET-HPIIS) provides support for
a High Performance International Internet Services connection between high-
performance research institutions attached to the Russian Backbone Network
(Rbnet) and the vBNs.
