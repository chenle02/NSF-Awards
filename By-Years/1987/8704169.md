This research program consists of a number of projects related to
supercomputing. They include the Fast Adaptive Composite Method, Multilevel Load
Balancing, Analysis of Preconditionings, Algebraic Multigrid, Multi-level
Methods for Optimization, etc. The research results will have applications in
many branches of science and engineering.
