9414374 Bras A Workshop on Sustainable Development in Urban Areas of the
Americans will take place in Santiago, Chile, April 5-9, 1994. The purpose of
this meeting is to bring an eclectic interdisciplinary group of experts to
develop the research agenda necessary to define the concept of sustainable
development within the context of urban areas in various parts of the Americas
where natural resources, environmental and economic conditions, and other
factors are distinct for each country and may influence their urban systems
sustainability. One beneficial result from this meeting is the development of
joint research projects between United States and other American countries for
the improvement of the knowledge base related to the healthy growth of urban
sites. ***
