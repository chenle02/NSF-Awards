Augustana College is establishing a connection to NSFNET via a 19,200 bits per
second line to the National Center for Supercomputer Applications. The
connection enhances collaborations of campus scientists with colleagues at other
institutions, and allows access to libraries and other remote facilities
throughout the internet. This award funds part of the costs for two years.***//
