Professor Ronald DiPerna of the University of California at Berkeley will be
principal lecturer at an NSF-CBMS Regional Conference to be held at the
University of Miami in January of 1989. The lectures will discuss a number of
topics in continuum mechanics such as shock waves, vortex theory, all the scene
of timely and significant new developments.
