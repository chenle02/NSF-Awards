This is an award to provide partial support for travel of five US participants
to a US-China Conference on Bioreaction and Bioseparation in Hangzhou, People's
Republic of China from June 8 to 12, 1992. The main subjects of the Conference
are Bioreaction Kinetics, Transport Phenomena in a Bioreactor, Bioreactor
Design, Principles of Bioseparation, New Approaches in Bioseparation Methods,
Integrated Bioreaction and Separation, and Process Modeling and Control of
Bioreaction and Bioseparation. Conference Proceedings are to be published.
