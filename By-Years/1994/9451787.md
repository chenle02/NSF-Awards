9451787 Pentony The project augments and modernizes computer and multi-media
technology for use in laboratories that serve the international relations,
political science, and urban studies student majors. The aim of the project is
to increase student competence in data discovery, accessing global communication
networks, data analysis, problem solving, and the presentation of their
findings.
