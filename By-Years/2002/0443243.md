This award provides partial funding to the National Research Council for
carrying out a study of the decadal status of the field of Atomic, Molecular,
and Optical physics, assessing programs and looking ahead to the future. The
work will be done under the auspices of the NRC Committee on Atomic, Molecular,
and Optical Science (CAMOS).
