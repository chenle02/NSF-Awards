This award provides partial funding for a technician's position in the
Department of Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences at the Massachusetts
Institute of Technology. The funds will be used to support a technician for
three years. The technician will be responsible for operating and repairing
solid- source and gas-source mass spectrometers housed in adjoining
laboratories.
