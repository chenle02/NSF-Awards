This award suppors the international travel of 10 US scientists to attend the
Grid Computing - The Next Decade workshop that will be held in January 4-6,
2012, in Zakopane, Poland. This interdisciplinary, international workshop will
discuss the state of the art of Grid computing, its applications and, more
importantly, its future. The detailed results of the workshop will be published
in the form of a report that will be made available to the public.
