Travel support will be provided to permit greater participation than would
otherwise be possible for contributing US participants in the upcoming 18th.
International Congress for Theoretical and Applied Mechanics. The congress is to
be held in August 1992 in Haifa. US contributions are rigorously screened by a
select technical committee. Travel help will be biased towards deserving young
scientists and engineers. US participation will afford a unique opportunity for
updating the comunity on international developments in this important area, key
to technologies in Chemical, Mechanical, Manufacturing, and Civil/Environmental
Engineering.
