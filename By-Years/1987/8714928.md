The purpose of this conference is to promote interaction between university and
industrial researchers, to focus the attention of power system researchers on
computer relaying as an emerging technology and, to provide a forum for
discussing new research directions. The conference is international in its
flavor with both European and Japanese participants. This will be the first
conference of this type to be held in the U.S.
