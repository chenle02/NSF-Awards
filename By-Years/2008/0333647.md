This proposal will establish an advanced network connection from the University
of the South, through Vanderbilt University to the Southern Crossroads Gigapop
and on to the Abilene/Internet2 network for research and education. Driving
collaborative research applications include&lt;br/&gt;* Landscape
analysis&lt;br/&gt;* Appalachian Watershed Research,&lt;br/&gt;* GIS Information
for the Cumberland Plateau, and &lt;br/&gt;* Astronomy to share research
data.&lt;br/&gt;
