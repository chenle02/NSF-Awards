This award supports a Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) site
proposal to provide research experiences for selected undergraduates in the
marine and fresh water sciences, in order to aquaint them with the excitement
and opportunities of academic research and to encourage them to pursue graduate
studies and a career of research.
