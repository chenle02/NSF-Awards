The Department of Physics will purchase a hyper-pure, intrinsic germanium
detector and a MCA-card for an IBM-PC. The equipment would be used to strengthen
the department's advanced laboratory program by introducing experiments in
neutron activation, X-ray fluorescence and environmental studies.
