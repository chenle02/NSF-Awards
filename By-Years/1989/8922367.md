This award is made to The Magic Lantern for the complete production, 16 mm
color with full sound track consisting of narration, dialogue, original music,
sound effects (and animation, if any) of a 25-30 minute film focusing on the
Engineering Research Centers. The film's purpose is to stimulate interest in
NSF's Engineering Research Centers and their relevance to international
industrial competitiveness-- highlighting their varied programs for research,
education, and industrial liaison. The intended audience for this film is
Engineering schools, Industry, Professional societies and Civic leaders.
