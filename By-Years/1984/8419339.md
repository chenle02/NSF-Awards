This research project is concerned with the application of speech recognition
technology to the development of a speech training aid to help in teaching
hearing impaired children to speak intellegibly. The research team involves
people in speech recognition, speech analysis and speech therapy. The aim is to
develop an inexpensive, personal computer-based speech training device to be
used by schools and speech clinics.
