9353888 Stinemetz Rhodes College will initiate a four week,commuter, Young
Scholars project in biology for 32 students entering grades 10 and 11. Students
will learn four techniques fore investigating scientific problems in biology.
Working in pairs, students individually design. conduct, analyze, summarize and
present their research at weekly participant scientific meetings. Panels of
professionals introduce the participants to careers in biology. Follow up
activities include presentations at participants high schools, further expansion
and completion of summer research projects. Career and ethics of science
activities complete the project activities. ***
